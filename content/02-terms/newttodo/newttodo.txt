Title: newtToDo

----

Date: 09 November 2017

----

Text:

An [application](/terms/application) developed by [Adam Tow](/sources/adam-tow) that allows the user to efficiently manage tasks using a Newton device. Features include alarmed reminders, repeating tasks, additional priority levels, flexible options for sorting and filing, a customizable [user interface](/terms/user-interface), and the ability to automatically import [to do list](/terms/to-do-list) items from the [Date Book](/terms/date-book) application.

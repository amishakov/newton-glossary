Title: Clamshell

----

Date: 11 March 2017

----

Text:

A computer case design consisting of a base and a hinged lid which mimic the appearance of a clam or similar bivalve mollusk. The [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) features a translucent clamshell style case.

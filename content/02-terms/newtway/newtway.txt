Title: Newtway

----

Date: 03 September 2017

----

Text:

A simple hardware adapter created by [Doug Parker](/sources/doug-parker) to easily connect a [Palm Portable Stowaway Keyboard](https://web.archive.org/web/20000510122503/http://thinkoutside.com:80/palm.html) to a Newton device.

Title: Indiglo Newton

----

Date: 07 April 2017

----

Text:

A nickname applied to the [MessagePad 130](/terms/messagepad-130) in reference to its electroluminescent [backlight](/terms/backlight). [Indiglo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indiglo) backlighting was a feature available on Timex watches around the same time that backlit Newton devices were being developed.

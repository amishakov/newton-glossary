Title: Overview

----

Date: 27 August 2018

----

Text:

A [view](/terms/view) that displays a summarized list of multiple items at once. From an overview, a user can [tap](/terms/tap) on a item to see more detailed information. To select, view, or interact with multiple items in an overview list, the user taps the [checkboxes](/terms/checkbox) beside each item and performs another action, such as [routing](/terms/routing). The overview is presented by tapping the [overview button](/terms/overview-button) in an [application](/terms/application) such as [Notepad](/terms/notepad) or the [Extras Drawer](/terms/extras-drawer).

Title: Overview

----

Date: 27 August 2018

----

Text:

A [view](/terms/view) that displays a summarized list of multiple items. From an overview, a user can either [tap](/terms/tap) an item to see more detailed information or select, view, and interact with multiple items by [tapping](/terms/tapping) a [checkbox](/terms/checkbox) beside each item and performing an action, such as [routing](/terms/routing).

An overview is displayed by tapping the [overview button](/terms/overview-button) in an [application](/terms/application) such as [Notepad](/terms/notepad) or the [Extras Drawer](/terms/extras-drawer).

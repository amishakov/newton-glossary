Title: Dante

----

Date:

----

Text:

One of the code names for the [MessagePad 130](/terms/messagepad-130) device, also known as [Quark](/terms/quark).

Dante also refers to version 2.0 of [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os), as well as the connection [protocol](/terms/protocol) used to communicate between a Newton device and another computer.

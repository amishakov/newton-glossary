Title: Universal Scroll Arrows

----

Date:

----

Text:

A pair of icon-based [buttons](/terms/button) used to [scroll](/terms/scroll) an area of content displayed on the [screen](/terms/screen). Universal scroll arrows are located above and below the [overview button](/terms/overview-button) in the standard [button bar](/terms/button-bar) found on all Newton devices.

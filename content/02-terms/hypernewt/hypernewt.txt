Title: HyperNewt

----

Date: 01 October 2017

----

Text:

An [application](/terms/application) developed by [Adam Tow](/sources/adam-tow) that extends the built-in [folder](/terms/folder) filing system of [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os). HyperNewt provides flexible data management, hierarchical organization, and hyperlinking of items created by most Newton applications.

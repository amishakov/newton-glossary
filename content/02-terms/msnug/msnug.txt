Michigan State Newton Users Group

Originally founded by [Douglas B. Luckie](https://web.archive.org/web/20060914193322/http://www.msu.edu/~luckie/dluckie.html) in April 1997, this user group became a [special interest group](/terms/special-interest-group) (Newton SIG) within the Michigan State Palmtop Users Group (MSPUG) after the Newton was discontinued.

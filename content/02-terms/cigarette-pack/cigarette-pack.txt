Title: Cigarette Pack

----

Date:

----

Text:

A nickname for the [Newton Fax Modem](/terms/newton-fax-modem). So called because its dimensions were similar to that of a package of cigarettes. Also referred to as the [Cigarette Case](/terms/cigarette-case) or [Cigarette Box](/terms/cigarette-box) modem.

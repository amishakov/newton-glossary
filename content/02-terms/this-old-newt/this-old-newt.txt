Title: This Old Newt

----

Date: 20 March 2017

----

Text:

A website established in 1997 by [Rich Lindsay](/sources/rich-lindsay) which provided a comprehensive assortment of Newton resources including news updates, documentation, tips, software downloads, a pricing guide, and links. This Old Newt also hosted the [Newtie Awards](/terms/newtie-award), an annual award that recognized contributions made to the Newton community.

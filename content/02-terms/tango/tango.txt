Title: Tango

----

Date: 05 November 2017

----

Text:

A two-way wireless pager developed by [Motorola](http://www.motorola.com/) that featured preprogrammed responses and an [RS-232](/terms/rs-232) data port for downloading messages. The Tango was compatible with Newton devices using the [Newton Messaging Enabler](/terms/newton-messaging-enabler).

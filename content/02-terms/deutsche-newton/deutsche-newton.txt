Title: Deutsche Newton

----

Date: 19 November 2018

----

Text:

A colloquial term referring to a Newton device running a German version of [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os). German localized operating systems were available for the [Original MessagePad](/terms/original-messagepad), [MessagePad 100](/terms/messagepad-100), [MessagePad 120](/terms/messagepad-120), [MessagePad 130](/terms/messagepad-130), [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100), and certain third-party Newton devices such as the Siemens [NotePhone](/terms/notephone).

Title: eMate 300

----

Date:

----

Text:

A ruggedized, translucent [clamshell](/terms/clamshell) style Newton device that featured a built-in keyboard, extremely long battery life, and an integrated suite of [applications](/terms/application) called [Newton Works](/terms/newton-works). The device was primarily aimed at the educational market.

The eMate 300 was known by various code names, including [Project K](/terms/project-k), [Schoolbook](/terms/schoolbook), and [Shay](/terms/shay).

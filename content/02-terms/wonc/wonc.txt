Worldwide Online Newton Conference

A virtual gathering of Newton users, developers, and aficionados capturing the spirit of the previous [Worldwide Newton Conference](/terms/wwnc) events. Organized by [Pawel Piotrowski](http://applenewton.co.uk/), the inaugural WONC was held in May 2020.

Title: Prota

----

Date: 27 August 2018

----

Text:

An implementation of [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript) written in the C programming language. Originally called [Proto](/terms/proto), Prota was created by [Walter Smith](/sources/walter-smith), the original designer of NewtonScript.

Title: QuickFigure Pro

----

Date: 23 November 2017

----

Text:

A full-featured, standalone [application](/terms/application) developed by [PelicanWare](/sources/pelicanware) that allows the user to create and edit [spreadsheets](/terms/spreadsheet) on a Newton device. A simplified version called [QuickFigure Works](/terms/quickfigure-works) provides most of the same functionality within [Newton Works](/terms/newton-works).

Also referred to as [QF Pro](/terms/qf-pro).

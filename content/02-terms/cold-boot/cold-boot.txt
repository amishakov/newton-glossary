Title: Cold Boot

----

Date:

----

Text:

A method of [resetting](/terms/reset) the [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) to its factory defaults. A cold boot erases all data in the internal memory including [applications](/terms/application) and [packages](/terms/package) installed by the user, removes all [system updates](/terms/system-update), and resets [pen](/terms/pen) alignment, date, time, time zone, and other [preferences](/terms/preferences).

Not to be confused with [hard reset](/terms/hard-reset) or [brain wipe](/terms/brain-wipe), which do not remove any [system updates](/terms/system-update).

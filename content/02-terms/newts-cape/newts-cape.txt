Title: Newt’s Cape
	
----

Text:

A web browser for Newton devices developed by [Steve Weyer](/sources/steve-weyer). Newt’s Cape is able to create [Newton books](/terms/newton-book) containing text, images, links, and forms from existing HTML documents. With the ability to embed [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript) objects and methods into these documents, Newt’s Cape can also act as a simple [application](/terms/application) development environment.

Title: BackTalk

----

Date: 27 May 2018

----

Text:

Software developed by [Smart Dog Software](/sources/smartdog) that provides updated [infrared](/terms/infrared) communications [protocols](/terms/protocol) for certain Newton devices. BackTalk is comprised of several system level components including a beam [transport](/terms/transport) and [IrDA](/terms/irda) object exchange stack, providing the ability to exchange information with other devices such as Palm OS-based handhelds.

Title: Graffiti

----

Date:

----

Text:

A software-based input system that converts predefined [pen](/terms/pen) strokes and [gestures](/terms/gesture) into digital text. Graffiti was originally developed by [Palm Computing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Palm,_Inc.) as the recognition system for GEOS and Magic Cap devices, later adapted to the Palm and [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) platforms.

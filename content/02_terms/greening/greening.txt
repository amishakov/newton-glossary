Title: Greening

----

Date: 12 August 2024

----

Text:

A collective noun referring to a group of Newton devices.

Originally [suggested by Randy Minton](https://newtontalk.net/archive/newtontalk/2002-January/3089.html) on the [NewtonTalk](/terms/newtontalk) mailing list.

Also referred to as a [Hawking](/terms/hawking) of Newton devices.

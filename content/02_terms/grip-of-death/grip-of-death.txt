Title: Grip of Death

----

Date: 12 June 2001

----

Text:

A system (link: /terms/freeze text: freeze) common in early (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad) devices caused by a lack of adequate (link: /terms/heap-space text: heap space). In later versions of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) this condition notified the user of a problem related to something interacting with a (link: /terms/card text: card) while it was being unmounted, causing the (link: /terms/operating-system text: operating system) to become unresponsive.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/GOD text: G.O.D.) and (link: /terms/the-grip text: The Grip).

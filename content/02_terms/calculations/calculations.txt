Title: Calculations

----

Date: 01 January 2018

----

Text:

The document format (or (link: /terms/stationery text: stationery)) used by the (link: /terms/graphing-calculator text: graphing calculator) component of the (link: /terms/newton-works text: Newton Works) application.

When a new calculations document is created, it is assigned the default title “(link: /terms/untitled-calculations text: Untitled Calculations)”, which can be changed in the (link: /terms/title slip text: title slip).

Title: Meeting Notes

----

Date: 28 December 2024

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/slip text: slip) that appears when (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the “Add Notes” (link: /terms/button text: button) for a (link: /terms/meeting text: meeting) or (link: /terms/event text: event). This slip provides a means to annotate or provide additional information about the item.

On devices running (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) 2.0 and later, the notes slip for an event is named (link: /terms/event-notes text: Event Notes).

Also referred to as (link: /terms/calendar-notes text: Calendar Notes) and (link: /terms/day-notes text: Day Notes).

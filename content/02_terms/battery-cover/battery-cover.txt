Title: Battery Cover

----

Date: 05 August 2024

----

Text:

A removable part on a [MessagePad](/terms/messagepad) device or [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) which covers the area where individual [batteries](/terms/battery) or a [battery pack](/terms/battery-pack) are stored.

Also referred to as an [Access Cover](/terms/access-cover), [Access Door](/terms/access-door), [Battery Access Cover](/terms/battery-access-cover), [Battery Compartment Cover](/terms/battery-compartment-cover), or [Cover](/terms/cover).

Title: Corrupt Package

----

Date: 11 October 2024

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/package-file text: package file) that has become damaged and can no longer be installed or removed from a Newton device. In the case of a corrupted (link: /terms/application text: application), attempting to launch the application by (link: /terms/tapped text: tapping) its (link: /terms/icon text: icon) will likely produce an (link: /terms/alert-box text: alert box), indicating that there is a problem with the package.

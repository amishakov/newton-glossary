Title: Getting Things Done

----

Date: 08 June 2024

----

Text:

A time management and personal productivity system developed by author and consultant David Allen in 2001. Also known as (link: /terms/gtd text: GTD), the system is based on efficiently storing, tracking, and retrieving information related to tasks that are to be accomplished.

According to (link: /sources/eckhart-koppen text: Eckhart Köppen), this type of task management is perfectly suited to the (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) platform. To prove this, Köppen developed two pieces of Newton software specifically supporting the concepts of GTD, (link: /terms/flashpoint text: Flashpoint) and (link: /terms/nitch text: Nitch).

Title: Messenotizen

----

Date: 13 November 2017

----

Text:

An alternate (and erroneously applied) name for the prototype Newton device developed by [Siemens](https://www.siemens.com/) known as the (link: /terms/siemens-online-terminal text: Siemens Online Terminal). Literally translated from German, the term “Messenotizen” becomes “Fair Notes”.

Title: Mail V

----

Date:

----

Text:

An email [transport](/terms/transport) created by [Simon Bell](/sources/simon-bell) that allows the user to send and receive messages over the internet using the POP3, IMAP4, and SMTP. Mail V is also MIME compliant, enabling the user to send, receive, and forward attachments such as Newton [packages](/terms/package), [Notepad](/terms/notepad) sketches and recordings, as well as [vCards](/terms/vcard).

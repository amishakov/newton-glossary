Title: Mail V

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

An email (link: /terms/transport text: transport) created by (link: /sources/simon-bell text: Simon Bell) that allows the user to send and receive messages over the internet using the POP3, IMAP4, and SMTP. Mail V is also MIME compliant, enabling the user to send, receive, and forward attachments such as Newton (link: /terms/package text: packages), (link: /terms/notepad text: Notepad) sketches and recordings, as well as (link: /terms/vcard text: vCards).

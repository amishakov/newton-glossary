Title: Notepad

----

Date:

----

Text:

The built-in [application](/terms/application) for creating and organizing items containing various types information. Items can be in the form of a [note](/terms/note), checklist, outline, or audio recording using different types of [stationery](/terms/stationery). On a [MessagePad](/terms/messagepad) device, Notepad is the default [backdrop](/terms/backdrop) application.

Also referred to as [Notes](/terms/notes).

Notepad is also the original name of the first Newton device, later changed to [MessagePad](/terms/messagepad) to better reflect its communication capabilities.

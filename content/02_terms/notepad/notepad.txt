Title: Notepad

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The built-in (link: /terms/application text: application) for creating and organizing items containing various types information. Items can be in the form of a (link: /terms/note text: note), checklist, outline, or audio recording using different types of (link: /terms/stationery text: stationery). On a (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad) device, Notepad is the default (link: /terms/backdrop text: backdrop) application.

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), the (link: /terms/notepad text: Notepad) is referenced programmatically as `paperroll`.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/notes text: Notes).

Notepad is also the original name of the first Newton device, later changed to (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad) to better reflect its communication capabilities.

Title: Spreadsheet

----

Date: 23 November 2017

----

Text:

The document format (or (link: /terms/stationery text: stationery)) provided by the (link: /terms/quickfigure-works text: QuickFigure Works) component of the (link: /terms/newton-works text: Newton Works) application. A spreadsheet allows the user to store, analyze, manipulate, and calculate data such as numbers, formulas, and text.

When a new spreadsheet document is created, it is assigned the default title “(link: /terms/untitled-spreadsheet text: Untitled Spreadsheet)”, which can be changed in the (link: /terms/title slip text: title slip).

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/worksheet text: worksheet).

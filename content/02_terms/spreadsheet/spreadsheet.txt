Title: Spreadsheet

----

Date: 23 November 2017

----

Text:

The document format (or [stationery](/terms/stationery)) provided by the [QuickFigure Works](/terms/quickfigure-works) component of the [Newton Works](/terms/newton-works) application. A spreadsheet allows the user to store, analyze, manipulate, and calculate data such as numbers, formulas, and text.

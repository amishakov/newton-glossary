Title: Newton Works

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

An integrated (link: /terms/application text: application) developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) for devices running (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) 2.1 only. Functionality includes a word processor, drawing tools, and a (link: /terms/graphing-calculator text: graphing calculator). When installed on a Newton device, the application appears as (link: /terms/works text: Works) in the (link: /terms/extras-drawer text: Extras Drawer). On an (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300), Newton Works is the default (link: /terms/backdrop text: backdrop) application.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/newtworks text: NewtWorks) or (link: /terms/works text: Works).

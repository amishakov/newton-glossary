Title: Dylan

----

Date: 12 June 2001

----

Text:

The (link: /terms/lisp text: Lisp)-like dynamic programming language developed at [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) and used to develop portions of the original (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) and its (link: /terms/user-interface text: user interface).

Also referred to as (link: /terms/denali text: Denali). An early version of Dylan was known as (link: /terms/ralph text: Ralph).

Title: Dylan

----

Date:

----

Text:

The [Lisp](/terms/lisp)-like dynamic programming language developed at [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) and used to develop portions of the original [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) and its [user interface](/terms/user-interface).

Also referred to as [Denali](/terms/denali). An early version of Dylan was known as [Ralph](/terms/ralph).

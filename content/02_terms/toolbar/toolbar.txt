Title: Toolbar

----

Date: 26 August 2025

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/user-interface text: user interface) element consisting of a row of (link: /terms/icon text: icons) representing various tools, (link: /terms/command text: commands), or (link: /terms/action text: actions) associated with an (link: /terms/application text: application).

(link: /terms/tapping text: Tapping) an icon will perform the action or change the mode of operation related to that icon.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/floating-palette text: floating palette).

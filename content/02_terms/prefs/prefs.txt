Title: Prefs

----

Date: 03 September 2017

----

Text:

The built-in (link: /terms/application text: application) for changing system-wide (link: /terms/preferences text: preferences) related to the operation of a Newton device. (link: /terms/tapping text: Tapping) a topic in the Preferences list will open a (link: /terms/slip text: slip) where settings pertaining to that topic can be adjusted.

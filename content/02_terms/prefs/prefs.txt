Title: Prefs

----

Date: 03 September 2017

----

Text:

The built-in [application](/terms/application) for changing system-wide [preferences](/terms/preferences) related to the operation of a Newton device. [Tapping](/terms/tapping) a topic in the Preferences list will open a [slip](/terms/slip) where settings pertaining to that topic can be adjusted.

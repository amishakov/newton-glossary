Title: Owner Info

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The built-in [application](/terms/application) for setting [owner](/terms/owner) and [worksite](/terms/worksite) information on a Newton device. It is possible to set up multiple owner profiles or [personas](/terms/persona) for the same owner.

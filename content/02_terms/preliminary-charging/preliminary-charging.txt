Title: Preliminary Charging

----

Date: 21 February 2025

----

Text:

The (link: /terms/charge-state text: charge state) of a rechargeable (link: /terms/battery text: battery) indicating that it is currently having its electrical energy restored using a pulsed schedule that gradually increases its voltage to a level at which it can be efficiently (link: /terms/fast-charging text: fast charged). Generally, (link: /terms/charging text: charging) occurs when a device is connected to an external power source.

This mode of charging is initially used for charging a heavily (link: /terms/discharged text: discharged) battery.

Title: Inheritance

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript), the mechanism by which attributes ([slots](/terms/slot) or data) and behaviours ([methods](/terms/method)) are made available to [objects](/terms/object). [Parent](/terms/parent) inheritance allows [views](/terms/view) of dissimilar types to share slots containing data or methods. Prototype inheritance allows a [template](/terms/template) to base its definition on that of another template or [prototype](/terms/prototype).

Title: Transport

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript), an [object](/terms/object) in [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript) that provides a communication service to the [InOut Box](/terms/inout-box) application, interfacing with an [endpoint](/terms/endpoint). Examples include the print, fax, [beam](/terms/beam), and email transports.

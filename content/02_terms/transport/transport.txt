Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), an (link: /terms/object text: object) in (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript) that provides a communication service to the (link: /terms/inout-box text: InOut Box) application, interfacing with an (link: /terms/endpoint text: endpoint). Examples include the print, fax, (link: /terms/beam text: beam), and email transports.

Title: Tap-and-a-Half

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

To (link: /terms/tap text: tap), then quickly lift, touch, and hold the (link: /terms/pen text: pen) in the same spot on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen). Essentially performing a down, up, down action with the pen.

A tap-and-a-half action is used when copying a selected item to the (link: /terms/clipboard text: clipboard).

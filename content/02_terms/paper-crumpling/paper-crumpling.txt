Title: Paper Crumpling

----

Date: 13 June 2025

----

Text:

The initial (link: /terms/action-sound-effect text: action sound effect) that is played when deleting an item in the (link: /terms/Notepad text: Notepad) application. The sound accompanies an animation which simulates an item being wadded into a ball before be tossed into a trash can.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/crumple text: Crumple).

Title: Paper Crumpling

----

Date: 13 June 2025

----

Text:

The initial (link: /terms/action-sound-effect text: action sound effect) that is played when (link: /terms/delete text: deleting) an item in an (link: /terms/application text: application) such as the (link: /terms/Notepad text: Notepad). The sound accompanies an animation which simulates an item being wadded into a ball before be tossed into a (link: /terms/trash-can text: trash can). When the animated item hits the trash can, a second “(link: /terms/plunk text: plunk)” sound is played.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/crumple text: Crumple).

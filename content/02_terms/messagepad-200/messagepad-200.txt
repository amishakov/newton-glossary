Title: MessagePad 200

----

Date: 18 August 2024

----

Text:

A common typographic error made when referring to the [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000).

Also a rumoured successor to the [MessagePad 120](/terms/messagepad-120).

Not to be confused with Matthew B. McRae’s proposed [Newton 200](/terms/newton-200) design concept.

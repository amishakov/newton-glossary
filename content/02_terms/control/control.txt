Title: Control

----

Date:

----

Text:

A [user interface](/terms/user-interface) element or [object](/terms/object) in a [container view](/terms/container-view) that a user can manipulate with a [pen](/terms/pen) to cause instant action with visible results or to change settings that modify a future action.

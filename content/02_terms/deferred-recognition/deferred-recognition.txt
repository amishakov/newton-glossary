Title: Deferred Recognition

----

Date:

----

Text:

The process of recognizing an [ink word](/terms/ink-word) that was drawn by the user at an earlier time. Usually initiated when the user [double taps](/terms/double-tap) on an ink word. Deferred recognition was introduced with [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) 1.3.

Title: The Newton Store

----

Date: 18 October 2018

----

Text:

A defunct chain of Canadian retail outlets that specialized in showcasing and supporting Newton hardware, software, and accessories. At one point, The Newton Store had locations in Toronto, Montréal, and Vancouver. They were know for providing toll-free ordering, a customer club, and a seminar series.

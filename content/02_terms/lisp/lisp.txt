Title: Lisp

----

Date: 29 June 2017

----

Text:

A high-level programming language known for its abundant use of parentheses. (link: /terms/macintosh-common-lisp text: Macintosh Common Lisp) is an implementation of Lisp that provided a development environment with tight integration with the Mac OS toolbox. An early, unreleased version of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) known as (link: /terms/bauhaus text: Bauhaus) was developed using the Lisp-like language (link: /terms/ralph text: Ralph).

Title: Recently Written Words

----

Date: 14 October 2017

----

Text:

A [slip](/terms/slip) which displays a list of words from the [auto add dictionary](/terms/auto-add-dictionary) when the user opens the [Personal Word List](/terms/personal-word-list). Words in this list are not contained in the built-in [system dictionary](/terms/system-dictionary) and have been automatically added to the [user dictionary](/terms/user-dictionary).

The Recently Written Words slip prompts the user to indicate whether each of the words it displays should remain in the user dictionary.

Title: Magic Pointers Table

----

Date:

----

Text:

A series of NewtonScript [pointers](/terms/pointer) that indicate the location of various [objects](/terms/object). This table can be partially replaced by [system patch pages](/terms/system-patch-pages), allowing a [system patch](/terms/system-patch) to replace specific [magic pointers](/terms/magic-pointer).

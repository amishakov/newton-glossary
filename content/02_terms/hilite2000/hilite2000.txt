Title: Hilite2000

----

Date: 11 December 2024

----

Text:

A system enhancement developed by (link: /sources/byte-size text: Byte-Size Software) that provides easy access to various tools for editing text, changing (link: /terms/font text: font) attributes, and managing clipboard actions. The tools are automatically displayed as (link: /terms/button text: buttons) in a floating (link: /terms/palette text: palette) when text is (link: /terms/select text: selected).

(image: /assets/images/hilite2000-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of the Hilite2000 floating palette width: 80)

Also known as (link: /terms/h2k text: H2K).

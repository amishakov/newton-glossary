Title: EM300

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

An internal memory upgrade for the (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) developed by (link: /sources/david-humphreys text: David Humphreys). When installed, the (link: /terms/system-ram text: system RAM) is increased from 1MB to 4MB and the default data path is widened, improving the responsiveness of the device.

Title: EM300

----

Date:

----

Text:

An internal memory upgrade for the [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) developed by [David Humphreys](/sources/david-humphreys). When installed, the [system RAM](/terms/system-ram) is increased from 1MB to 4MB and the default data path is widened, improving the responsiveness of the device.

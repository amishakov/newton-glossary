Title: RS-232

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A standard for serial communication between computers and peripheral devices such as (link: /terms/modem text: modems) and terminals. Devices that support RS-232 are generally outfitted with either (link: /terms/db-25 text: DB-25) or (link: /terms/de-9 text: DE-9) connectors.

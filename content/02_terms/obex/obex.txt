Text:

Object Exchange

A communications (link: /terms/protocol text: protocol) that facilitates the exchange of (link: /terms/binary-object text: binary objects) such as contact information or applications between devices over (link: /terms/infrared text: infrared). Although initially designed for infrared, OBEX has also been adopted for use over Bluetooth and other serial connections.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/irobex text: IrOBEX) and (link: /terms/irda-obex text: IrDA OBEX).

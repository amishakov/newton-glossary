Title: Synonyms

----

Date: 10 December 2023

----

Text:

Alternate [request words](/terms/request-word) that trigger an [action](/terms/action) when processed by the [Assist](/terms/assist) function.

For example, the request word “call” has several synonyms, including “ring”, “dial”, and “phone”.

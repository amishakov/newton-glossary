Title: Workfile

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The main data file created by the (link: /terms/newton-connection text: Newton Connection) software. The workfile contains all of the information used when (link: /terms/synchronize text: synchronizing) a Newton device with a (link: /terms/desktop-computer text: desktop computer), (link: /terms/laptop-computer text: laptop), or other device.

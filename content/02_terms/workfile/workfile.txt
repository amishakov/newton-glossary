Title: Workfile

----

Date:

----

Text:

The main data file created by the [Newton Connection](/terms/newton-connection) software. The workfile contains all of the information used when [synchronizing](/terms/synchronize) a Newton device with a [desktop computer](/terms/desktop-computer), [laptop](/terms/laptop-computer), or other device.

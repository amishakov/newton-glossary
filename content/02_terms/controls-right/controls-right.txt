Title: Controls Right

----

Date: 03 January 2025

----

Text:

An option in the (link: /terms/rotate-button text: Rotate Button) picker which moves the (link: /terms/overview-button text: overview button) and (link: /terms/universal-scroll-arrows text: universal scroll arrows) to the right side of the (link: /terms/button-bar text: button bar) when the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) is in (link: /terms/portrait-mode text: portrait mode).

If the controls are currently displayed on the right side of the screen, the option will be named (link: /terms/controls-left text: Controls Left).

(image: /assets/images/controls-right-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of the Rotate Button with Picker showing the Controls Right option width: 164)

This option only appears on (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000) and (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100) devices.

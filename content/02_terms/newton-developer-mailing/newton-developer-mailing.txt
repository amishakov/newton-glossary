Title: Newton Developer Mailing

----

Date: 24 April 2017

----

Text:

A monthly package mailed to Newton developer program members that included the quarterly [Newton Developer CD](/terms/newton-developer-cd), the bimonthly [Newton Technology Journal](/terms/newton-technology-journal), and other periodicals published by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/). The Newton Developer Mailing contained information such as sample code, human interface guidelines, programming advice, product news, and market research.

Title: User Dictionary

----

Date: 14 October 2017

----

Text:

A [dictionary](/terms/dictionary) accessed through the [Personal Word List](/terms/personal-word-list) slip. The built-in vocabulary of [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) can be expanded by adding new words to the user dictionary via the [Recently Written Words](/terms/recently-written-words) slip.

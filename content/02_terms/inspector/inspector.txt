Title: Inspector

----

Date: 09 October 2017

----

Text:

A [debugging](/terms/debugging) window in the [Newton Toolkit](/terms/newton-toolkit) development environment that allows a user to browse the [object](/terms/object) storage system and execute [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript) code on a Newton device.

The Inspector communicates with the [Toolkit](/terms/toolkit) application installed on a Newton device connected to a [desktop computer](/terms/desktop-computer) or [laptop computer](/terms/laptop-computer) via a serial cable or [AppleTalk](/terms/appletalk) network.

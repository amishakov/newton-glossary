Title: MessagePad

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The base model name for all [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) manufactured Newton devices except the (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300). The original name of the first Newton device was actually (link: /terms/notepad text: Notepad). It was changed to MessagePad to better reflect the communication capabilities of the device.

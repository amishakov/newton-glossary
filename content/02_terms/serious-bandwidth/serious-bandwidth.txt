Title: Serious Bandwidth

----

Date: 12 June 2001

----

Text:

A legendary anecdote where a Newton user named (link: /sources/robert-mcnally text: Robert McNally) entered Lewis Carroll’s _Jabberwocky_ into an original Newton device and had the (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) generate what was basically a new lyric poem — known variously as (link: /terms/tablespoons text: Tablespoons) or (link: /terms/newtonwocky text: Newtonwocky). The phrase _“Serious Bandwidth”_ was the translation of _“Frumious Bandersnatch”_ from the original poem.

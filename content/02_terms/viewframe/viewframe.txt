----

Text:

A (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript) inspection and (link: /terms/debugging text: debugging) utility for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) developed by [Jason Harper](http://nixietube.info/) and distributed by (link: /sources/creative-digital-publishing text: Creative Digital Publishing). ViewFrame allows the user to navigate, inspect, and modify NewtonScript (link: /terms/object text: objects) and (link: /terms/soup text: soup) data.

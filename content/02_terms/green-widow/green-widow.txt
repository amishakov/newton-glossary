Title: Green Widow

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A somewhat negative name given to the partner, spouse, or significant other of a Newton user, particularly a Newton user suffering from the (link: /terms/green-bug text: Green Bug). Refers to the inexplicable amount of time some Newton users spend with their devices, rather than their partners.

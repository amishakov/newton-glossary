Title: Gauge

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

An (link: /terms/object text: object) with a marker that indicates an amount, degree, or value in relation to a range of possible values. A user can only read a gauge, not manipulate it like a (link: /terms/slider text: slider) or other (link: /terms/user-interface text: user interface) element.

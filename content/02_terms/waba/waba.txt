Title: Waba

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A highly portable, simplified implementation of the [Java](https://java.com/) virtual machine created specifically for small computing devices. [Sean Luke](/sources/sean-luke) is responsible for the [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) version of Waba.

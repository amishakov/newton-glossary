Title: Print Recognizer

----

Date:

----

Text:

The algorithmic component of the [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) system that identifies printed characters, as opposed to [cursive](/terms/cursive) or handwritten text.

Also referred to as the [Disconnected Recognizer](/terms/disconnected-recognizer).

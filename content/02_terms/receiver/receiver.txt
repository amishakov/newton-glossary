Title: Receiver

----

Date:

----

Text:

The [frame](/terms/frame) that was sent a [message](/terms/message) to execute a [method](/terms/method). The receiver for the invocation of a function [object](/terms/object) is accessible through the pseudo-[variable](/terms/variable) `self`.

Title: Receiver

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), the (link: /terms/frame text: frame) that was sent a (link: /terms/message text: message) to execute a (link: /terms/method text: method). The receiver for the invocation of a function (link: /terms/object text: object) is accessible through the pseudo-(link: /terms/variable text: variable) `self`.

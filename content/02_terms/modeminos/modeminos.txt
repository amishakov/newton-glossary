Title: Modeminos

----

Date: 17 March 2018

----

Text:


A chain of dominos created by members of Apple’s [Developer Technical Services](/terms/developer-technical-services) team using 4,000 [Newton Fax Modems](/terms/newton-fax-modem). Originally shot by [Maurice “Dr Llama” Sharp](/sources/maurice-sharp) on a “slow” day in 1996, the “Modeminos” video was included on several editions of the [Newton Developer CD](/terms/newton-developer-cd).

Title: The Incredible Shrinking Newt

----

Date: 04 November 2023

----

Text:

An experimental project by (link: /sources/igor-boris-bertolucci text: Igor Boris Bertolucci), where he attempted to reduce the overall physical size of a (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000). The project involved removing the casing, replacing the original power source with a modern Lithium Polymer battery and charging system, as well as a (link: /terms/ftdi text: FTDI)-compliant USB to serial converter.

Also referred to as the (link: /terms/shrinking-newt text: Shrinking Newt).

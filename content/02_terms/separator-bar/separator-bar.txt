Title: Separator Bar

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A thick black (link: /terms/line text: line) located at the top of each item in a (link: /terms/view text: view) that is displaying multiple items at once. The separator bar contains information related to the item appearing directly below it and includes controls which allow the user to interact with the item.

For example, the separator bar in the (link: /terms/application text: Notepad) contains an (link: /terms/item-info-button text: item info button), the date when the (link: /terms/note text: note) was created, an (link: /terms/action-button text: action button), and a (link: /terms/folder-button text: folder button).

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/divider-bar text: Divider Bar).

Title: Sound Frame

----

Date: 10 January 2024

----

Text:

In [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript), the [frame](/terms/frame) that defines a sound. The sound frame stores sound [sample](/terms/sample) data and additional information used internally by the system in one or more [slots](/terms/slot) defining various parameters. This information can include [binary objects](/terms/binary-object) of the sound samples, the number of samples, the sampling rate, the [codec](/terms/codec) used to encode the samples, the [volume](/terms/volume) the samples should be played back at, and how many times they should be repeated or looped.

Title: Slot Jam

----

Date:

----

Text:

A mechanical condition where a (link: /terms/pc-card text: PC card) cannot be ejected from a Newton device.

Slot jam can also refer to the schmutz that collects in a PC card (link: /terms/slot text: slot) over time.

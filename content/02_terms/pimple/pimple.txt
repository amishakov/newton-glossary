Title: PIMple

----

Date: 17 July 2025

----

Text:

A system enhancement developed by (link: /sources/byte-size text: Byte-Size Software) that provides a compact (link: /terms/calendar text: calendar) view of information from the (link: /terms/date-book text: Date Book) application, allowing the user to quickly view their schedule, including available time slots, as well as conflicting (link: /terms/meeting text: meetings).

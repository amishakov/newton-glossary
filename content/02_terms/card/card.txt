Title: Card

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/view text: view) of information pertaining to an entry in the (link: /terms/name-file text: Name File) application (link: /terms/soup text: soup), graphically formatted as a business card. A card can display names, addresses, telephone numbers, email addresses, notes, and other information (link: /terms/field text: fields) created by the user.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/name-card text: Name Card).

Card is also an (link: /terms/application text: application) that allows the user to retrieve information about a PC card inserted into a Newton device, displaying that information in a (link: /terms/card-slip text: card slip).

Card can also refer to a (link: /terms/pc-card text: PC Card) or (link: /terms/compactflash text: CompactFlash) card.

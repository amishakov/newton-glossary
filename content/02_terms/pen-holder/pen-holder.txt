Title: Pen Holder

----

Date: 10 December 2023

----

Text:

A slot or well built into a Newton device to store or hold the [pen](/terms/pen) when not in use. Along with a pair of wells on either side of the keyboard, the [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) also has a recessed [pen tray](/terms/pen-tray) to store the pen when the lid is closed.

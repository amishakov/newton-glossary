Title: Corrupted

----

Date: 15 December 2024

----

Text:

The state in which a file, (link: /terms/application text: application), (link: /terms/package text: package), (link: /terms/soup text: soup), or some other data in memory has been accidentally or inadvertently modified or damaged by a hardware or software failure. Because it has been changed, the data is rendered either unreadable or scrambled.

Title: Newton Language Kit

----

Date: 06 February 2024

----

Text:

Software developed by [Enfour](/sources/enfour) that provided a Japanese language environment for devices running [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) 2.1. Although focused on Japanese, Newton Language Kit was designed to support multiple languages.

Also abbreviated as [NLK](/terms/nlk).

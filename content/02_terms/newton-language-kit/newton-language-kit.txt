Title: Newton Language Kit

----

Date: 06 February 2024

----

Text:

Software developed by (link: /sources/enfour text: Enfour) that provided a Japanese language environment for devices running (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) 2.1. Although focused on Japanese, Newton Language Kit was designed to support multiple languages.

Also abbreviated as (link: /terms/nlk text: NLK).

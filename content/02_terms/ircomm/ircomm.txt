Infrared Communications Protocol

The optional communications protocol layer of the [IrDA](/terms/irda) communications [protocol](/terms/protocol) specification, which allows an [infrared](/terms/infrared) device to behave like a serial or parallel port. IrCOMM is built on the [Tiny TP](/terms/tiny-tp) transport, which is turn sits on top of the [IrLMP](/terms/irlmp) layer.

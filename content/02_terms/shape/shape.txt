Title: Shape

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/picture text: picture) composed of geometric constructs such as (link: /terms/line text: lines), (link: /terms/arc text: arcs), circles, (link: /terms/oval text: ovals), (link: /terms/rectangle text: rectangles), and other (link: /terms/polygon text: polygons).

A shape is also a structured piece of data used by the (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) drawing system to render an image on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen).

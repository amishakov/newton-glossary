Title: Slow Infrared

----

Date:

----

Text:

Transferring data using an [IrDA](/terms/irda)-compatible [infrared](/terms/infrared) connection at a rate up to 115 Kbps. As opposed to [Fast Infrared](/terms/fast-infrared) which only transfers data at a rate up to 4 Mbps.

Slow Infrared is also referred to as [SIR](/terms/sir).

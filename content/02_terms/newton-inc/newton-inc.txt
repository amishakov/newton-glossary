Title: Newton Inc.

----

Date:

----

Text:

The short-lived subsidiary of [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) that was spun out of the [Newton Systems Group](/terms/newton-systems-group). Its focus was developing, marketing, and distributing products and technologies based on the [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os). The subsidiary was announced on May 22nd, 1997 and subsequently rolled back into Apple several months later, after the return of Steve Jobs.

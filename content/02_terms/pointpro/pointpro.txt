A presentation (link: /terms/application text: application) for the Newton developed by (link: /sources/pelicanware text: PelicanWare). PointPro allows the user to create full-colour, full-screen slide presentations that can be displayed on external monitors, televisions, and video projectors using a (link: /terms/pc-card text: PC card) such as the (link: /terms/ez2tv text: EZ2TV) or (link: /terms/ez2vga text: EZ2VGA) products from (link: /sources/ezshow-systems text: EZShow Systems).

Text:

Upgraded MessagePad

Generally referring to a [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000) device that has had the system memory upgraded to the equivalent of a [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100). Also known variously as [MP2000+](/terms/mp2000-plus), [MP2000U](/terms/mp2000u), [MP2KU](/terms/mp2ku), and [UMP2K](/terms/ump2k).

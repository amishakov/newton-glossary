Title: Craft Digital Assistant

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A specialized (link: /terms/personal-digital-assistant text: personal digital assistant) (PDA) that can access the [Craft Access System](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Craft_access_system), allowing telecommunication field technicians to access work orders and other information through the telephone system. Also referred to as a (link: /terms/cda text: CDA), playing off the term (link: /terms/pda text: PDA). The Harris Dracon (link: /terms/supertech-2000 text: SuperTech 2000) is an example of a craft digital assistant running (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS).

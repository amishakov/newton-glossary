Title: Paper

----

Date: 23 November 2017

----

Text:

The document format (or (link: /terms/stationery text: stationery)) used by the word processor component of the (link: /terms/newton-works text: Newton Works) application. The Newton Works word processor does not utilize (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition), so text must be entered into the word processor using a keyboard.

When a new paper document is created, it is assigned the default title “(link: /terms/untitled-paper text: Untitled Paper)”, which can be changed in the (link: /terms/title slip text: title slip).

(image: /assets/images/paper-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of a Paper document in Newton Works width: 482)

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/word-processing-document text: Word Processing Document).

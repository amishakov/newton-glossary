Title: Gesture

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A handwritten mark that is recognized as having a special meaning by (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS). Standard gestures recognized on the Newton include (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping), (link: /terms/scrubbing text: scrubbing), and the (link: /terms/caret text: caret).

The (link: /terms/graffiti text: Graffiti) input system is also based on gestures.

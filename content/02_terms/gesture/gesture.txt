Title: Gesture

----

Date:

----

Text:

A handwritten mark that is recognized as having a special meaning by the [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os). Standard gestures recognized on the Newton include [tapping](/terms/tapping), [scrubbing](/terms/scrubbing), and the [caret](/terms/caret).

The [Graffiti](/terms/graffiti) input system is also based on gestures.

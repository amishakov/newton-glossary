Title: Realign

----

Date: 26 June 2024

----

Text:

The process of resetting the (link: /terms/pen-alignment text: pen alignment) or the accuracy of the (link: /terms/pen text: pen) when it interacts with the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) on a Newton device. For example, if a (link: /terms/tap text: tap) affects an area of the screen that is not directly under the tip of the pen, the user would tap the (link: /terms/align-pen text: Align Pen) button located in the (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: Handwriting Recognition) slip.

Title: Realign

----

Date: 26 June 2024

----

Text:

The process of resetting the [pen alignment](/terms/pen-alignment) or the accuracy of the [pen](/terms/pen) when it interacts with the [screen](/terms/screen) on a Newton device. For example, if a [tap](/terms/tap) affects an area of the screen that is not directly under the tip of the pen, the user would tap the [Align Pen](/terms/align-pen) button located in the [Handwriting Recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) slip.

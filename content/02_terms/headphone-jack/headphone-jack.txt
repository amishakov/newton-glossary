Title: Headphone Jack

----

Date: 01 June 2025

----

Text:

A connector located on an (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) that allows the user to plug in headphones or other audio (link: /terms/output text: output) devices that uses a standard (link: /terms/mini-plug text: mini-plug). When something is plugged into the headphone jack on an eMate, the internal speaker is disabled.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/headphone-port text: Headphone Port).

Title: Dictionary Button

----

Date: 14 October 2017

----

Text:

A [picture button](/terms/picture-button) containing an [icon](/terms/icon) representing a [dictionary](/terms/dictionary). [Tapping](/terms/tapping) the dictionary button displays the [Personal Word List](/terms/personal-word-list) slip.

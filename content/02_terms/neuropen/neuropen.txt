Title: Neuropen

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

The official product name for the software-based (link: /terms/print-recognizer text: print recognizer) system developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/). More commonly known as (link: /terms/rosetta text: Rosetta) and (link: /terms/mondello text: Mondello), the Neuropen moniker was never used publicly.

Title: Memory Info Slip

----

Date: 31 May 2001

----

Text:

An (link: /terms/item-info-slip text: item info slip) that reports statistics on the amount of memory installed and available to a Newton device.

The (link: /terms/slip text: slip) is displayed by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the (link: /terms/info-button text: info button) in the (link: /terms/status-bar text: status bar) at the bottom of the (link: /terms/extras-drawer text: Extras Drawer) and selecting (link: /terms/memory-info text: Memory Info) in the (link: /terms/picker text: picker).

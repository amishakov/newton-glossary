Title: Intelligent Assistance Architecture

----

Date: 24 March 2017

----

Text:

Marketing parlance for the [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) technology that [assists](/terms/assist) users in completing repetitive tasks, such as those related to communication, scheduling events, searching for information, and setting reminders.

Text:

A simplified version of the (link: /terms/quickfigure-pro text: QuickFigure Pro) application developed by (link: /sources/pelicanware text: PelicanWare). QuickFigure Works provides the (link: /terms/spreadsheet text: spreadsheet) functionality found in the (link: /terms/newton-works text: Newton Works) application.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/qf-works text: QF Works).

Title: QuickFigure Works

----

Date: 23 November 2017

----

Text:

A simplified version of the [QuickFigure Pro](/terms/quickfigure-pro) application developed by [PelicanWare](/sources/pelicanware). QuickFigure Works provides the [spreadsheet](/terms/spreadsheet) functionality found in the [Newton Works](/terms/newton-works) application.

Also referred to as [QF Works](/terms/qf-works).

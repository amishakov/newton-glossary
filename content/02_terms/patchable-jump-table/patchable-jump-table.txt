Title: Patchable Jump Table

----

Date:

----

Text:

A series of branching instructions directly pointing to native [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) functions. These instructions are ordered differently depending on the version of the [ROM](/terms/rom). This [jump table](/terms/jump-table) can be partially replaced by [system patch pages](/terms/system-patch-pages), allowing [system patches](/terms/system-patch) to replace specific [operating system](/terms/operating-system) functions.

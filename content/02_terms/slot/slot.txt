Title: Slot

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), an element of a (link: /terms/frame text: frame) or (link: /terms/array text: array) that can hold an (link: /terms/immediate text: immediate) value or (link: /terms/reference text: reference) value.

Slot can also refer to (link: /terms/card-slot text: card slot), an opening where a (link: /terms/pc-card text: PC card) is inserted into a Newton device.

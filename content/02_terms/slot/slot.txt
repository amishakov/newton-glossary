Title: Slot

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript), an element of a [frame](/terms/frame) or [array](/terms/array) that can hold an [immediate](/terms/immediate) value or [reference](/terms/reference) value.

Slot can also refer to [card slot](/terms/card-slot), an opening where a [PC card](/terms/pc-card) is inserted into a Newton device.

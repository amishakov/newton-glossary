Title: Paperback Book

----

Date: 02 June 2025

----

Text:

An electronic document designed to be viewed on a Newton device. This type of document is created using tools such as the (link: /terms/paperback text: Paperback) application or the online book maker developed by (link: /sources/victor-rehorst text: Victor Rehorst) and delivered as an installable (link: /terms/package text: package).

Paperback books do not feature the robust formatting options or (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript) support found in (link: /terms/newton-book text: Newton books).

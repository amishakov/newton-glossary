Title: Expand Dictionary

----

Date: 27 November 2017

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/dictionary text: dictionary) that allows the user to define words and phrases that are automatically substituted for abbreviations. Along with providing common substitutions, the expand dictionary can be used to correct recurring (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) mistakes, since each word to be expanded is added to both the (link: /terms/user-dictionary text: user dictionary) and the expand dictionary.

Also referred to as the (link: /terms/auto-expansion-dictionary text: Autoexpansion Dictionary).

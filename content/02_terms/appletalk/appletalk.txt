Title: AppleTalk

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A routable networking (link: /terms/protocol text: protocol) developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) for communications between Mac OS computers and other platforms over a (link: /terms/local-area-network text: local area network). AppleTalk is a variation of (link: /terms/rs-422 text: RS-422) serial communication and is supported by physical networking such as (link: /terms/localtalk text: LocalTalk) (originally also called AppleTalk) and (link: /terms/ethernet text: Ethernet). AppleTalk over Ethernet in known as (link: /terms/ethertalk text: EtherTalk).

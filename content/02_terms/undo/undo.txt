Title: Undo

----

Date: 20 May 2024

----

Text:

A system wide feature built into [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) for cancelling the last action performed by a user. In most situations, [tapping](/terms/tapping) the Undo [icon](/terms/icon) in the [button bar](/terms/button-bar) will revert the last action, such as entering text or erasing a [sketch](/terms/sketch). Tapping the Undo icon again will redo the original action.

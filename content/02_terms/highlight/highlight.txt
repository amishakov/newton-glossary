Title: Highlight

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

To make something visually distinct, typically when text or a (link: /terms/control text: control) is (link: /terms/select text: selected). Usually indicated by reversing or (link: /terms/invert text: inverting) black and white areas.

For example, if the user (link: /terms/select text: selects) a word on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) using the (link: /terms/pen text: pen), the highlighted word will appear to have a (link: /terms/dark-box text: dark box) around it, distinguishing it from the unselected text.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/hilite text: Hilite), (link: /terms/hiliting text: Hiliting), and (link: /terms/highlighting text: Highlighting).

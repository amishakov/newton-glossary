Title: Worksite

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

An entry in the (link: /terms/owner-info text: Owner Info) application where a Newton user establishes the address and other information related to a specific location. For example, a user can configure different worksites for home and office. A worksite can include information such as street address, telephone number, dialing prefix, time zone, printer settings, and email address.

Also referred to as an (link: /terms/emporium text: Emporium) or (link: /terms/locale text: Locale).

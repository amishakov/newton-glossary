Title: Advanced Technology Group

----

Date: 18 July 2025

----

Text:

The corporate research group within [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) from 1986 and 1997. The group was focused on future technologies and research that fell outside the normal scope of other product or organizational groups. It was also known for a short period as (link: /terms/apple-research-labs text: Apple Research Labs) (ARL).

The second generation of (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) found in (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS), particularly the (link: /terms/print-recognizer text: print recognizer), was one of the key technologies developed by the Advanced Technology Group.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/atg text: ATG).

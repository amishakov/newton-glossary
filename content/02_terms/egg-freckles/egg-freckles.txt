Title: Egg Freckles

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

An (link: /terms/easter-egg text: easter egg) found in (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) 2.0 which when activated displays an image from a [Doonesbury comic strip](https://web.archive.org/web/20170629100722/http://doonesbury.washingtonpost.com/strip/set/24) lampooning the accuracy of the early Newton (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition).

(image: /assets/images/egg-freckles-2x.gif alt: Egg Freckles from Doonesbury — August 27th, 1993 width: 900)

The easter egg was removed from Newton OS 2.1, it can be restored using Avi Drissman’s (link: /terms/egg-freckles-enabler text: Egg Freckles Enabler) package.

Egg Freckles is also the name of Newton-themed weblog written by [Thomas Brand](https://eggfreckles.net/).

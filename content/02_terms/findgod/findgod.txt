Title: FindGOD

----

Date: 30 October 2019

----

Text:

A software [package](/terms/package) used in conjunction with the [Newton Toolkit](/terms/newton-toolkit) (NTK) [inspector](/terms/inspector) to help locate the [object](/terms/object) causing the infamous “[Grip of Death](/terms/grip-of-death)” issue in other packages.

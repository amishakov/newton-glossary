Title: Sharp

----

Date: 04 May 2025

----

Text:

A Japanese electronics company that manufactured several Newton devices for [Apple](https://www.apple.com/), including the (link: /terms/original-messagepad text: Original MessagePad) and (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000).

Sharp produced two versions of the (link: /terms/expertpad text: ExpertPad), their own take on a (link: /terms/pda text: PDA) running (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS).

The company was also responsible for developing the ASK (link: /terms/infrared text: infrared) communication (link: /terms/protocol text: protocol) used in early Newton devices.

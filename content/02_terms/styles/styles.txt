Title: Styles

----

Date:

----

Text:

An [application](/terms/application) in the form of a floating [palette](/terms/palette) that is used to modify various attributes of the currently [selected](/terms/select) text. Attributes include the [font](/terms/font) family, [font style](/terms/font-style), and [point](/terms/point) size. Styles is also used to adjust the pen width when drawing [shapes](/terms/shapes) and [sketches](/terms/sketches).

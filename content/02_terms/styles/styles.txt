Title: Styles

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

An (link: /terms/application text: application) in the form of a floating (link: /terms/palette text: palette) that is used to modify various attributes of the currently (link: /terms/select text: selected) text. Attributes include the (link: /terms/font text: font) family, (link: /terms/font-style text: font style), and (link: /terms/point text: point) size. Styles is also used to adjust the pen width when drawing (link: /terms/shapes text: shapes) and (link: /terms/sketches text: sketches).

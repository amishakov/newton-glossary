Title: This Old Newt

----

Date: 20 March 2017

Text:

A website established in 1997 by (link: /sources/rich-lindsay text: Rich Lindsay) which provided a comprehensive assortment of Newton resources including news updates, documentation, tips, software downloads, a pricing guide, and links. This Old Newt also hosted the (link: /terms/newtie-award text: Newtie Awards), an annual award that recognized contributions made to the Newton community.

Title: Letter Shapes

----

Date: 07 November 2017

----

Text:

A setting which helps improve (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) by allowing the user to indicate which character shapes are more common or seldom used when entering (link: /terms/cursive text: cursive) text.

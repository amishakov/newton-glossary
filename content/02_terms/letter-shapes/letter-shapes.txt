Title: Letter Shapes

----

Date: 07 November 2017

----

Text:

A setting which helps improve [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) by allowing the user to indicate which character shapes are more common or seldom used when entering [cursive](/terms/cursive) text.

Title: Object

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A typed piece of data that can be an (link: /terms/immediate text: immediate) value, (link: /terms/array text: array), (link: /terms/frame text: frame), or (link: /terms/binary-object text: binary object). In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), only frame objects can hold (link: /terms/method text: methods) and receive (link: /terms/message text: messages).

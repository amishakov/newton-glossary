Title: Info Picker

----

Date: 29 December 2017

----

Text:

A list of items pertaining to the current (link: /terms/application text: application) that can include (link: /terms/preferences text: preferences), onscreen help, or general information about the application. Activated by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) on the (link: /terms/info-button text: info button).

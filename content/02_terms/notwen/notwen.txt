Title: Notwen

----

Date: 05 February 2025

----

Text:

A website operated by (link: /sources/adriano-angelillis text: Adriano Angelillis) that provided information about his various hardware accessories for the Newton platform. Most of the projects took advantage of the multifunctional (link: /terms/newton-interconnect text: Newton InterConnect) port, such as the (link: /terms/newtpod text: NewtPod), (link: /terms/newtcast text: NewtCast), (link: /terms/newtusb text: NewtUSB), (link: /terms/newtk text: NewtK), (link: /terms/NewtSplit text: NewtSplit), and (link: /terms/newtlight text: NewtLight).

Notwen is Newton spelled backwards.

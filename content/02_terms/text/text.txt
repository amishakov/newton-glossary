Title: Text

----

Date: 05 November 2017

----

Text:

A [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) setting selected by [tapping](/terms/tapping) the [recognition button](/terms/recognition-button). When this setting is selected, words and numbers written on the Newton device will be converted to [recognized text](/terms/recognized-text).

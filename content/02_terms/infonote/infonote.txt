Title: InfoNote

----

Date: 16 August 2024

----

Text:

A French-language electronic newsletter published by the [MPadClub](/terms/mpadclub) user group in Lyon, France.

InfoNote is also a software [package](/terms/package) developed by Pierre Vettier which provided a simple means to connect to the MPadClub server via [modem](/terms/modem) and automatically download the latest news to a [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) device.

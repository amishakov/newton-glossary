Title: Cell

----

Date: 21 November 2004

----

Text:

A device that converts chemical energy into electrical energy. A (link: /terms/battery text: battery) is usually comprised of two or more interconnected cells.

A cell is also a component of a (link: /terms/spreadsheet text: spreadsheet) document that can contain text labels, numeric values, or formulas. The (link: /terms/address text: address) of a spreadsheet (link: /terms/cell text: cell) corresponds to the column and the row where it is located. For example, the address for a cell in the first column of the second row is designated as A2.

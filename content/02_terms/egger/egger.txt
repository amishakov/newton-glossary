Title: Egger

----

Date: 14 November 2017

----

Text:

Software developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) that provided a means to run and (link: /terms/debug text: debug) Newton software on a Mac OS (link: /terms/desktop-computer text: desktop computer) outfitted with a NuBus-based (link: /terms/armageddon text: ARMageddon) or (link: /terms/armistice text: ARMistice) card. Egger would load a (link: /terms/rom text: ROM) image containing the (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) and display a functional representation of the Newton (link: /terms/screen text: screen) in a window on the Macintosh.

Title: Option Frame

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript), a [frame](/terms/frame) passed as a parameter to an [endpoint](/terms/endpoint) method that selects the communication tool to use; controls its configuration and operation; and returns result code information from the endpoint [method](/terms/method).

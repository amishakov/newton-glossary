Title: Codec

----

Date: 23 March 2017

----

Text:

Hardware or software that converts analogue information into its digital equivalent or digital information into its analogue equivalent. On a Newton device, the built-in codecs include muLaw, IMA, GSM, and a synthesizer codec for sound generation. The [MacinTalk](/terms/macintalk) extension provides a an additional codec which converts [recognized text](/terms/recognized-text) into synthesized speech.

Codec is acronym of “coder-decoder” or “compressor-decompressor” depending on the application.

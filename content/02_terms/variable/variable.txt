Title: Variable

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A named location in memory where an executable program or (link: /terms/function text: function) can store and retrieve intermediate results. (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript) supports both (link: /terms/local text: local) and (link: /terms/global text: global) variables.

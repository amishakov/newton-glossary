Title: Battery Tray

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A removable component of a [MessagePad](/terms/messagepad) device which holds a set of individual [batteries](/terms/battery). Batteries held in the battery tray can be easily replaced by the user.

Also referred to as a [Battery Cage](/terms/battery-cage), [Battery Sled](/terms/battery-sled), [Cage](/terms/cage), [Sled](/terms/sled), or [Tray](/terms/tray).

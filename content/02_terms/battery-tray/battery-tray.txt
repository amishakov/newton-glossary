Title: Battery Tray

----

Date:

----

Text:

A removable component of a [MessagePad](/terms/messagepad) device which holds a set of individual [batteries](/terms/battery). Batteries held in the battery tray can be easily replaced by the user. Also referred to as a [battery cage](/terms/battery-cage) or [battery sled](/terms/battery-sled).

Title: Battery Tray

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A removable component of a (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad) device which holds a set of individual (link: /terms/battery text: batteries). Batteries held in the battery tray can be easily replaced by the user.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/battery-cage text: Battery Cage), (link: /terms/battery-sled text: Battery Sled), (link: /terms/cage text: Cage), (link: /terms/sled text: Sled), or (link: /terms/tray text: Tray).

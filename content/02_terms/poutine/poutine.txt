Title: Poutine

----

Date: 06 October 2002

----

Text:

A culinary anomaly originating in Quebec, consisting of cheese curds and gravy poured over French fried potatoes. Poutine is also [an oddly recurrent theme](https://marc.info/?l=newtontalk&w=2&r=1&s=poutine&q=t) on the (link: /terms/newtontalk text: NewtonTalk) mailing list.

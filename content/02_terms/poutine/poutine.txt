Title: Poutine

----

Date:

----

Text:

A culinary anomaly originating in Quebec, consisting of cheese curds and gravy poured over French fried potatoes. Poutine is also [an oddly recurrent theme](http://marc.info/?l=newtontalk&w=2&r=1&s=poutine&q=t) on the [NewtonTalk](/terms/newtontalk) mailing list.

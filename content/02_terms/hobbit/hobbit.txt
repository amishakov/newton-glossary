Title: Hobbit

----

Date:

----

Text:

A code name of the processor chip developed by AT&amp;T, originally targeted to be used for the Newton platform. The Hobbit chip was replaced by the [RISC](/terms/risc) chip developed by [ARM](/terms/arm).

AT&amp;T went on to use the Hobbit processor in its own pen-based computer, the EO.

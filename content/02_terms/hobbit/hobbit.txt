Title: Hobbit

----

Date: 12 June 2001

----

Text:

A code name of the processor chip developed by AT&amp;T, originally targeted to be used for the Newton platform. The Hobbit chip was replaced by the (link: /terms/risc text: RISC) chip developed by (link: /terms/arm text: ARM).

AT&amp;T went on to use the Hobbit processor in its own pen-based computer, the EO.

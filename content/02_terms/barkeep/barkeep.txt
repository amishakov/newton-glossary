Title: BarKeep

----

Date: 26 November 2017

----

Text:

Software developed by [Innovative Computer Solutions](/sources/innovative-computer-solutions) to enhance the [button bar](/terms/button-bar) on [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000) and [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100) devices. BarKeep allows the user to control the location, size, and appearance of the button bar, including the ability able to customize these settings for each of the four possible [screen](/terms/screen) orientations.

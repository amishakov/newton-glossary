Title: Real-Time Clock

----

Date: 23 September 2024

----

Text:

An electronic circuit or piece of software that accurately measures the passage of time. Real-time clocks are used in any device that is used to keep time. (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) includes a high resolution real-time clock.

Also referred to as an (link: /terms/rtc text: RTC).

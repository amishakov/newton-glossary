Title: Docking

----

Date: 15 January 2018

----

Text:

The process of connecting a Newton device to another computer using the [Dock](/terms/dock) or [Classroom Dock](/terms/classroom-dock) application. After [docking](/terms/docking) with another computer, the user can initiate the transfer of data between the two devices.

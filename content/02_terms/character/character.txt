Title: Character

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

Any symbol or glyph that has a widely understood meaning and can convey information. Most characters — such as letters, numbers, and punctuation — can be displayed on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen), sent in an email message, transmitted in a fax, or (link: /terms/output text: output) on a printer.

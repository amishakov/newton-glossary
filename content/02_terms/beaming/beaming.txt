Title: Beaming

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

Transmitting data to or receiving data from another device via an [infrared](/terms/infrared) connection. Colloquially (and somewhat disturbingly) referred to as [squirting](/terms/squirting) by early Newton developers.

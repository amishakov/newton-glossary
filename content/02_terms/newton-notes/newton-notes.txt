Title: Newton Notes

----

Date: 14 May 2025

----

Text:

A column focused on the Newton platform written by David MacNeill. The column began in September 1993 and continued until February 1999, appearing is several publications, including _Pen Computing_, _Mac Monthly_, and _On The Go Magazine_.

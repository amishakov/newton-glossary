Title: NewtEye

----

Date: 15 February 2025

----

Text:

A hardware accessory designed by (link: /sources/adriano-angelillis text: Adriano Angelillis) consisting of a customized (link: /terms/led text: LED) that plugged into a (link: /terms/mini-din text: Mini-DIN) serial port on a Newton device. The NewtEye could be turned on and off using Frank Gründel’s (link: /terms/newtlight text: NewtLight) utility.

Title: BLTG

----

Date: 25 September 2001

----

Text:

A value found in (link: /terms/ram text: RAM) on a Newton device which is used to indicate whether (link: /terms/global text: global) values that are maintained after a Newton device has experienced a (link: /terms/restart text: restart) are valid or not. The BLTG acronym is defined in the Newton development environment header files and can also be found in the (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) source code.

Also referred to using the playful mnemonic (link: /terms/bacon-lettuce-tomato-garlic text: Bacon Lettuce Tomato & Garlic). Yum.

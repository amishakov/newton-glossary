Date: 20 June 2025

Text:

An (link: /terms/alert-sound text: alert sound) which can be selected in the (link: /terms/sound text: Sound) section of the (link: /terms/prefs text: Prefs) application.

Xylo is one of four alert sounds available in all versions of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS).

Also referred to as (link: /terms/plink text: Plink) and (link: /terms/plinkbeep text: plinkBeep).

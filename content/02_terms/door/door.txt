Title: Door

----

Date: 05 August 2024

----

Text:

A movable [cover](/terms/cover) that protects various ports when not in use.

On [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000] and [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100] devices, a hinged door on the top of the case opens to reveal the [power port](/terms/power-port) and [InterConnect](/terms/interconnect) ports.

On an eMate 300, a [sliding door](/terms/sliding-door) covers the [communication ports](/terms/communication-port).

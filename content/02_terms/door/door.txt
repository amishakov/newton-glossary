Title: Door

----

Date: 05 August 2024

Text:

A movable (link: /terms/cover text: cover) that protects various ports when not in use.

On (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000) and (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100) devices, a hinged door on the top of the case opens to reveal the (link: /terms/power-port text: power port) and (link: /terms/interconnect text: InterConnect) ports.

On an eMate 300, a (link: /terms/sliding-door text: sliding door) covers the (link: /terms/communication-port text: communication ports).

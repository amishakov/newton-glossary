Title: Text Button

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/control text: control) bordered by a (link: /terms/rounded-rectangle text: rounded rectangle) that the user (link: /terms/tap text: taps) to designate, confirm, or cancel an action described by a text label inside the border.

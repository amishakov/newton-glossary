Title: Text Button

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A [control](/terms/control) bordered by a [rounded rectangle](/terms/rounded-rectangle) that the user [taps](/terms/tap) to designate, confirm, or cancel an action described by a text label inside the border.

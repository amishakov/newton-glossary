Title: Squeak

----

Date: 18 December 2001

----

Text:

The (link: /terms/pen-sound-effects text: pen sound effect) made by a Newton device after touching and holding the (link: /terms/pen text: pen) on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) until a large dot known as a (link: /terms/heavy-mark text: heavy mark) appears. Used to indicate the start of the action when (link: /terms/select text: selecting) a block of text or a (link: /terms/shape text: shape).

Also referred to as (link: /terms/hilitesound text: hiliteSound), (link: /terms/hilite-sound text: Hilite Sound), and (link: /terms/squeek text: Squeek).

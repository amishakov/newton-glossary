Title: Twerx

----

Date:

----

Text:

A utility by [Innovative Computer Solutions](/sources/innovative-computer-solutions) which allows the user to import documents from [Notepad](/terms/notepad) and other [applications](/terms/application) into [Newton Works](/terms/newton-works) using the [action picker](/terms/action-picker). Also referred to as [Twerks](/terms/twerks) on the [NewtonTalk](/terms/newtontalk) mailing list.

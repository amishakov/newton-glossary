Title: Notification Slip

----

Date:

----

Text:

A [view](/terms/view) that appears onscreen as part of the [user interface](/terms/user-interface), warning the user or reporting an error. A notification slip may or may not be accompanied by an [alert sound](/terms/alert-sound).

Title: Frozen Ghost

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

An alternate name for the clear case (link: /terms/frozen-newton text: Frozen Newton) project, which involved the creation of a clear case (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100).

Definitely not a reference to the [Canadian rock band](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frozen_Ghost) from the 1980s.

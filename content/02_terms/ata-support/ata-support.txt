Title: ATA Support

----

Date: 28 September 2017

----

Text:

A [device driver](/terms/device-driver) developed by [Paul Guyot](/sources/paul-guyot) that allows a Newton device to use large capacity, non-linear [storage cards](/terms/storage-card) such as [CompactFlash](/terms/compactflash). ATA Support works with [ATA](/terms/ata) formatted [stores](/terms/store) under [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) 2.0 and later.

Title: ATA Support

----

Date: 28 September 2017

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/device-driver text: device driver) developed by (link: /sources/paul-guyot text: Paul Guyot) that allows a Newton device to use large capacity, non-linear (link: /terms/storage-card text: storage cards) such as (link: /terms/compactflash text: CompactFlash). ATA Support works with (link: /terms/ata text: ATA) formatted (link: /terms/store text: stores) under (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) 2.0 and later.

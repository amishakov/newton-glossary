Infrared Link Access Protocol

The mandatory link access layer of the (link: /terms/irda text: IrDA) communications (link: /terms/protocol text: protocol) specification. IrLAP is built on top of either (link: /terms/slow-infrared text: Slow Infrared) (SIR) and (link: /terms/fast-infrared text: Fast Infrared) (FIR).

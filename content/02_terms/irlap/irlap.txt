Infrared Link Access Protocol

The mandatory link access layer of the [IrDA](/terms/irda) communications [protocol](/terms/protocol) specification. IrLAP is built on top of [Serial Infrared](/terms/serial-infrared) (SIR) and/or [Fast Infrared](/terms/fast-infrared) (FIR).

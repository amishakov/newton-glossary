Title: RS-422

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A standard for serial communication between computers and peripheral devices that features increased data transfer rates and cabling distance than (link: /terms/rs-232 text: RS-232). Devices supporting the RS-422 standard are outfitted with DB-25 or DC-37 connectors. An RS-232 compatible version of RS-422 using a [Mini-DIN}(/terms/mini-din) connector was included on Mac OS computers and peripherals, eventually being replaced by (link: /terms/usb text: USB).

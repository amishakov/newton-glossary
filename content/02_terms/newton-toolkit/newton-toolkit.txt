Title: Newton Toolkit

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A development environment created by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) for writing (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript) programs for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS). A prototype for Newton Toolkit called (link: /terms/etchasketch text: EtchASketch) was written using (link: /terms/macintosh-common-lisp text: Macintosh Common Lisp).

Also referred to as (link: /terms/ntk text: NTK).

Newton Toolkit for Mac OS X (also referred to as (link: /terms/ntx text: NTX)) is a modern replacement for the classic Newton Toolkit being developed by (link: /sources/simon-bell text: Simon Bell).

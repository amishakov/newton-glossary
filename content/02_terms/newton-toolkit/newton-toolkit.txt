Title: Newton Toolkit

----

Date:

----

Text:

A development environment created by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) for writing [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript) programs for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os). A prototype for Newton Toolkit called [EtchASketch](/terms/etchasketch) was written using [Macintosh Common Lisp](/terms/macintosh-common-lisp).

Also referred to as [NTK](/terms/ntk).

Newton Toolkit for Mac OS X (also known as [NTX](/terms/ntx)) is a modern replacement for the classic Newton Toolkit being developed by [Simon Bell](/sources/simon-bell).

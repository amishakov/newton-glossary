Title: Tab Stop

----

Date: 24 August 2025

----

Text:

A location along a horizontal line used to control the position, indentation, and alignment of text and images in a document. In a (link: /terms/word-processing-document text: word processing document), a tab stop is usually indicated by a small (link: /terms/icon text: icon) called a (link: /terms/tab-marker text: tab marker) on a (link: /terms/ruler text: ruler). Different tab stops can be selected to specify whether text and images are aligned to the left, to the right, centered, or using the decimal point in numeric information.

The (link: /terms/tab-key text: Tab key) located on a keyboard moves the (link: /terms/caret text: caret) or text (link: /terms/insertion-point text: insertion point) from one tab stop to the next.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/tab text: Tab).

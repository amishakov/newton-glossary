Worldwide Online Newton Conference

A virtual, online gathering of Newton users, developers, and aficionados capturing the spirit of the previous [Worldwide Newton Conference](/terms/wwnc) events. Organized by [Pawel Piotrowski](/sources/pawel-piotrowski), the inaugural WONC was held in May 2020.

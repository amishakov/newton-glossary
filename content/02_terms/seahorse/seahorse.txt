Title: Seahorse

----

Date:

----

Text:

A ruggedized Newton device developed by [Digital Ocean](https://web.archive.org/web/19970124001013/http://digitalocean.com/) based on the [MessagePad 130](/terms/messagepad-130). Similar to the Digital Ocean [Tarpon](/terms/tarpon), the Seahorse featured technologies such as the first [CDPD](/terms/cdpd) modem found in a [personal digital assistant](/terms/personal-digital-assistant), built-in wireless networking, peer-to-peer voice communication, plus optional GPS and diffused [infrared](/terms/infrared) capabilities.

A third-party device that utilizes a licensed version of [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) is commonly referred to as a [clone](/terms/clone).

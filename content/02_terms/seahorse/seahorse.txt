Title: Seahorse

----

Date: 31 May 2001

----

Text:

A ruggedized Newton device developed by (link: /sources/digital-ocean text: Digital Ocean) based on the (link: /terms/messagepad-130 text: MessagePad 130). Similar to the Digital Ocean (link: /terms/tarpon text: Tarpon), the Seahorse featured technologies such as the first (link: /terms/cdpd text: CDPD) modem found in a (link: /terms/personal-digital-assistant text: personal digital assistant), built-in wireless networking, peer-to-peer voice communication, plus optional GPS and diffused (link: /terms/infrared text: infrared) capabilities.

A third-party device that utilizes a licensed version of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) is commonly referred to as a (link: /terms/clone text: clone).

Title: MessagePad 2100

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

The last Newton device released by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) through their (link: /terms/newton-inc text: Newton Inc.) subsidiary. It shared the same specifications as its predecessor, the (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000), with the exception of an increased amount of (link: /terms/system-memory text: system memory). A MessagePad 2000 device can been upgraded to the equivalent of a MessagePad 2100 by increasing the total amount of (link: /terms/ram text: RAM) from 5MB to 8MB.

Like its predecessor, the MessagePad 2100 was code named alternately (link: /terms/n2 text: N2) and (link: /terms/q text: Q).

It is commonly referred to as an (link: /terms/mp2100 text: MP2100).

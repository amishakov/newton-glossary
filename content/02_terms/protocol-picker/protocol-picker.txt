Title: Protocol Picker

----

Date: 24 December 2024

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/picker text: picker) in an (link: /terms/internet-setup text: Internet Setup) settings slip that displays a list of connection (link: /terms/protocol text: protocols) when (link: /terms/tapped text: tapped). Protocols include (link: /terms/ppp text: PPP) and (link: /terms/slip-protocol text: SLIP).

(image: /assets/images/protocol-picker-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of the Protocol Picker width: 105)

On devices with (link: /terms/newton-internet-enabler text: Newton Internet Enabler) 1.0 installed, the picker is labeled “Link Protocol”, rather than simply “Protocol” on devices with (link: /terms/nie text: NIE) 2.0.

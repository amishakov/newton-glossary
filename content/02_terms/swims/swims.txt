Title: SWiMS

----

Date: 18 January 2024

----

Text:

Socket Wireless Messaging Services

A set of services developed by [Socket Communications](https://web.archive.org/web/19980208233349/http://www.socketcom.com/) which included voicemail, operator dispatch, an email gateway, and a (link: /terms/modem text: modem) access line for computer-based messaging. SWiMS replaced the (link: /terms/apple-mobile-message-system text: Apple Mobile Message System) and its related (link: /terms/apple-notification-service text: Apple Notification Service).

Also referred to as (link: /terms/wms text: WMS).

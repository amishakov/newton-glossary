Title: NewtHack

----

Date: 06 August 2025

----

Text:

A role-playing adventure game for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) developed by Christopher A. Dopp. NewtHack echoes the same multi-level dungeon gaming style popularized by classic titles such as [Rogue](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rogue_(video_game)) and [NetHack](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/NetHack).

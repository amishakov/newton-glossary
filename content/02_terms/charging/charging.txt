Title: Charging

----

Date: 21 February 2025

----

Text:

The (link: /terms/charge-state text: charge state) of a rechargeable (link: /terms/battery text: battery) indicating that it is currently having its electrical energy restored. Generally, charging occurs when a device is connected to an external power source.

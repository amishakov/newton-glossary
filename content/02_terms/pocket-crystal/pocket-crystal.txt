Title: Pocket Crystal

----

Date: 16 July 2025

----

Text:

The code name of a portable, agent-based, networked device being developed at the same time as the (link: /terms/Figaro text: Figaro) project at [Apple](https://www.apple.com/). Pocket Crystal was eventually spun out as [General Magic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/General_Magic) to become [Magic Cap](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magic_Cap), the (link: /terms/operating system text: operating system) used in the [Sony Magic Link](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magic_Link) and [Motorola Envoy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Motorola_Envoy) devices.

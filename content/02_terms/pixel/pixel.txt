Title: Pixel

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

Originally, a contraction of the words “picture” and “element”. The smallest element a device can draw on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen). On a Newton (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad), there are 80 pixels to an inch. Each pixel can be either black or white, so it can be represented by a single bit. Thus, the image displayed is referred to as a (link: /terms/bitmap text: bitmap).

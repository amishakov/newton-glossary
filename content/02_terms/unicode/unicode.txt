Title: Unicode

----

Date: 04 February 2024

----

Text:

A universal character encoding standard maintained by the [Unicode Consortium](/sources/unicode-consortium) that provides the basis for processing, storage, and exchange of text data in any language, using various software and information technology protocols.

The [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) stores text as UTF-16 (16-bit Unicode Transformation Format).

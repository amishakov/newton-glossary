Title: Calculator

----

Date: 04 May 2025

----

Text:

The built-in (link: /terms/application text: application) that performs simple arithmatic and calculations. Information can be copied to the (link: /terms/clipboard text: clipboard) to be used in other applications.

More advanced calculations can be performed using the (link: /terms/graphing-calculator text: Graphing Calculator) in the (link: /terms/newton-works text: Newton Works) application.

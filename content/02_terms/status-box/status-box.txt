Title: Status Box

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A black-framed (link: /terms/container-view text: container view) displaying a static message indicating that a Newton device is busy completing a lengthy process. For example, a (link: /terms/status-slip text: status slip) uses a status box (link: /terms/view text: view).

Title: NewtK

----

Date: 16 February 2025

----

Text:

A hardware accessory designed by (link: /sources/adriano-angelillis text: Adriano Angelillis) that provided a way to connect both an external keyboard and a (link: /terms/usb text: USB) powered (link: /terms/led text: LED) lamp using the serial port on a Newton device.

NewtK adapters were available for both the [Palm Portable Stowaway Keyboard](https://web.archive.org/web/20000510122503/http://thinkoutside.com/palm.html) and the (link: /terms/newton-keyboard text: Newton Keyboard).

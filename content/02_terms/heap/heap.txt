Title: Heap

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The area of (link: /terms/system-memory text: system memory) reserved for use by program functions and executable code. On a Newton device, heap often refers specifically to the (link: /terms/newtonscript-heap text: NewtonScript heap) and (link: /terms/c-heap text: C++ heap).

Also referred to as (link: /terms/frames-heap text: Frames Heap).

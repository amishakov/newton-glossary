Title: Sketches

----

Date: 05 November 2017

----

Text:

A [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) setting selected by [tapping](/terms/tapping) the [recognition button](/terms/recognition-button). When this setting is selected, all drawings are stored as [electronic ink](/terms/electronic-ink) and not converted to [shapes](/terms/shapes).

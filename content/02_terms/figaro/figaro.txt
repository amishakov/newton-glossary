Title: Figaro

----

Date:

----

Text:

A speculative, pen-based tablet computer concept that kicked off the original Newton project. Figaro featured a hard drive, an active matrix [LCD](/terms/lcd) display, and [infrared](/terms/infrared) networking. The form factor would eventually evolve into the [Newton Plus](/terms/newton-plus) device.

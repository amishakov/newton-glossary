Title: Figaro

----

Date: 25 September 2001

----

Text:

A speculative, pen-based tablet computer concept that kicked off the original Newton project. Figaro featured a hard drive, an active matrix (link: /terms/lcd text: LCD) display, and (link: /terms/infrared text: infrared) networking. The form factor would eventually evolve into the (link: /terms/newton-plus text: Newton Plus) device.

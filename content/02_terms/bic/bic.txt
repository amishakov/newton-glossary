Title: Bic

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The code name of the second generation, tablet-style Newton hardware prototype, sucessor to the (link: /terms/cadillac text: Cadillac).

In the (link: /sources/appledesign text: AppleDesign: The Work of the Apple Industrial Design Group) book, the Bic device shown has the name (link: /terms/messageslate text: MessageSlate) printed on the case.

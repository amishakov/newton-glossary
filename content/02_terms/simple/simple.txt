Title: Simple

----

Date: 10 February 2017

----

Text:

One of the default bitmap (link: /terms/font text: fonts) on the Newton. Simple is a proportionally spaced sans serif typeface based on [Geneva](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geneva_(typeface)).

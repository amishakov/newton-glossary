Title: Simple

----

Date: 10 February 2017

Text:

One of the default bitmap [fonts](/terms/font) on the Newton. Simple is a proportionally spaced sans serif typeface based on [Geneva](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geneva_(typeface)).

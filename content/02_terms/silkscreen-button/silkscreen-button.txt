Title: Silkscreen Button

----

Date: 30 October 2001

----

Text:

A colloquial term for one of the (link: /terms/hard-button text: hard buttons) found in the (link: /terms/button-bar text: button bar) below the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) of early Newton devices, including the (link: /terms/omp text: OMP), (link: /terms/messagepad-100 text: MessagePad 100), (link: /terms/messagepad-110 text: MessagePad 110), (link: /terms/messagepad-120 text: MessagePad 120), and (link: /terms/messagepad-130 text: MessagePad 130). So called because the (link: /terms/icon text: icon) representing each (link: /terms/button text: button) is silkscreened onto the surface of the device.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/silk-button text: Silk Button).

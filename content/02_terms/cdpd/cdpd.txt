Text:

Cellular Digital Packet Data

A defunct wireless data service developed in the early 1990s. CDPD was available as a networking option in the ruggedized (link: /terms/seahorse text: Seahorse) and (link: /terms/tarpon text: Tarpon) devices manufactured by (link: /sources/digital-ocean text: Digital Ocean).

Title: Away City

----

Date:

----

Text:

The [locale](/terms/locale) or [emporium](/terms/emporium) that is displayed as a counterpoint to your [home city](/terms/home-city). It defines the country dialing code, time zone, and other locale specific information. Sometimes called the _“I’m here”_ city.

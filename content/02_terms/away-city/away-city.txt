Title: Away City

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The (link: /terms/locale text: locale) or (link: /terms/emporium text: emporium) that is displayed as a counterpoint to your (link: /terms/home-city text: home city). It defines the country dialing code, time zone, and other locale specific information. Sometimes called the _“I’m here”_ city.

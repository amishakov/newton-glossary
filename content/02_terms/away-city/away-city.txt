Text:

The (link: /terms/locale text: locale) or (link: /terms/emporium text: emporium) that is displayed as a counterpoint to your (link: /terms/home-city text: home city). It defines the country dialing code, (link: /terms/time-zones text: time zone), and other locale specific information.

Title: Confirmation Alert

----

Date: 20 December 2023

----

Text:

An (link: /terms/alert-box text: alert box) that appears on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) requiring the user confirm or cancel an action that can have “far-reaching” consequences. For example, a confirmation alert appears before erasing the data on a (link: /terms/storage-card text: storage card).

A confirmation alert has no (link: /terms/close-box text: close box). Instead, it has labeled buttons, usually named “Cancel” and “OK”.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/confirmation-slip text: Confirmation Slip).

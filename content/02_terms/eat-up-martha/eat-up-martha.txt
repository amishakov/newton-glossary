Title: Eat Up Martha

----

Date: 24 May 2017

----

Text:

A reference to the questionable quality of early Newton (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) from the [Lisa on Ice](http://www.simpsonsarchive.com/episodes/2F05.html) episode of (link: /sources/the-simpsons text: The Simpsons). In the scene, Dolph writes _“Beat up Martin”_ on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) and the handwriting recognition converts it to _“Eat up Martha”_. Kearney then throws the Newton at Martin.

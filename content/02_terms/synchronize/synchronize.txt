Title: Synchronize

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A process where the data stored in two different locations is compared and updated so that both copies end up the same. For example, (link: /terms/newton-connection-utilities text: Newton Connection Utilities) can synchronize data between a Newton device and a (link: /terms/desktop-computer text: desktop computer) or (link: /terms/laptop-computer text: laptop).

Title: Newton Press

----

Date:

----

Text:

Software developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) for easily creating electronic documents in [Newton book](/terms/newton-book) format. Newton Press was designed to be used by non-programmers, whereas [Newton Book Maker](/terms/newton-book-maker) provided more sophisticated development tools for creating Newton books and [application](/terms/application) help files.

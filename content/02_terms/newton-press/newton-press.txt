Title: Newton Press

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

Software developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) for easily creating electronic documents in (link: /terms/newton-book text: Newton book) format. Newton Press was designed to be used by non-programmers, whereas (link: /terms/newton-book-maker text: Newton Book Maker) provided more sophisticated development tools for creating Newton books and (link: /terms/application text: application) help files.

Title: NewtonID

----

Date: 03 September 2017

----

Text:

A free utility developed by (link: /sources/adam-tow text: Adam Tow) that generates a universal identifier for Newton devices running (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) 2.0 and later. NewtonID provides developers a means of creating software registration systems that do not rely on information that can change, such as the (link: /terms/owner text: owner) or the serial number of a Newton device.

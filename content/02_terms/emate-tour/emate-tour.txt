Title: eMate Tour

----

Date: 29 August 2018

----

Text:

The built-in [application](/terms/application) which provides an interactive tutorial for learning the basics of an [eMate](/terms/emate-300) device and its various features. On a [MessagePad](/terms/messagepad) device, a similar tutorial is named [Newton Tour](/terms/newton-tour).

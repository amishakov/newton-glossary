Title: NewtIR

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

An (link: /terms/infrared text: infrared) communications (link: /terms/protocol text: protocol) based on the (link: /terms/ask text: ASK) standard and used in older Newton devices. This protocol includes extensions developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) which make it substantially faster.

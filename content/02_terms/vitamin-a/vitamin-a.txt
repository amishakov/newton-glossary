Title: Vitamin A

----

Date: 08 June 2024

----

Text:

A [user interface](/terms/user-interface) enhancement [package](/terms/package) developed by [Stand Alone](https://standalone.com/) and published by [Power Media](/sources/power-media) that facilitates the creation of custom menus providing quick access to various user-defined actions. A menu can be assigned to each corner of the [screen](/terms/screen), the areas below each of the [soft buttons](/terms/soft-button) in the [button bar](/terms/button-bar) the bottom of the screen, or [caret picker](/terms/caret-picker).

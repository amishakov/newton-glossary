A (link: /terms/user-interface text: user interface) enhancement (link: /terms/package text: package) developed by [Stand Alone](https://standalone.com/) and published by (link: /sources/power-media text: Power Media) that facilitates the creation of custom menus providing quick access to various user-defined actions. A menu can be assigned to each corner of the (link: /terms/screen text: screen), the areas below each of the (link: /terms/soft-button text: soft buttons) in the (link: /terms/button-bar text: button bar) the bottom of the screen, or (link: /terms/caret-picker text: caret picker).

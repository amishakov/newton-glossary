Title: Content Script

----

Date:

----

Text:

A piece of [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript) code attached to a content item in a [Newton book](/terms/newton-book) source file, before any [Book Maker commands](/terms/book-maker-commands). The actions defined in content scripts override those in [page scripts](/terms/page-script), just as page scripts take precedence over [book scripts](/terms/book-script).

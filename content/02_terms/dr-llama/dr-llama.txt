Title: Dr Llama

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

The nom de plume of (link: /sources/maurice-sharp text: Maurice Sharp), who worked as a developer technical support engineer in the (link: /terms/newton-systems-group text: Newton Systems Group) at [Apple](https://www.apple.com/). The nickname stems from the unofficial mascot of Apple’s (link: /terms/developer-technical-services text: Developer Technical Services) team … the (link: /terms/llama text: Llama).

For reasons yet to be determined, Sharp was also known as (link: /terms/commander-clueless text: Commander Clueless) within the group.

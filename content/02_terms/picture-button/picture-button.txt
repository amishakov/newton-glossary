Title: Picture Button

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A control that when (link: /terms/tapped text: tapped) by the user, performs or confirms the action implied by the (link: /terms/icon text: icon) displayed on the (link: /terms/button text: button).

For example, (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the small picture button in the top left corner of a (link: /terms/note text: note) will open an (link: /terms/item-info-slip text: item info slip), containing the title of the note, and date and time the note was created, the amount of space the note uses, and where the note is (link: /terms/store text: stored).

Title: Picture Button

----

Date:

----

Text:

A control that when [tapped](/terms/tapped) by the user, performs or confirms the action implied by the [icon](/terms/icon) displayed on the [button](/terms/button).

For example, [tapping](/terms/tapping) the small picture button in the top left corner of a [note](/terms/note) will open an [item info slip](/terms/item-info-slip), containing the title of the note, and date and time the note was created, the amount of space the note uses, and where the note is [stored](/terms/store).

Title: Desktop Computer

----

Date:

----

Text:

A computer system that generally resides on a desk or table, as opposed to a portable [laptop](/terms/laptop-computer) or [tablet computer](/terms/tablet-computer). In terms of a [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) environment, the desktop computer would likely be running either the Mac OS or Windows [operating system](/terms/operating-system).

Title: Desktop Computer

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A computer system that generally resides on a desk or table, as opposed to a portable (link: /terms/laptop-computer text: laptop) or (link: /terms/tablet-computer text: tablet computer). In terms of a (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) environment, the desktop computer would likely be running either the Mac OS or Windows (link: /terms/operating-system text: operating system).

----

Date: 04 November 2017

Text:

A monthly email newsletter published by the (link: /terms/newton-systems-group text: Newton Systems Group) between October 1994 and May 1997. The target audience of the newsletter focused on systems integrators, value added resellers, and independent software vendors who were representing the Newton platform.

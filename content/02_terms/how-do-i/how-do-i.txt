Title: How Do I?

----

Date: 10 December 2023

----

Text:

The [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) includes a [help browser](help-browser) for built-in applications and system services. Users can view the onscreen help by [tapping](/terms/tapping) the “How Do I?” [button](/terms/button) in the [Assist slip](/terms/assist-slip). When a user taps the button, an outline-like overview of help topics is displayed. Tapping a topic expands the outline to display that topic’s subtopics or, if that topic has no subtopics, it will display instructions for that topic.

Title: Newton 200

----

Date: 18 August 2024

----

Text:

A conceptual Newton device proposed by Matthew B. McRae that utilized a PowerPC processor. The (link: /terms/newton-200c text: Newton 200c) variation of the hardware implemented an 8-bit colour (link: /terms/display text: display). The (link: /terms/newton-200i text: Newton 200i) variation utilized an Indiglo (link: /terms/backlight text: backlight).

Not to be confused with the rumoured (link: /terms/messagepad-200 text: MessagePad 200) device.

Title: Newton 200

----

Date: 18 August 2024

----

Text:

A conceptual Newton device proposed by Matthew B. McRae that utilized a PowerPC processor. The [Newton 200c](/terms/newton-200c) variation of the hardware implemented an 8-bit colour [display](/terms/display). The [Newton 200i](/terms/newton-200i) variation utilized an Indiglo [backlight](/terms/backlight).

Not to be confused with the rumoured [MessagePad 200](/terms/messagepad-200) device.

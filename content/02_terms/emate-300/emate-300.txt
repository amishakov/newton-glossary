Title: eMate 300

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A durable, translucent (link: /terms/clamshell text: clamshell) style Newton device that featured a built-in keyboard, extremely long battery life, and an integrated suite of (link: /terms/application text: applications) called (link: /terms/newton-works text: Newton Works). The device was primarily aimed at the educational market.

The eMate 300 was known by various code names, including (link: /terms/k text: K), (link: /terms/project-k text: Project K), (link: /terms/schoolbook text: Schoolbook), and (link: /terms/shay text: Shay).

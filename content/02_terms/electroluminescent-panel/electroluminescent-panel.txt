Title: Electroluminescent Panel

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

An illumination technology consisting of a thin film phosphor layer sandwiched between a substrate. When charged, the phosphor in that emits visible light. The electroluminescent (link: /terms/backlight text: backlight) found in Newton devices starting with the [MessagePad 130](messagepad-130) has a distinctive bright green colour.

Also referred to as an (link: /terms/elp text: ELP).

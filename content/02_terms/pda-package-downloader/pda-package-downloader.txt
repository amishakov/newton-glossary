Title: PDA Package Downloader

----

Date: 10 May 2026

----

Text:

An (link: /terms/application text: application) developed by (link: /sources/lunatech text: Lunatech Research) that provided a way to easily download (link: /terms/package text: packages) from a MacOS computer to a Newton device using drag and drop.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/PPD text: PPD).

PDA Package Downloader was the successor to Lunatech’s (link: /terms/newton-package-downloader text: Newton Package Downloader) (NPD) utility.

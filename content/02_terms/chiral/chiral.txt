Title: Chiral

----

Date: 01 August 2024

----

Text:

A game developed by (link: /sources/scrawl-software text: Scrawl Software) based on the Mac OS version originally conceived by [Ambrosia Software](https://web.archive.org/web/20171001021126/http://www.ambrosiasw.com/games/chiral/). The object of the game is to form inert molecules, described as “a cross between Tetris and that 10th grade chemistry class you tried to forget”.

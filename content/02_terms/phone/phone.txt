Text:

An onscreen (link: /terms/keyboard text: keyboard) that appears when the (link: /terms/keyboard-button text: keyboard button) is (link: /terms/tapped text: tapped). The phone keyboard is designed for easily entering telephone numbers into (link: /terms/application text: applications) such as (link: /terms/name-file text: Name File). Several other onscreen keyboards are available for entering specific types of data, including (link: /terms/typewriter text: Typewriter), (link: /terms/numeric text: Numeric), and [Time/Date](/terms/time-date).

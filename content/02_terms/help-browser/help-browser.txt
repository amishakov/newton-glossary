Title: Help Browser

----

Date: 18 December 2001

----

Text:

An outline-based help interface that uses [help book](help-book) data created by the (link: /terms/newton-book-maker text: Newton Book Maker). Generally used to provide information about a specific (link: /terms/application text: applications). The help browser provides only a subset of the capabilities of the (link: /terms/newton-book-reader text: Newton Book Reader).

The Help browser can be accessed by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the “How Do I?” button in the (link: /terms/assist-slip text: Assist slip).

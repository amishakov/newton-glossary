Title: Battery Latch

----

Date: 07 August 2024

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/latch text: latch) located on (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000) and (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100) devices which releases the (link: /terms/battery-pack text: battery pack) or (link: /terms/battery-tray text: battery tray), allowing it to be removed.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/battery-compartment-latch text: Battery Compartment Latch).

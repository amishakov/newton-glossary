Title: Battery Latch

----

Date: 07 August 2024

----

Text:

A [latch](/terms/latch) located on [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000) and [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100) devices which releases the [battery pack](/terms/battery-pack) or [battery tray](/terms/battery-tray), allowing it to be removed.

Also referred to as a [battery compartment latch](/terms/battery-compartment-latch).

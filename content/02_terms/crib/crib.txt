Title: Crib

----

Date:

----

Text:

The code name of the charging [cradle](/terms/cradle) designed to work with the [MessagePad 110](/terms/messagepad-110), [MessagePad 120](/terms/messagepad-120), and [MessagePad 130](/terms/messagepad-130) devices. Also referred to as the [Lindy Crib](/terms/lindy-crib), after the [Lindy](/terms/lindy) code name of the MessagePad 110.

Title: Crib

----

Date: 25 September 2001

----

Text:

The code name of the charging (link: /terms/cradle text: cradle) designed to work with the (link: /terms/messagepad-110 text: MessagePad 110), (link: /terms/messagepad-120 text: MessagePad 120), and (link: /terms/messagepad-130 text: MessagePad 130) devices. Also referred to as the (link: /terms/lindy-crib text: Lindy Crib), after the (link: /terms/lindy text: Lindy) code name of the MessagePad 110.

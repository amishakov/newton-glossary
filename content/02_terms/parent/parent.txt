Title: Parent

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), a (link: /terms/frame text: frame) referenced through the parent (link: /terms/slot text: slot) of another frame. With regard to (link: /terms/view text: views), a parent view encloses its child views.

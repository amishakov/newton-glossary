Title: Immediate

----

Date:

----

Text:

A value that is stored directly rather than through an indirect [reference](/terms/reference) to a [heap](/terms/heap) [object](/terms/object). Immediates are [characters](/terms/character), integers, or [Booleans](/terms/boolean).

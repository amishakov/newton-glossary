Title: Immediate

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), a value that is stored directly rather than through an indirect (link: /terms/reference text: reference) to a (link: /terms/heap text: heap) (link: /terms/object text: object). Immediates are (link: /terms/character text: characters), integers, or (link: /terms/boolean text: Booleans).

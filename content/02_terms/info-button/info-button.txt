Title: Info Button

----

Date: 06 December 2017

----

Text:

A small square (link: /terms/button text: button) an “I” character, located in the (link: /terms/status-bar text: status bar) of an (link: /terms/application text: application). When (link: /terms/tapped text: tapped), the button displays a (link: /terms/picker text: picker) which gives the user access to (link: /terms/preferences text: preferences), onscreen help, or general information about the application.

Also known as the (link: /terms/information-button text: Information Button) in earlier versions of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS).

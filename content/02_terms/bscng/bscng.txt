Boston Computer Society Newton Group

Formed by Al Willis in 1992, it later changed its name to (link: /terms/bospdaug text: BosPDAUG) (Boston PDA Users Group) and describing itself as “… an organization of professional and non-professional programmers and hardware designers for various PDA platforms including Pilot and Newton.”

Title: Out Box

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A list in the (link: /terms/inout-box text: InOut Box) application where sent or queued items can be can be viewed, filed, (link: /terms/routing text: routed), and otherwise managed. The (link: /terms/in-box text: In Box) and Out Box are separate (link: /terms/application text: applications) in earlier versions of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS).

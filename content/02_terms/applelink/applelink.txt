Title: AppleLink

----

Date: 19 March 2017

----

Text:

An online service used by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) between 1986 and 1994 to support and provide information to employees, dealers, developers, and end users. Newton devices could exchange messages with (link: /terms/applelink text: AppleLink) using the (link: /terms/newtonmail text: NewtonMail) service. AppleLink was eventually superseded by the short lived (link: /terms/eworld text: eWorld) service in 1994.

Title: Diamond

----

Date: 12 April 2017

----

Text:

A small symbol located adjacent to a text label or (link: /terms/button text: button) indicating that a pop-up menu or (link: /terms/picker text: picker) will appear when (link: /terms/tapped text: tapped).

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/black-diamond text: Black Diamond).

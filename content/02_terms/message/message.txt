Title: Message

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), an instruction to execute a (link: /terms/method text: method), as in a programmed (link: /terms/function text: function). Also referred to as a (link: /terms/symbol text: symbol) with a set of arguments. A message is sent using the message send syntax `frame:messageName()`, where the message `messageName` is sent to the (link: /terms/receiver text: receiver) `frame`.

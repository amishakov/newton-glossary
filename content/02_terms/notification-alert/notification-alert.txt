Title: Notification Alert

----

Date: 20 December 2023

----

Text:

An (link: /terms/alert-box text: alert box) that appears on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) conveying information that is useful to the user but does not present any “far-reaching” consequences such as a loss of data. Some notification alerts have a (link: /terms/snooze-button text: snooze button), which enables a user to temporarily dismiss the alert.

A notification alert includes a (link: /terms/close-box text: close box) and can be accompanied by an (link: /terms/alert-sound text: alert sound).

(image: /assets/images/notification-alert-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of a Notification Alert width: 208)

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/message-box text: Message Box), (link: /terms/notice text: Notice), and (link: /terms/notification-slip text: Notification Slip).

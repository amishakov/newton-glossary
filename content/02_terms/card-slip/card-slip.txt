Title: Card Slip

----

Date: 22 July 2024

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/container-view text: container view) that displays information about a (link: /terms/pc-card text: PC card) that is inserted into the (link: /terms/card-slot text: card slot) of a Newton device.

This information includes the name of the card and what type of card it is, such as a (link: /terms/storage-card text: storage card) or (link: /terms/modem text: modem). If the card is a storage card, additional information is displayed including used storage space, available storage space, whether or not there is a (link: /terms/backup text: backup) stored on the card, and whether or not the card is locked or (link: /terms/write-protected text: write-protected).

When viewing the information for a storage card, the (link: /terms/slip text: slip) can be used to rename the card. The user is also able to (link: /terms/backup text: backup) information to the card, and (link: /terms/restore text: restore) or erase information that has been previously stored on the card, providing the cardâ€™s (link: /terms/write-protection-switch text: write protection switch) is in the unlocked position.

(image: /assets/images/card-slip-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of a Card Slip displaying information about a flash storage card width: 216)

A card slip is displayed automatically when a PC card is inserted. It can also be accessed by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the (link: /terms/card text: Card) application in the (link: /terms/extras-drawer text: Extras Drawer).

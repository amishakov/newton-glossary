Title: Card Slip

----

Date: 22 July 2024

----

Text:

A [container view](/terms/container-view) that displays information about a [PC card](/terms/pc-card) that is inserted into the [card slot](/terms/card-slot) of a Newton device.

This information includes the name of the card and what type of card it is, such as a [storage card](/terms/storage-card) or [modem](/terms/modem). If the card is a storage card, additional information is displayed including used storage space, available storage space, whether or not there is a [backup](/terms/backup) stored on the card, and whether or not the card is locked or [write-protected](/terms/write-protected).

When viewing the information for a storage card, the [slip](/terms/slip) can be used to rename the card. The user is also able to [backup](/terms/backup) information to the card, and [restore](/terms/restore) or erase information that has been previously stored on the card, providing the cardâ€™s [write protection switch](/terms/write-protection-switch) is in the unlocked position.

(image: /assets/images/card-slip-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of a Card Slip displaying information about a flash storage card width: 216)

A card slip is displayed automatically when a PC card is inserted. It can also be accessed by [tapping](/terms/tapping) the [Card](/terms/card) application in the [Extras Drawer](/terms/extras-drawer).

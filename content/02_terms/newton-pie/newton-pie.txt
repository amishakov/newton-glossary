Title: Newton PIE

----

Date: 17 February 2024

----

Text:

A song written by [Avi Drissman](/sources/avi-drissman) in 1999 that was “dedicated to all the Newton engineers who made the Newton what it is” and sung to the tune of the [Don McLean](https://donmclean.com/) classic “American Pie”.

The “PIE” in the title references Apple’s [Personal Interactive Electronics](/terms/pie) division.

Title: VT100

----

Date: 14 July 2024

----

Text:

A video terminal developed by Digital Equipment Corporation (DEC) that was the first to support ANSI control codes, the eventual standard for other hardware terminals and terminal emulators.

(link: /terms/pt100 text: PT100) is terminal emulator (link: /terms/application text: application) for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) that is based on the VT100 standard.

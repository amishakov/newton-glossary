Title: VT100

----

Date: 14 July 2024

----

Text:

A video terminal developed by Digital Equipment Corporation (DEC) that was the first to support ANSI control codes, the eventual standard for other hardware terminals and terminal emulators.

[PT100](/terms/pt100) is terminal emulator [application](/terms/application) for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) that is based on the VT100 standard.

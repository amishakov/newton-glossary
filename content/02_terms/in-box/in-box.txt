Title: In Box

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A list in the (link: /terms/inout-box text: InOut Box) application where received items can be viewed, filed, (link: /terms/routing text: routed), and otherwise managed. The In Box and (link: /terms/out-box text: Out Box) are separate (link: /terms/application text: applications) in earlier versions of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS).

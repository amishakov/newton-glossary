Title: Newton Messaging Card

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/pc-card text: PC card) developed by [Motorola](https://www.motorola.com/) that allowed a Newton device to receive and store wireless messages, email, files, and other information over paging systems and news services. The Newton Messaging Card appears to have been based on the Motorola (link: /terms/newscard text: NewsCard), which shares similar specifications.

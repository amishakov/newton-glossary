Title: Y2010 Diagnostic

----

Date: 02 September 2017

----

Text:

A utility [package](/terms/package) created by [Eckhart Köppen](/sources/eckhart-koppen) that gathers information about scheduled alarms which may tigger an “infinite alarm loop” due to the [Year 2010 Problem](/terms/year-2010-problem). The utility allows the user to clear any alarms that are flagged as potentially troublesome.

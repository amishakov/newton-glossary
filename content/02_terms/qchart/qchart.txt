Text:

A graphing and charting (link: /terms/extension text: extension) for the (link: /terms/quickfigure-works text: QuickFigure Works) and (link: /terms/quickfigure-pro text: QuickFigure Pro) applications. QChart can display data from a (link: /terms/worksheet text: worksheet) as an x-y plot, line chart, bar chart, or pie chart.

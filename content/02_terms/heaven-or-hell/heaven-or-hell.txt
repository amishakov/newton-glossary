Title: Heaven or Hell

----

Date: 05 January 2025

----

Text:

A utility by Yosuke “Basuke” Suzuki that addresses the annoying -10061 error that occurs on certain Newton devices with a large number of installed (link: /terms/package text: packages). Heaven or Hell proactively checks for invalid (link: /terms/address text: addresses) in the (link: /terms/virtual-memory text: virtual memory) manager.

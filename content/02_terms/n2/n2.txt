Title: N2

----

Date: 31 May 2001

----

Text:

The code name of the low cost, high performance hardware platform built on the (link: /terms/voyager text: Voyager) chipset and the base platform for the (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000) and (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100) devices. The N2 platform could theoretically support up to four (link: /terms/pcmcia text: PCMCIA) slots, 4MB of (link: /terms/dram text: DRAM), 12MB of (link: /terms/flash-ram text: Flash RAM), and 16MB of (link: /terms/rom text: ROM). The platform was developed with third-party licensing in mind.

N2 was also one of the code names for the (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000).

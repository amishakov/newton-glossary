Title: N2

----

Date:

----

Text:

The code name of the low cost, high performance hardware platform built on the [Voyager](/terms/voyager) chipset and the base platform for the [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000) and [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100) devices. The N2 platform could theoretically support up to four [PCMCIA](/terms/pcmcia) slots, 4MB of [DRAM](/terms/dram), 12MB of [Flash RAM](/terms/flash-ram), and 16MB of [ROM](/terms/rom). The platform was developed with third-party licensing in mind.

N2 was also one of the code names for the [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000).

Title: Flash Card

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A memory (link: /terms/card text: card) using (link: /terms/flash-ram text: flash RAM) chips that can be rewritten and maintain the storage of information when power is removed. Commonly referred to as a (link: /terms/storage-card text: storage card). Not to be confused with a (link: /terms/compactflash text: CompactFlash) (CF) card, which is similar in function but different in size and implementation.

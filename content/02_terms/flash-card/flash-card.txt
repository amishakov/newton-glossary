Title: Flash Card

----

Date:

----

Text:

A memory [card](/terms/card) using [flash RAM](/terms/flash-ram) chips that can be rewritten and maintain the storage of information when power is removed. Commonly referred to as a [storage card](/terms/storage-card). Not to be confused with a [CompactFlash](/terms/compactflash) (CF) card, which is similar in function but different in size and implementation.

Title: NetHopper

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

The first commercial web browser for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) developed by (link: /sources/allpen text: AllPen Software) and released in August 1996.

NetHopper began as an (link: /terms/application text: application) that allowed Newton devices to access the web through a Mac OS computer running as an internet (link: /terms/gateway text: gateway). Subsequent versions of NetHopper used Apple’s (link: /terms/newton-internet-enabler text: Newton Internet Enabler) (NIE) software to connect directly to the internet through a (link: /terms/modem text: modem) or other network connection.

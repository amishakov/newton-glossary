Title: Bootstrapping

----

Date: 22 April 2024

----

Text:

The process of installing (link: /terms/package text: packages) on a Newton device without a serial or network connection. Usually performed using a (link: /terms/bootstrap-card text: bootstrap card).

The term “bootstrap” refers to using the straps attached to boots in order to help put them on.

Title: Newton Partners Program

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A defunct [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) development support program which provided comprehensive technical and marketing resources to Newton developers beyond what was available through the entry-level (link: /terms/newton-associates-program text: Newton Associates Program). The Newton Partners Program was preceded by the (link: /terms/pie-partners-program text: PIE Partners Program).

Also referred to as (link: /terms/npp text: NPP).

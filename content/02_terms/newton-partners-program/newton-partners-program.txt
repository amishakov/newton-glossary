Title: Newton Partners Program

----

Date:

----

Text:

A defunct [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) development support program which provided comprehensive technical and marketing resources to Newton developers beyond what was available through the entry-level [Newton Associates Program](/terms/newton-associates-program). The Newton Partners Program was preceded by the [PIE Partners Program](/terms/pie-partners-program). Also referred to as [NPP](/terms/npp).

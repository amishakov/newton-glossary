Title: Newton Messaging Enabler

----

Date: 05 November 2017

----

Text:

A developer tool for enabling wireless messaging devices under (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) 2.0 and later. The enabler (link: /terms/package text: package) works in conjunction with an (link: /terms/extension text: extension) known as a (link: /terms/message-module text: message module), which provides communication level functionality for a specific messaging device, such as the (link: /terms/newton-messaging-card text: Newton Messaging Card), Socket (link: /terms/pagecard text: PageCard), or Motorola (link: /terms/tango text: Tango) pager.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/nme text: NME).

Title: Function Key

----

Date: 22 August 2025

----

Text:

A key located on an (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) that provides a shortcut or specific action such as closing a (link: /terms/slip text: slip), launching an (link: /terms/application text: application), toggling the (link: /terms/backlight text: backlight), or other actions usually found in the (link: /terms/button-bar text: button bar) on other Newton devices.

Function keys are located in their own row at the top of the eMate keyboard.

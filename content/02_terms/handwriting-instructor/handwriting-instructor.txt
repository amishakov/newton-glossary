Title: Handwriting Instructor

----

Date: 03 September 2017

----

Text:

The built-in [application](/terms/application) which provides an interactive tutorial with information regarding [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) and setting the various [preferences](/terms/preferences) related to writing and drawing on a Newton device.

Also referred to as [HW Instructor](/terms/hw-instructor).

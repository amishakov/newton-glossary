Title: Implant-2000

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A hardware accelerator upgrade for the (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000) and (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100) developed by [Oliver Harms](http://www.pixsolution.com/) and his Pixsolution Group in Germany. Both the Implant-2000 and the (link: /terms/messagepad-130 text: MessagePad 130) for (link: /terms/messagepad-130 text: MessagePad 130) devices consist of a small (link: /terms/daughterboard text: daughterboard) connected to the (link: /terms text: motherboard).

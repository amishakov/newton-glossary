Title: Cadenza Connect

----

Date: 27 September 2024

----

Text:

An (link: /terms/application text: application) that could be installed on Newton device as part of the (link: /terms/cadenza text: Cadenza) software suite. Cadenza Connect allowed users to (link: /terms/synchronize text: synchronize) contacts from the (link: /terms/name-file text: Name File), (link: /terms/to-do-list text: To Do List) tasks, and (link: /terms/date-book text: Date Book) items with Lotus Notes.

Title: Structured List

----

Date: 18 September 2024

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/user-interface text: user interface) element for entering structured or hierarchal information such as an (link: /terms/outline text: outline) or (link: /terms/checklist text: checklist) consisting of multiple topics. Each topic in the structured list has a small circular (link: /terms/topic-marker text: topic marker). If the structured list is a checklist, a (link: /terms/checkbox text: checkbox) is also displayed next to each topic. A topic can be moved above or below another topic by (link: /terms/dragging text: dragging) the topic marker.

Title: ParaGraph

----

Date: 07 September 2017

----

Text:

Another name for the cursive (or connected) recognizer according to the (link: /sources/catamount text: Catamount Software) handwriting tips page. Likely in reference to (link: /sources/paragraph text: ParaGraph International), the company who created the (link: /terms/calligrapher text: CalliGrapher) handwriting recognition engine.

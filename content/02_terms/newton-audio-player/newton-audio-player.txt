Title: Newton Audio Player

----

Date: 05 September 2025

----

Text:

Software developed by (link: /terms/brad-tober text: Brad Tober) that allows playback of self-contained audio files on a Newton device. Newton Audio Player and (link: /terms/newton-movie-player text: Newton Movie Player) are based on the same underlying software known as the (link: /terms/newton-media-system text: Newton Media System).

(image: /assets/images/newton-audio-player-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of Newton Audio Player width: 216)

Also referred to as (link: /terms/nmp text: NAP).

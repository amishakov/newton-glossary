Title: Grouper 100MP

----

Date: 01 October 2017

----

Text:

A member of the [Grouper](/terms/grouper) family of devices developed by [Digital Ocean](/sources/digital-ocean), this radio-based peripheral provided [wireless AppleTalk](/terms/wireless-appletalk) connectivity over a [local area network](/terms/local-area-network). Identical in functionality to the [Grouper 110MP](/terms/grouper-110mp), but designed to fit the case of a [MessagePad 100](/terms/messagepad-100).

Also referred to as a [100MP](/terms/100mp).

Resource:

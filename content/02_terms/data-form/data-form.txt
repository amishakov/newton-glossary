Title: Data Form

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript), a [symbol](/terms/symbol) that describes the transformations that must occur when data is exchanged with other environments. When you send data or set [endpoint options](/terms/endpoint-option), the data form defines how to convert the data from its NewtonScript format. When you receive data or get endpoint options, the data form defines the type of data expected.

Title: Ko Isono

----

Date: 26 January 2025

----

Text:

A software engineer at [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) who worked on portions of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS).

A few weeks before the first scheduled demonstration of the Newton at the 1993 Consumer Electronics Show in Las Vegas, Isono died of a self-inflicted gunshot wound.

In Newton OS 1.0, an (link: /terms/easter-egg text: easter egg) known as (link: /terms/about-newton text: About Newton) displays the names of members of the Newton development team followed by the message _“In Memory of Ko Isono”_.

The tribute was removed in later versions of the (link: /terms/operating-system text: operating system).

Title: PhoneNET

----

Date: 23 June 2024

----

Text:

An implementation of [AppleTalk](/terms/appletalk) networking developed by Farallon Communications. PhoneNET used [Mini-DIN](/terms/mini-din) connectors to connect to [LocalTalk](/terms/localtalk) ports found on printers and Mac OS computers combined with standard [RJ-11](/terms/rj-11) jacks and four-conductor telephone wire, enabling it share the same cable used by existing telephone wiring.

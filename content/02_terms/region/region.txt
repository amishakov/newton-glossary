Title: Region

----

Date: 03 October 2014

----

Text:

In [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript), an arbitrary area or set of areas on the coordinate plane, specifically when referring to the contents of a [view](/terms/view). The outline of a region should be one or more closed loops.

Title: Store

----

Date:

----

Text:

A physical repository that can contain [soups](/terms/soup) and [packages](/terms/package). A store is the equivalent of a disk volume on a [desktop computer](/terms/desktop-computer) or [laptop](/terms/laptop-computer).

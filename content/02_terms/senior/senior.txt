Title: Senior

----

Date:

----

Text:

The code name of the larger, unreleased [MessagePad](/terms/messagepad) device also known as the [Newton Plus](/terms/newton-plus). The debugger [ROM](/terms/rom) for the [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000) and [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100) devices was referred to as the “Senior Cirrus Debug”, perpetuating the theory that the Newton Plus actually existed and ended up being developed as the MessagePad 2000.

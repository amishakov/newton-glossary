Title: Class

----

Date:

----

Text:

A [symbol](/terms/symbol) that describes the data referenced by an [object](/terms/object). In [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript), [arrays](/terms/array), [frames](/terms/frame), and [binary objects](/terms/binary-object) can have user-defined classes.

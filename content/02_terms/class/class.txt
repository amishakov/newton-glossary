Title: Class

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/symbol text: symbol) that describes the data referenced by an (link: /terms/object text: object). In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), (link: /terms/array text: arrays), (link: /terms/frame text: frames), and (link: /terms/binary-object text: binary objects) can have user-defined classes.

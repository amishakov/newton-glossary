Title: ExpertPad

----

Date: 31 May 2001

----

Text:

A Newton device developed by (link: /terms/Sharp text: Sharp). Two versions of the ExpertPad were produced, the ExpertPad (link: /terms/pi-7000 text: PI-7000) was equivalent to the (link: /terms/original-messagepad text: Original MessagePad) (OMP) running Newton OS 1.0 or 1.1 and the ExpertPad (link: /terms/pi-7100 text: PI-7100) was equivalent to a (link: /terms/messagepad-100 text: MessagePad 100) running Newton OS 1.3.

An unreleased Japanese version of the ExpertPad was code named (link: /terms/galileo text: Galileo).

A third-party device that utilizes a licensed version of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) is commonly referred to as a (link: /terms/clone text: clone).

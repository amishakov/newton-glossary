Title: Connection

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The built-in (link: /terms/application text: application) used to perform the action of connecting or docking to another computer. In prerelease versions of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) 1.0, the application is named (link: /terms/computer text: Computer). In Newton OS 2.0 and later, the application is named (link: /terms/dock text: Dock).

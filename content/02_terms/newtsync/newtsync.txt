Title: NewtSync

----

Date:

----

Text:

Software developed by John Anderson of [Everchanging Software](https://web.archive.org/web/20040611090738/http://www.everchanging.com/newton/) which facilitated the [synchronization](/terms/synchronization) of data between a Newton device and a [desktop computer](/terms/desktop-computer) or [laptop](/terms/laptop-computer) running Mac OS X.

NewtSync was originally known as [nSync](/terms/nsync).

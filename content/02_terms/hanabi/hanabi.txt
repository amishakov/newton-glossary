Title: Hanabi

----

Date: 19 January 2025

----

Text:

A utility developed by Yoshiyuki Hashimoto that provides an easy way to (link: /terms/input text: input) Japanese Hiragana (link: /terms/character text: characters) on a Newton device.

Using the (link: /terms/tapbar text: TapBar) button manager, Hanabi implemented a set of multifunction (link: /terms/button text: buttons) in a (link: /terms/floating-palette text: floating palette).

Title: Minspector

----

Date: 15 March 2018

----

Text:

A debugging tool for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) developed by [Mason Mark](/sources/mason-mark) of [Five Speed Software](http://www.fivespeedsoftware.com/). Although Minspector runs on the Newton platform, its appearance is modeled after the [Inspector](/terms/inspector) from the Mac OS version of [Newton Toolkit](/terms/newton-toolkit).

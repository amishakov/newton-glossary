Title: Minspector

----

Date: 15 March 2018

----

Text:

A debugging tool for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) developed by (link: /sources/mason-mark text: Mason Mark) of [Five Speed Software](http://www.fivespeedsoftware.com/). Although Minspector runs on the Newton platform, its appearance is modeled after the (link: /terms/inspector text: Inspector) from the Mac OS version of (link: /terms/newton-toolkit text: Newton Toolkit).

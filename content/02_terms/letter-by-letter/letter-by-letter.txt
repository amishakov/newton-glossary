Title: Letter-by-Letter

----

Date: 07 November 2017

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) setting which controls how (link: /terms/cursive text: cursive) text that is not in the (link: /terms/dictionary text: dictionary) is recognized. Essentially, written text is evaluated one letter at a time until a match is made and the text is recognized.

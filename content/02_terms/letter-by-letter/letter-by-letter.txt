Title: Letter-by-Letter

----

Date: 07 November 2017

----

Text:

A [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) setting which controls how [cursive](/terms/cursive) text that is not in the [dictionary](/terms/dictionary) is recognized. Essentially, written text is evaluated one letter at a time until a match is made and the text is recognized.

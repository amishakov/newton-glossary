Title: Newton Book

----

Date:

----

Text:

An electronic document designed to be viewed on a Newton device. A Newton book is created using software such as [Newton Press](/terms/newton-press) or [Newton Book Maker](/terms/newton-book-maker) and delivered as an installable [package](/terms/package). Also referred to as a [Newton Digital Book](/terms/newton-digital-book).

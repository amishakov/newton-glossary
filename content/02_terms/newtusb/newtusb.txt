Title: NewtUSB

----

Date: 15 May 2026

----

Text:

A hardware accessory designed by (link: /sources/adriano-angelillis text: Adriano Angelillis) that converted a standard (link: /terms/usb text: USB) port to a (link: /terms/mini-din text: Mini-DIN) port for connecting to a Newton device. An additional (link: /terms/dongle text: dongle) allowed the NewtUSB adapter to charge and (link: /terms/synchronize text: synchronize) a device through the (link: /terms/newton-interconnect text: Newton InterConnect) port.

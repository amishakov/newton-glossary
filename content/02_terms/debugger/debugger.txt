Title: Debugger

----

Date:

----

Text:

A tool used to monitor, control, and evaluate software or code being developed. The goal of [debugging]/terms/debugging) is to locate and fix “bugs” or otherwise unintended behaviour in a piece of software. [ViewFrame](/terms/viewframe) is an example of a debugger for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os).

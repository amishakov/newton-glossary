Title: Debugger

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A tool used to monitor, control, and evaluate software or code being developed. The goal of (link: /terms/debugging text: debugging) is to locate and fix “bugs” or otherwise unintended behaviour in a piece of software. (link: /terms/viewframe text: ViewFrame) is an example of a debugger for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS).

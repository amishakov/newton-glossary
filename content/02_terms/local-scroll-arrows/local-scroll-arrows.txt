Title: Local Scroll Arrows

----

Date: 30 October 2001

----

Text:

A pair of icon-based (link: /terms/button text: buttons) modeled after standard (link: /terms/scroll-arrows text: scroll arrows) that are used to control a (link: /terms/scrolling-list text: scrolling list) of text items within a specific (link: /terms/application text: application).

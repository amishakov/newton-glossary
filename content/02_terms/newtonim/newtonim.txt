Title: NewtonIM

----

Date: 30 November 2017

----

Text:

An instant messaging client for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) developed by [Jake Bordens](/sources/jake-bordens) and [Paul Guyot](/sources/paul-guyot). NewtonIM is compatible with the [Jabber](https://www.jabber.org/) protocol, but only implements a subset of its features.

Also referred to as [NIM](/terms/nim).

Title: NewtonIM

----

Date: 30 November 2017

----

Text:

An instant messaging client for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) developed by (link: /sources/jake-bordens text: Jake Bordens) and (link: /sources/paul-guyot text: Paul Guyot). NewtonIM is compatible with the [Jabber](https://www.jabber.org/) protocol, but only implements a subset of its features.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/nim text: NIM).

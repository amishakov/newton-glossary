Title: Frozen Package

----

Date:

----

Text:

A [package](/terms/package) that has been suspended by [freezing](/terms/freezing) and is not active. When you [freeze](/terms/freeze) a package, the [icon](/terms/icon) changes to a [snowflake](/terms/snowflake).

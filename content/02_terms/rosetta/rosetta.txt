Title: Rosetta

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The code name of the software-based [print recognizer](print-recognizer) system developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/). Rosetta first shipped in the second revision of the (link: /terms/messagepad-120 text: MessagePad 120) device. The name was eventually changed to (link: /terms/mondello text: Mondello) for legal reasons. Although never used publicly, the official product name for the print recognizer was (link: /terms/neuropen text: Neuropen).

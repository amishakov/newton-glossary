Title: Local Area Network

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A data communications network that covers a small or geographically limited area. A (link: /terms/lan text: LAN) typically facilitates the interconnection of computers and other devices within a single building or multiple adjacent buildings. (link: /terms/ethernet text: Ethernet) and (link: /terms/localtalk text: LocalTalk) are examples of local area networks.

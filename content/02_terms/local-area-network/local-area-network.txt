Title: Local Area Network

----

Date:

----

Text:

A data communications network that covers a small or geographically limited area. A [LAN](/terms/lan) typically facilitates the interconnection of computers and other devices within a single building or multiple adjacent buildings. [Ethernet](/terms/ethernet) and [LocalTalk](/terms/localtalk) are examples of local area networks.

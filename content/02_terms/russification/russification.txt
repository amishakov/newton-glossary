Title: Russification

----

Date: 08 April 2017

----

Text:

A term coined by (link: /sources/anton-balaban text: Anton Balaban) to describe the process of using the Russian language on a Newton device. Russification generally involves installing Cyrillic (link: /terms/font text: fonts), a special (link: /terms/keyboard text: keyboard) layout, (link: /terms/system-patch text: system patches) to modify various bits of text, and other software (link: /terms/package text: packages) to localize the English language version of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS).

Once a Newton device has gone through this process, it is said to have been (link: /terms/russified text: Russified).

Title: Cliphoard

----

Date: 30 January 2025

----

Text:

An (link: /terms/extension text: extension) created by (link: /sources/jeremy-wyld text: Jeremy Wyld) that adds a setting to the (link: /terms/prefs text: Prefs) application allowing the user to specify how many (link: /terms/clipboard text: clipboards) are available on (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000), (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100), and (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) devices.

Support for multiple clipboards was introduced in (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) 2.0, but the feature was not enabled by default.

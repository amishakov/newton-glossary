Title: WikiWikiNewt

----

Date: 07 June 2024

----

Text:

A defunct wiki-based reference site for the Newton platform created by [Victor Rehorst](/sources/victor-rehorst) in 2002. The current caretaker of WikiWikiNewt is [Morgan Aldridge](/sources/morgan-aldridge), who also maintains the [UNNA](/terms/unna) software repository. He plans on resurrecting the site at some point.

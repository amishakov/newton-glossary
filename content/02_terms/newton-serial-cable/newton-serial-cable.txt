Title: Newton Serial Cable

----

Date: 20 May 2025

----

Text:

A cable sold by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) for connecting a Newton device to a Mac OS or Windows computer via the serial connector. Different cables were available for each platform, the Mac OS cable featuring a (link: /terms/mini-din text: Mini-DIN) connector and the Windows version sporting a D-sub connector.

Newton serial cables were included as part of the (link: /terms/newton-connection-kit text: Newton Connection Kit).

Title: J & K Sales

----

Date: 12 April 2017

----

Text:

A reseller of new and used Newton hardware, software, parts, and related accessories. While specializing in the Newton platform, J & K Sales also sold Fujitsu (link: /terms/tablet-computer text: tablet computers) and other portable systems. The business folded in 2009 with the remaining inventory being sold off sometime in 2012.

Title: Bubble

----

Date: 13 September 2017

----

Text:

A colloquial term for one of the [hard buttons](/terms/hard-button) or [silkscreen buttons](/terms/silkscreen-button) found in the [button bar](/terms/button-bar) below the [screen](/terms/screen) on early prototype Newton devices.

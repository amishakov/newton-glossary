Title: Action

----

Date: 24 March 2017

----

Text:

An event or behaviour that is triggered when a specific item is selected in an [action picker](/terms/action-picker), a [key combination](/terms/key-combination) is pressed, or a [request word](/terms/request-word) is processed by the [assist](/terms/assist) function.

For example, entering the “call” [request word](/terms/request-word) into the [assist drawer](/terms/assist-drawer) will trigger an action to dial a telephone number.

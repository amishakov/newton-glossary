Title: Action

----

Date: 24 March 2017

----

Text:

An (link: /terms/event text: event) or behaviour that is triggered when a specific item is selected in an (link: /terms/action-picker text: action picker), a (link: /terms/key-combination text: key combination) is pressed, or a (link: /terms/request-word text: request word) is processed by the (link: /terms/assist text: Assist) function.

For example, entering the “call” (link: /terms/request-word text: request word) into the (link: /terms/assist-slip text: assist slip) will trigger an action to dial a telephone number.

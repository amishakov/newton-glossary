Title: Battery Contact Bounce

----

Date: 19 May 2022

----

Text:

A momentary loss of contact between the battery and its connection to circuitry when a Newton device is tossed or dropped onto a hard surface. When the power from the battery is disrupted, the device can [reset](/terms/system-reset). If a Newton device is in the middle of a storage update when this occurs, some data will be lost.

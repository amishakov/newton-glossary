Title: Macintosh Programmers Workshop

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A comprehensive collection of core development tools from [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) designed to support C, C++, and assembly language programmers developing Mac OS software.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/mpw text: MPW).

Macintosh Programmers Workshop is required by (link: /terms/newton-c-tools text: Newton C++ Tools) when creating or preparing C++ code for the Newton.

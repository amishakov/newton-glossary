Title: Grouper 100MPS

----

Date: 01 October 2017

----

Text:

A member of the (link: /terms/grouper text: Grouper) family of devices developed by (link: /sources/digital-ocean text: Digital Ocean), this radio-based peripheral provided (link: /terms/wireless-appletalk text: wireless AppleTalk) connectivity over a (link: /terms/local-area-network text: local area network). Basically, an upgraded version of the (link: /terms/grouper-110mp text: Grouper 110MP), designed to fit the case of a (link: /terms/messagepad-110 text: MessagePad 110).

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/100mps text: 100MPS).

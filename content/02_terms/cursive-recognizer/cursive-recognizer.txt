Title: Cursive Recognizer

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The algorithmic component of the (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) system that identifies handwritten or (link: /terms/cursive text: cursive) characters, as opposed to printed or block characters.

Also referred to as the (link: /terms/connected-recognizer text: Connected Recognizer).

Title: Charging Status Light

----

Date: 21 February 2025

----

Text:

An (link: /terms/led text: LED) located on the top of an (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) device that indicates the whether the (link: /terms/battery text: battery) is being (link: /terms/charged text: charged) or not.

When the light is green, the battery is fully charged. When the light is orange the battery is (link: /terms/charging text: charging). If the light is off, a power adapter is not currently plugged into the device.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/charging-light text: Charging Light).

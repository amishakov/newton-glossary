Title: Backup Battery

----

Date: 03 April 2017

----

Text:

A small (link: /terms/battery text: battery) that supplies power to a Newton device when the main batteries are removed. Without a backup battery, early Newton devices such as the (link: /terms/messagepad-100 text: MessagePad 100) will lose any information stored in (link: /terms/ram text: RAM).

Title: Backup Battery

----

Date: 03 April 2017

----

Text:

A small [battery](/terms/battery) that supplies power to a Newton device when the main batteries are removed. Without a backup battery, early Newton devices such as the [MessagePad 100](/terms/messagepad-100) will lose any information stored in [RAM](/terms/ram).

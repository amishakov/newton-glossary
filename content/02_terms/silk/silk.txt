Title: Silk

----

Date:

----

Text:

A [package](/terms/package) developed by [Catamount Software](/sources/catamount) that enhances the activation of the [backlight](/terms/backlight). Silk also provides a floating [palette](/terms/palette) of standard controls on Newton devices equipped with [hard buttons](/terms/hard-button), otherwise known as [silkscreen buttons](/terms/silkscreen-button).

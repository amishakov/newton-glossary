Title: Slip

----

Date:

----

Text:

A [container view](/terms/container-view) with a [matte border](/terms/matte-border) displayed by an [application](/terms/application) in order to obtain user [input](/terms/input), or to present alternatives which a user may choose to determine the outcome of a task that has been started.

For the protocol, see [SLIP](/terms/slip-protocol).

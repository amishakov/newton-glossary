Austin Newton Users Group

Founded by Deb Ayers, Rob Cox, and Kent Spaulding in October 1995 as a way for enthusiasts to explore mobile technologies. ANUG eventually changed its name to the Austin Digital Assistant Users Group ([ADAUG](/terms/adaug)) as a refection of the expanding marketplace.

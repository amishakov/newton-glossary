Title: Bar Gauge

----

Date: 20 September 2024

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/user-interface text: user interface) element consisting of a horizontal rectangle which is filled from left to right, as a visual (link: /terms/indicator text: indicator) of the current progress in relation to an entire operation or process.

The (link: /terms/battery-gauge text: battery gauge) is an example of a bar (link: /terms/gauge text: gauge).

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/thermometer-gauge text: Thermometer Gauge).

Title: Going Up In Smoke

----

Date: 30 July 2024

----

Text:

The animation which appears after deleting text or ink drawings by [scrubbing](/terms/scrubbing) with the [pen](/terms/pen) on the [screen](/terms/screen) of a Newton device. The “dissipating smoke” is accompanied by a “[poof](/terms/poof)” sound.

Also referred to the [puff of smoke](/terms/puff-of-smoke) and [smoke clouds](/terms/smoke-clouds).

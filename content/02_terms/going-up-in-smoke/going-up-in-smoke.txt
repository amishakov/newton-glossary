Title: Going Up In Smoke

----

Date: 30 July 2024

----

Text:

The animation which appears after deleting text or ink drawings by (link: /terms/scrubbing text: scrubbing) with the (link: /terms/pen text: pen) on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) of a Newton device. The “(link: /terms/dissipating-smoke text: dissipating smoke)” is accompanied by a “(link: /terms/poof text: poof)” sound.

Title: Unflatten

----

Date: 21 November 2017

----

Text:

To convert a stream of bytes from a soup [entry](/terms/entry) into a frame [object](/terms/object). To reverse this process, you would [flatten](/terms/flatten) the [soup](/terms/soup) entry.

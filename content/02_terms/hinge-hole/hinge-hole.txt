Title: Hinge Hole

----

Date: 23 October 2024

----

Text:

A small opening located on the case of a (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad) device that accepts a (link: /terms/hinge-tab text: hinge tab).

On (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000) and (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100) devices, unfastening the (link: /terms/lid text: lid) involves retracting one of the hinge tabs from a hinge hole by sliding a (link: /terms/small-latch text: small latch).

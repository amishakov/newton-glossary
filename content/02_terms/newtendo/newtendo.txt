Title: Newtendo

----

Date: 05 November 2017

----

Text:

An emulator [application](/terms/application) developed by [Eagan Rackley](/sources/eagan-rackley) that allows [Nintendo Entertainment System](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nintendo_Entertainment_System) (NES) games to be played on a Newton device. To do this, an NES [ROM](/terms/rom) image is converted into a [package](/terms/package) using the [NES Packager](/terms/nes-packager) utility.

Title: Newtendo

----

Date: 05 November 2017

----

Text:

An emulator (link: /terms/application text: application) developed by (link: /sources/eagan-rackley text: Eagan Rackley) that allows [Nintendo Entertainment System](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nintendo_Entertainment_System) (NES) games to be played on a Newton device. To do this, an NES (link: /terms/rom text: ROM) image is converted into a (link: /terms/package text: package) using the (link: /terms/nes-packager text: NES Packager) utility.

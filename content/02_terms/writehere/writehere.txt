Title: WriteHere

----

Date: 08 November 2017

----

Text:

A suite of software components developed by [Adam Tow](/sources/adam-tow) that allows a Newton device or Palm organizer to be used as a [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) engine and input device when connected to a [desktop computer](/terms/desktop-computer) or [laptop computer](/terms/laptop-computer).

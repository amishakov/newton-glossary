Title: Local

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/variable text: variable) whose scope is the (link: /terms/function text: function) within which it is defined. In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), you must use the local keyword to explicitly create a local variable within a function.

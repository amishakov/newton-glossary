Title: Request Word

----

Date: 24 March 2017

----

Text:

A term or phrase that triggers an [action](/terms/action) when processed by the [Assist](/terms/assist) function.

For example, entering the “call” request word into the [assist slip](/terms/assist-slip) will trigger an action to dial a telephone number.

Request words can have [synonyms](/terms/synonyms) for a action. For example, “ring”, “dial”, and “phone” are synonyms for the request word “call”.

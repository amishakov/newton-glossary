Title: Request Word

----

Date: 24 March 2017

----

Text:

A term or phrase that triggers an (link: /terms/action text: action) when processed by the (link: /terms/assist text: Assist) function.

For example, entering the “call” request word into the (link: /terms/assist-slip text: Assist slip) will trigger an action to dial a telephone number.

Request words can have (link: /terms/synonyms text: synonyms) for a action. For example, “ring”, “dial”, and “phone” are synonyms for the request word “call”.

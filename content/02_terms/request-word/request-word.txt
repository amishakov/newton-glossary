Title: Request Word

----

Date: 24 March 2017

----

Text:

A term or phrase that triggers an [action](/terms/action) when processed by the [assist](/terms/assist) function.

For example, entering the “call” request word into the [assist drawer](/terms/assist-drawer) will trigger an action to dial a telephone number.

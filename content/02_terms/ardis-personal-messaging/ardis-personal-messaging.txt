Title: ARDIS Personal Messaging

----

Date: 04 June 2024

----

Text:

A service based on the [ARDIS](/terms/ardis) wireless data network that provided peer-to-peer messaging, fax services, notifications, and dialup connectivity.

Also referred to as [ARDIS PM](/terms/ardis-pm) in the [Newton Technology Journal](/terms/newton-technology-journal).

Title: NotePhone

----

Date: 13 November 2017

----

Text:

A Newton device developed by [Siemens](https://www.siemens.com/) for the German market. Based on the (link: /terms/original-messagepad text: Original MessagePad), the NotePhone integrated a telephone, fax (link: /terms/modem text: modem), and compatibility with PBX switchboards into a single package. Siemens also developed a (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) prototype known as the (link: /terms/siemens-online-terminal text: Siemens Online Terminal).

A third-party device that utilizes a licensed version of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) is commonly referred to as a (link: /terms/clone text: clone).

Title: NotePhone

----

Date: 13 November 2017

----

Text:

A Newton device developed by [Siemens](https://www.siemens.com/) for the German market. Based on the [Original MessagePad](/terms/original-messagepad), the NotePhone integrated a telephone, fax [modem](/terms/modem), and compatibility with PBX switchboards into a single package. Siemens also developed a [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) prototype known as the [Siemens Online Terminal](/terms/siemens-online-terminal).

A third-party device that utilizes a licensed version of [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) is commonly referred to as a [clone](/terms/clone).

Title: QSort

----

Date: 23 February 2025

----

Text:

An (link: /terms/extension text: extension) for the (link: /terms/quickfigure-pro text: QuickFigure Pro) application that provides sorting tools when working with a (link: /terms/worksheet text: worksheet). Ranges of cells can be sorted by numeric, text, or date criteria, as well as in ascending or descending order.

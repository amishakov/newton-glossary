Title: ROM

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

Read-Only Memory

A type of non-volatile data storage which is manufactured with permanent data.

(link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) resides in ROM and cannot be changed or (link: /terms/delete text: deleted) directly.

Also known as (link: /terms/mrom text: MROM) (Mask Read-Only Memory) because the stored data is determined during the masking stage of manufacturing process.

Title: Kiosk

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A navigational page in a (link: /terms/newton-book text: Newton book). (link: /terms/tapping text: Tapping) an item on the kiosk page — such as a picture — takes the user directly to the subject matter it represents.

Title: Apple Mobile Message System

----

Date: 18 January 2024

----

Text:

A multi-platform, integrated wireless communications service that supported Newton devices, PowerBook laptops, and certain computers running Microsoft Windows. The system combined the (link: /terms/pagecard text: PageCard) hardware with software that allowed sending and receiving email over the now defunct PageNet wireless network.

Apple Mobile Message System was replaced by the (link: /terms/swims text: SWiMS) service provided by [Socket Communications](https://web.archive.org/web/19980208233349/http://www.socketcom.com/), makers of the PageCard.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/amms text: AMMS).

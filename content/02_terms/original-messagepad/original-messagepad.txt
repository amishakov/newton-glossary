Title: Original MessagePad

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

The first commercially released Newton model. Not to be confused with the (link: /terms/messagepad-100 text: MessagePad 100) which shares the same model number ((link: /terms/h1000 text: H1000)) and form factor. The prerelease version of the device was originally called the (link: /terms/notepad text: Notepad). The name was changed to (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad) to better reflect its communication capabilities.

The Original MessagePad is commonly referred to as (link: /terms/omp text: OMP).

The term “Original MessagePad” can also refer to any non-upgraded MessagePad, such as a (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000) without the optional memory expansion.

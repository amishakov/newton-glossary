Title: Snooze Picker

----

Date: 20 December 2023

----

Text:

A list of times in minutes that an alert or reminder can be postponed. Activated by [tapping](/terms/tapping) on the [snooze button](/terms/snooze-button) in a [notification alert](/terms/notification-alert).

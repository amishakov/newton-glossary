Title: Snooze Picker

----

Date: 20 December 2023

----

Text:

A list of times in minutes that an alert or reminder can be postponed. Activated by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) on the (link: /terms/snooze-button text: snooze button) in a (link: /terms/notification-alert text: notification alert).

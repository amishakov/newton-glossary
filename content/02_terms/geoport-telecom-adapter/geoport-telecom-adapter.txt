Title: GeoPort Telecom Adapter

----

Date: 26 June 2024

----

Text:

A external hardware device manufactured by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) based on the (link: /terms/geoport text: GeoPort) serial data standard. Essentially a large (link: /terms/dongle text: dongle), the adapter provided high speed (link: /terms/modem text: modem), telephony, and fax functionality through software.

The GeoPort Telecom Adapter was designed to work specifically with certain PowerPC-based Mac OS computers. Although the (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) is equipped with a GeoPort connector, but it is unknown whether it is actually compatible with the adapter.

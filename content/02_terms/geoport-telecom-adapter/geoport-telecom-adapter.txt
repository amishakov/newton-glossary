Title: GeoPort Telecom Adapter

----

Date: 26 June 2024

----

Text:

A external hardware device manufactured by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) based on the [GeoPort](/terms/geoport) serial data standard. Essentially a large [dongle](/terms/dongle), the adapter provided high speed [modem](/terms/modem), telephony, and fax functionality through software.

The GeoPort Telecom Adapter was designed to work specifically with certain PowerPC-based Mac OS computers. Although the [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) is equipped with a GeoPort connector, but it is unknown whether it is actually compatible with the adapter.

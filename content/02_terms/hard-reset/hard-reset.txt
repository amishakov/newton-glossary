Title: Hard Reset

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A method of (link: /terms/reset text: resetting) the (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) which completely erases all data in the internal memory of a Newton device. A hard reset erases all (link: /terms/application text: applications) and (link: /terms/package text: packages) installed by the user, and resets (link: /terms/pen text: pen) alignment, date, time, (link: /terms/time-zones text: time zone), and other (link: /terms/preferences text: preferences).

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/brain-wipe text: brain wipe).

Not to be confused with a (link: /terms/cold-boot text: cold boot), which also removes any (link: /terms/system-update text: system updates) that have been installed on certain Newton devices.

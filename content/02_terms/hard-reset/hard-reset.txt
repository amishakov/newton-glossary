Title: Hard Reset

----

Date:

----

Text:

A method of [resetting](/terms/reset) the [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) which completely erases all data in the internal memory of a Newton device. A hard reset erases all [applications](/terms/application) and [packages](/terms/package) installed by the user, and resets [pen](/terms/pen) alignment, date, time, time zone, and other [preferences](/terms/preferences).

Also referred to as a [Brain Wipe](/terms/brain-wipe).

Not to be confused with a [Cold Boot](/terms/cold-boot), which also removes any [system updates](/terms/system-update) that have been installed.

Title: Modem Setup

----

Date: 19 July 2025

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/package text: package) that provides configuration settings for a modem. Along with the (link: /terms/newton-modem-enabler text: Newton Modem Enabler) software, modem setup packages allowed the use of third-party modems with Newton devices.

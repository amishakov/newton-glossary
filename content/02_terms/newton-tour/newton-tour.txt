Title: Newton Tour

----

Date: 29 August 2018

----

Text:

The built-in (link: /terms/application text: application) which provides an interactive tutorial for learning the basics of a (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad) device and its various features. On an (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) device, a similar tutorial is named (link: /terms/emate-tour text: eMate Tour).

The (link: /terms/getting-started-card text: Getting Started Card) that shipped with the (link: /terms/original-messagepad text: Original MessagePad) and (link: /terms/messagepad-100 text: MessagePad 100) also included a tutorial application called (link: /terms/tour text: Tour).

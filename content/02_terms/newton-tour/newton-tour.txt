Title: Newton Tour

----

Date: 29 August 2018

----

Text:

The built-in [application](/terms/application) which provides an interactive tutorial for learning the basics of a [MessagePad](/terms/messagepad) device and its various features. On an [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) device, a similar tutorial is named [eMate Tour](/terms/emate-tour).

Title: Paperback

----

Date: 02 June 2025

----

Text:

Software developed by (link: /sources/david-fedor text: David Fedor) that converts lightly formatted (link: /terms/text-file text: text files) into book (link: /terms/package text: packages) that can be viewed on a Newton device.

A book created by the Paperback (link: /terms/application text: application) does not include the robust formatting options or (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript) support that a (link: /terms/newton-book text: Newton book) created using (link: /terms/newton-book-maker text: Newton Book Maker) or (link: /terms/Newton Press text: Newton Press) provides.

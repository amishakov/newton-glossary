Title: MMU Tables

----

Date:

----

Text:

A structured set of data used by the [memory management unit](/terms/memory-management-unit) (MMU) which instructs it on how to handle the translation of [virtual addresses](/terms/virtual-address) to [physical addresses](/terms/physical-address).

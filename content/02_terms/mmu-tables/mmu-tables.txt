Title: MMU Tables

----

Date: 31 May 2001

----

Text:

A structured set of data used by the (link: /terms/memory-management-unit text: memory management unit) (MMU) which instructs it on how to handle the translation of (link: /terms/virtual-address text: virtual addresses) to (link: /terms/physical-address text: physical addresses).

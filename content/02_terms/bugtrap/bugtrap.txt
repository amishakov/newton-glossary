Title: BugTrap

----

Date: 09 February 2017

----

Text:

A user-friendly [debugging](/terms/debugging) utility for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) developed by [Tactile Systems](/sources/tactile). BugTrap provides a simple way to determine which [application](/terms/application) on a Newton device is the source of a crash. The utility will generate a log of information which is automatically saved as a [notes](/terms/note) in the [Notepad](/terms/notepad) application.

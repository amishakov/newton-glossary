Title: BugTrap

----

Date: 09 February 2017

----

Text:

A user-friendly (link: /terms/debugging text: debugging) utility for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) developed by (link: /sources/tactile text: Tactile Systems). BugTrap provides a simple way to determine which (link: /terms/application text: application) on a Newton device is the source of a crash. The utility will generate a log of information which is automatically saved as a (link: /terms/note text: notes) in the (link: /terms/notepad text: Notepad) application.

Title: Newtophyte

----

Date: 21 November 2004

----

Text:

Originally [coined by Ben Truesdate](https://newtontalk.net/archive/newtontalk/2001-November/0154.html) on the (link: /terms/newtontalk text: NewtonTalk) mailing list, a “newtophyte” is a recent convert to the Newton platform. Newtophyte is a play on “neophyte”.

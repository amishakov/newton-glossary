Title: Andante

----

Date: 21 November 2004

----

Text:

The code name of the Newton device that was to be next in line after the (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100). According to (link: /sources/walter-smith text: Walter Smith), the development priorities of this device were “placed on size and price”. Andante never shipped due to Newton development being (link: /terms/steved text: Steved).

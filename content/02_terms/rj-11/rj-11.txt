Title: RJ-11

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A standard modular telephone connector, used mainly in North America. Similar to the (link: /terms/rj-45 text: RJ-45) modular connector used for (link: /terms/ethernet text: ethernet) connections.

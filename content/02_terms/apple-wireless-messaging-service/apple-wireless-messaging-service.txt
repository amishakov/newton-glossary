Title: Apple Wireless Messaging Service

----

Date:

----

Text:

A defunct subscription-based electronic messaging service that allowed a Newton device equipped with a [Newton Messaging Card](/terms/newton-messaging-card) to send and receive text messages, as well as send and receive voice messages via telephone. A seven-digit personal identification number or [PIN](/terms/pin) was used to identify a user on the service. A separate four digit [PassCode](/terms/passcode) was used to securely access text messages, voicemail messages, and change various account settings.

Title: Now Up To Date

----

Date: 06 October 2002

Text:

A desktop calendaring and scheduling [application](/terms/application) developed by [Now Software](https://sites.google.com/site/nowsoftwarecom/), some versions of which can [synchronize](/terms/synchronize) information with a Newton device.

Also referred to as [NUD](/terms/nud).

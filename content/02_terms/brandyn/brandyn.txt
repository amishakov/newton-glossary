Text:

An (link: /terms/easter-egg text: easter egg) hidden in (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) as a nod to (link: https://www.sifter.org/~brandyn/ text: Brandyn Webb), an engineer who worked on (link: /terms/rosetta text: Rosetta), part of the (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) system.

Writing “Brandyn” three times in succession will display a special bit of text.

Other phrases that work with this easter egg are (link: /terms/larryy text: larryy), (link: /terms/lesv text: lesv), (link: /terms/lyon text: lyon), and (link: /terms/stafford text: stafford).

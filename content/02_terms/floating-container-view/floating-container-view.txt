Title: Floating Container View

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), a (link: /terms/container-view text: container view) that initially appears in front of all open (link: /terms/sibling-view text: sibling views).

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/floater text: Floater) or (link: /terms/floating-window text: Floating Window).

Title: Trash Can

----

Date: 21 June 2025

----

Text:

A component of the animation that occurs when an item is (link: /terms/delete text: deleted) in an (link: /terms/application text: application) such as the (link: /terms/Notepad text: Notepad).

The animation simulates an item being wadded into a ball before be tossed into a trash can.

When the animated item hits the trash can, a “(link: /terms/plunk text: plunk)” is played.

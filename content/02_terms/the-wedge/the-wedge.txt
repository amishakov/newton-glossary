----

Date: 13 July 2025

Text:

A simple stand manufactured by (link: /sources/roadrunner-tracks text: RoadRunner Tracks) to hold a (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad) device. The stand was made from transparent thermoplastic and designed to hold the MessagePad at a slight angle when sitting on a desk or other work surface.

Title: RMP

----

Date: 19 July 2024

----

Text:

Round Metal Piece

According to [Frank Gründel](/sources/frank-grundel), a component found in a [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000) or [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100) MP2x00 battery pack when being refurbished or [recelled](/terms/recelled).

As known as the [krM](/terms/krm) or “kleines rundes Metallstück” in German.

Title: Callback Spec

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), a (link: /terms/frame text: frame) passed as an argument to an (link: /terms/endpoint text: endpoint) method. The callback spec frame contains (link: /terms/slot text: slots) that control how the endpoint (link: /terms/method text: method) executes, along with a completion method that is called when the endpoint operation completes.

Title: ASIC

Date: 24 May 2001

Text:

Application Specific Integrated Circuit

A custom (link: /terms/vlsi text: VLSI) computer chip or (link: /terms/chipset text: chipset) designed to perform one or more particular functions. Advantages include fewer discrete components, lower power consumption, and increased reliability.

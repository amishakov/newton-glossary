Title: Storage Card

----

Date:

----

Text:

Any type of removable [PC card](/terms/pc-card) used for storing data. A storage card can contain [applications](/terms/application), [packages](/terms/package), [soups](/terms/soup), or [backup](/terms/backup) data.

Also referred to as a [memory card](/terms/memory-card).

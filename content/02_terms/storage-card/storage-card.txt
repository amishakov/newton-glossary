Title: Storage Card

----

Date: 12 June 2001

----

Text:

Any type of removable (link: /terms/pc-card text: PC card) used for storing data. A storage card can contain (link: /terms/application text: applications), (link: /terms/package text: packages), (link: /terms/soup text: soups), or (link: /terms/backup text: backup) data.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/memory-card text: Memory Card).

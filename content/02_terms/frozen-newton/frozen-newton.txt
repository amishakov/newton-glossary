Title: Frozen Newton

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A Japanese project organized by Masaru Hozumi and Hiroaki Dehiro to create a custom, clear case (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100). A variation of the case featuring blue accents was known as the (link: /terms/blueberry-frozen text: Blueberry Frozen).

Also referred to as (link: /terms/frozen-ghost text: Frozen Ghost).

Title: Frozen Newton

----

Date:

----

Text:

A Japanese project organized by Masaru Hozumi and Hiroaki Dehiro to create a custom, clear case [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100). A variation of the case featuring blue accents was known as the [Blueberry Frozen](/terms/blueberry-frozen).

Also referred to as [Frozen Ghost](/terms/frozen-ghost).

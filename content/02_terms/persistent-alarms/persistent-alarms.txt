Title: Persistent Alarms

----

Date: 23 January 2025

----

Text:

A setting in the (link: /terms/alarms text: Alarms) section of the (link: /terms/prefs text: Prefs) application.

When enabled, a (link: /terms/notification-slip text: notification slip) appears and a series of beeps is played when an alarm goes off, continuing until the alarm is acknowledged or (link: /terms/snooze text: snoozed).

Title: NewtSplit

----

Date: 16 May 2026

----

Text:

A hardware accessory designed by (link: /sources/adriano-angelillis text: Adriano Angelillis) that provided both a (link: /terms/db-9 text: DB-9) serial adapter for connecting to a Windows computer and a powered (link: /terms/usb text: USB) port using the serial port on a Newton device.

The USB port was embedded in a magnetic base, similar to certain models of the (link: /terms/newtlight text: NewtLight) accessory.

An optional “High Speed Serial Converter” that utilized an (link: /terms/ftdi text: FTDI) chip to provide faster data transfer over a USB connection between a Newton device and a Mac OS or Windows computer.

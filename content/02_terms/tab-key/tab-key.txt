Title: Tab Key

----

Date: 24 August 2025

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/character-key text: character key) located on a keyboard that moves the (link: /terms/caret text: caret) or text (link: /terms/insertion-point text: insertion point).

In a (link: /terms/field text: field) that allows entering multiple lines of text, the Tab key moves the caret to the next (link: /terms/tab-stop text: tab stop). In a field that allows entering one line of text, the Tab key moves the caret to the next field or (link: /terms/input-line text: input line) in the same (link: /terms/container-view text: container view).

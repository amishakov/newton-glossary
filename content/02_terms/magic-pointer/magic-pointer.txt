Title: Magic Pointer

----

Date: 25 June 2001

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), a (link: /terms/constant text: constant) that represents a special type of (link: /terms/reference text: reference) to an (link: /terms/object text: object) in the Newton (link: /terms/rom text: ROM). Magic pointer references are resolved at run time by the (link: /terms/operating-system text: operating system), which substitutes the actual (link: /terms/address text: address) of the object in ROM for the magic pointer reference.

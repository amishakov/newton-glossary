Title: Options

----

Date: 30 January 2025

----

Text:

A utility created by (link: /sources/softwareburo-muller text: Softwarebüro Müller) that allows the user to access and control several undocumented features found in (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) 2.0 and later.

Settings include activating the ability to (link: /terms/freeze text: freeze) packages, disable the (link: /terms/extras-drawer text: Extras Drawer) from automatically closing after opening an (link: /terms/application text: application), and setting the number of (link: /terms/clipboard text: clipboards).

(image: /assets/images/options-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of Options width: 240)

The German localized version of Options is named (link: /terms/optionen text: Optionen).

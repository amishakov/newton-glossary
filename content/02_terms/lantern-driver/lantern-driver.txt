Title: Lantern Driver

----

Date:

----

Text:

A [data link layer](/terms/data-link-layer) driver that allows Newton [applications](/terms/application) to use specific hardware (typically an [ethernet](/terms/ethernet) card) in conjunction with the [Lantern Library](/terms/lantern-library) incorporated into the [Newton Devices](newton-devices) package.

Title: Lantern Driver

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/data-link-layer text: data link layer) driver that allows Newton (link: /terms/application text: applications) to use specific hardware (typically an (link: /terms/ethernet text: ethernet) card) in conjunction with the (link: /terms/lantern-library text: Lantern Library) incorporated into the [Newton Devices](newton-devices) package.

Title: EZMimic

----

Date: 08 March 2017

----

Text:

The term used by [EZShow Systems](/sources/ezshow-systems) to describe the screen mirroring functionality of their [EZ2TV](/terms/ez2tv) and [EZ2VGA](/terms/ez2vga) cards.

Personal Communication Services

Telecommunication services provided over a wireless connection that combines voice messaging, text messaging, voicemail, and other features for use on a portable device, such as a (link: /terms/personal-digital-assistant text: personal digital assistant). These services are usually delivered over a cellular or radio network.

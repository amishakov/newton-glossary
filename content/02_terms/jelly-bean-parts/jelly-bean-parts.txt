Title: Jelly Bean Parts

----

Date: 07 February 2017

----

Text:

An assortment of individual electronic components which can be replaced by a single multifunction chip or (link: /terms/chipset text: chipset). Mike Culbert, a member of the (link: /terms/newton-systems-group text: Newton Systems Group), used the term to explain why [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) chose to use the (link: /terms/voyager text: Voyager) chipset in later Newton devices.

Infrared Data Association

An industry body developing standards for [infrared](/terms/infrared) data transfer.

IrDA is also the infrared communications [protocol](/terms/protocol) used in later Newton devices, including the [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000), [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100), and [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300).

The IrDA standard consists of a hardware [serial infrared](/terms/serial-infrared) (SIR) interface specification, a link access protocol specification ([IrLAP](/terms/irlap)), a link management protocol specification ([IrLMP](/terms/irlmp)) and a transport protocol specification ([TinyTP](/terms/tinytp)).

Title: Chipset

----

Date: 07 February 2017

----

Text:

A collection of integrated circuits designed to be used together to provide specific functions. For example, early (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad) devices utilize the (link: /terms/runt text: Runt) chipset for memory management and hardware interface control.

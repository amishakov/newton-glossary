Title: Casual

----

Date: 10 February 2017

----

Text:

An upright, script-like typeface designed to look as if it was written by hand. Casual is one of the default bitmap (link: /terms/font text: fonts) included in (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) and is used when the (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) engine converts printed text into (link: /terms/recognized-text text: recognized text).

Title: Handwriting Recognition

----

Date:

----

Text:

The algorithmic conversion of handwritten or printed text into digital text.

Also referred to as [text recognition](/terms/text-recognition) and [HWR](/terms/hwr).

Handwriting Recognition is also a section found in the [Prefs](/terms/prefs) application, where the user can select their writing style, configure settings for a [guest user](/terms/guest-user), [realign](/terms/realign) the [pen](/terms/pen) with the [screen](/terms/screen), and set other related options.

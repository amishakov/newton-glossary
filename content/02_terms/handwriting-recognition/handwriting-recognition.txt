Title: Handwriting Recognition

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The algorithmic conversion of handwritten or printed text into digital text.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/text-recognition text: Text Recognition) and (link: /terms/hwr text: HWR).

Handwriting Recognition is also a section found in the (link: /terms/prefs text: Prefs) application.

In the Handwriting Recognition (link: /terms/slip text: slip), the user can select their writing style, configure settings for a (link: /terms/guest-user text: guest user), (link: /terms/realign text: realign) the (link: /terms/pen text: pen) with the (link: /terms/screen text: screen), and set other related options.

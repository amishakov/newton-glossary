Title: Button

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A small (link: /terms/graphical-user-interface text: graphical user interface) object that performs an action when (link: /terms/tapped text: tapped). Also, a physical button that performs a hardware action on the device. For example, pressing the the (link: /terms/reset-button text: reset button) on a Newton device performs a (link: /terms/soft-reset text: soft reset).

Title: Endpoint

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A type of communications connection such as a serial connection, (link: /terms/modem text: modem), (link: /terms/infrared text: infrared) beam, or (link: /terms/appletalk text: AppleTalk) network.

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), an endpoint is also (link: /terms/object text: object) created from `protoBasicEndpoint`, or one of its derivative (link: /terms/proto text: protos), that controls a real-time communication session. This object encapsulates and maintains the details of the specific connection, and allows you to control the underlying communication tool.

Title: Non-display Area

----

Date: 18 December 2001

----

Text:

The portion of a Newton device (link: /terms/screen text: screen) between the area where information can be displayed (the (link: /terms/display-area text: display area)) and the edge of the case or (link: /terms/bezel text: bezel) surrounding the screen.

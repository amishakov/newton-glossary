Title: Kbase

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A nickname for the AppleCare Knowledge Base or (link: /sources/applecare-knowledge-archive text: AppleCare Knowledge Archive), a technical support (link: /terms/database text: database) consisting of thousands of documents related to [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) products and services. Each document in the library could be referenced by a unique identification number. The Knowledge Archive was preceded by the Apple Tech Info Library ((link: /terms/til text: TIL)) and superseded by the current [Apple Support](https://support.apple.com/) website.

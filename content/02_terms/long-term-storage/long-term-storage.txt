Title: Long Term Storage

----

Date: 06 October 2002

----

Text:

An extended period of disuse of a Newton device or other piece of equipment.

Part of the preparation process prior to long term storage is to remove the main (link: /terms/battery text: battery) and the (link: /terms/backup-battery text: backup battery), if applicable. This helps prevent damage to the Newton device due to battery leakage.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/lts text: LTS).

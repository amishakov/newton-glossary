Title: Tag Button

----

Date: 06 October 2002

----

Text:

The small (link: /terms/button text: button) found in an (link: /terms/application text: application) that facilitates the editing or tagging of information related to the currently (link: /terms/select text: selected) item. When (link: /terms/tapped text: tapped), the tag button displays a (link: /terms/picker text: picker) listing available tagging options, otherwise known as a (link: /terms/tag-picker text: tag picker). Depending on the type of item selected, the tag button can be used to reply to a fax, change the recipient of an email, or (link: /terms/put-away text: put away) a (link: /terms/package text: package).

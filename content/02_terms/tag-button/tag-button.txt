Title: Tag Button

----

Date:

----

Text:

The small [button](/terms/button) found in an [application](/terms/application) that facilitates the editing or tagging of information related to the currently [selected](/terms/select) item. When [tapped](/terms/tapped), the tag button displays a [picker](/terms/picker) listing available tagging options, otherwise known as a [tag picker](/terms/tag-picker). Depending on the type of item selected, the tag button can be used to reply to a fax, change the recipient of an email, or [put away](/terms/put-away) a [package](/terms/package).

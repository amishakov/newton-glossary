----

Date: 24 May 2001

Text:

An (link: /terms/application text: application) that is displayed when all other applications are closed and cannot be closed itself. (link: /terms/notepad text: Notepad) is the default backdrop application on a (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad) device. (link: /terms/newton-works text: Newton Works) is the default backdrop application on an (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300). Another application can set as the backdrop by using the (link: /terms/action-picker text: action picker) located in the (link: /terms/extras-drawer text: Extras Drawer).

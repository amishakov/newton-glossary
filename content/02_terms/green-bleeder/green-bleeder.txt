Title: Green Bleeder

----

Date: 17 March 2017

----

Text:

A nickname for a diehard Newton user. Someone who is prone to [bleeding green](/terms/bleeding-green) displays their loyalty to the Newton platform more intensely than a [Newtie](/terms/newtie).

Not to be confused with someone of [Vulcan](http://memory-alpha.wikia.com/wiki/Vulcan) extraction.

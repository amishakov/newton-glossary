Title: Method

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), a programmed (link: /terms/function text: function). Each method contained in a (link: /terms/template text: template) processes a particular (link: /terms/message text: message) for the (link: /terms/view text: view) that the template defines. When a view (link: /terms/receiver text: receives) a message for which it has a method, the Newton executes that method. Also referred to as a function in a (link: /terms/frame text: frame) slot that is invoked in response to a message.

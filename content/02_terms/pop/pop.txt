Post Office Protocol

A standard protocol used to retrieve email messages from a mail server over a (link: /terms/tcp-ip text: TCP/IP) connection.

(link: /terms/simplemail text: SimpleMail) is a (link: /terms/application text: application) that uses POP to access email on a Newton device.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/pop3 text: POP3), the third and most current version of the standard.

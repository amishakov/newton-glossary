Title: Newton InterConnect

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The unique 26-pin, subminiature connector manufactured by (link: /terms/jae text: JAE) used on (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000), (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100), and (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) devices. The port provides access to serial and (link: /terms/appletalk text: AppleTalk) communication, audio (link: /terms/input text: input) and (link: /terms/output text: output), power, as well as automatic sensing of when a device is docked.

Also referred to as the (link: /terms/nic text: NIC).

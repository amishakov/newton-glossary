Title: Highlighting Mark

----

Date: 09 December 2024

----

Text:

A heavy line drawn through or around an (link: /terms/object text: object) in order to (link: /terms/highlight text: highlight) something or creation a (link: /terms/selection-box text: selection box). A highlighting mark can be drawn after holding the (link: /terms/pen text: pen) down in one place on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) creating a large dot known as a (link: /terms/heavy-mark text: heavy mark).

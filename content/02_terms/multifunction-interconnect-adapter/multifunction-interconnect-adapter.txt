Title: Multifunction InterConnect Adapter

----

Date: 04 November 2017

----

Text:

A prototype (link: /terms/dongle text: dongle) developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) that plugged into an (link: /terms/newton-interconnect text: Newton InterConnect) port. The adapter provided standard connections for serial and (link: /terms/appletalk text: AppleTalk) communication, audio (link: /terms/input text: input) and (link: /terms/output text: output), as well as power.

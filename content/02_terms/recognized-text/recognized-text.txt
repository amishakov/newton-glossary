Title: Recognized Text

----

Date: 24 May 2001

Text:

(link: /terms/ink-word text: Ink words) processed by the (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) system. (link: /terms/ink text: Ink) drawn by the user is converted into recognized text when the user has selected (link: /terms/text text: Text) in the “Recognition Preferences” (link: /terms/slip text: slip) or after (link: /terms/deferred-recognition text: deferred recognition) takes place.

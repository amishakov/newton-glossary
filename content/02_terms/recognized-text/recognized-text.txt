Title: Recognized Text

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

[Ink words](/terms/ink-word) processed by the [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) system. [Ink](/terms/ink) drawn by the user is converted into recognized text when the user has selected [Text](/terms/text) in the “Recognition Preferences” [slip](/terms/slip) or after [deferred recognition](/terms/deferred-recognition) takes place.

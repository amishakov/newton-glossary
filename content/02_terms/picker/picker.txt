Title: Picker

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A menu or (link: /terms/list text: list) of choices that appears when the user (link: /terms/tap text: taps) an adjacent text label marked by a solid (link: /terms/diamond text: diamond). A picker can also appear when a user taps a (link: /terms/button text: button) such as a (link: /terms/routing-button text: routing button). The user (link: /terms/select text: selects) one of the items in the list by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) it.

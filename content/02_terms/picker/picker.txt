Title: Picker

----

Date:

----

Text:

A menu or [list](/terms/list) of choices that appears when the user [taps](/terms/tap) an adjacent text label marked by a solid [diamond](/terms/diamond). A picker may also appear when a user taps a [button](/terms/button) such as a [routing button](/terms/routing-button). The user [selects](/terms/select) one of the items in the list by [tapping](/terms/tapping) it.

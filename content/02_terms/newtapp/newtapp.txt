Title: NewtApp

----

Date:

----

Text:

A programming framework designed to help build complete, full-featured Newton [applications](/terms/application) more quickly, compared to starting development from scratch. NewtApp was released in support of the [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) 2.0 update.

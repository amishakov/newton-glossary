Title: NewtApp

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A programming framework designed to help build complete, full-featured Newton (link: /terms/application text: applications) more quickly, compared to starting development from scratch. NewtApp was released in support of the (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) 2.0 update.

Title: Application Card

----

Date: 12 June 2001

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/pcmcia text: PCMCIA) card that contains an (link: /terms/application text: application) geared towards a specific use. Application cards are usually ROM-based, and therefore the user cannot modify or delete the data contained on them.

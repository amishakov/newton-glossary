Title: Charged

----

Date: 21 February 2025

----

Text:

The current state of a (link: /terms/battery text: battery) that has reached its electrical energy storage capacity.

On an (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) device, the (link: /terms/charging-status-light text: charging light) on the top of the case is green when the battery is fully charged.

Being charged is the opposite of (link: /terms/charged text: discharged).

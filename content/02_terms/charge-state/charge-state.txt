Title: Charge State

----

Date: 01 December 2016

----

Text:

The current status of a rechargeable (link: /terms/battery text: battery) in a Newton device. States can include Charging, Discharging, Trickle Charging, and (link: /terms/deep-toast text: Deep Toast). The charge state of a battery can be observed using an (link: /terms/application text: application) such as (link: /terms/newttest text: NewtTest) or (link: /terms/sbm-utilities text: SBM Utilities).

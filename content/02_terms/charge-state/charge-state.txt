Title: Charge State

----

Date: 01 December 2016

----

Text:

The current status of a rechargeable (link: /terms/battery text: battery) in a Newton device. States can include (link: /terms/charging text: Charging), (link: /terms/discharging text: Discharging), (link: /terms/trickle-charging text: Trickle Charging), (link: /terms/fast-charging text: Fast Charging), (link: /terms/preliminary-charging text: Preliminary Charging), and (link: /terms/deep-toast text: Deep Toast).

Detailed information regarding the charge state of a battery can be observed using an (link: /terms/application text: application) such as (link: /terms/newttest text: NewtTest) or (link: /terms/sbm-utilities text: SBM Utilities).

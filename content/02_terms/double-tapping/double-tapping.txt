Title: Double-Tapping

----

Date: 17 December 2023

----

Text:

The action of briefly touching or [tapping](/terms/tapping) the same spot on the [screen](/terms/screen), or nearly the same spot, twice in rapid succession with the [pen](/terms/pen). Essentially the same action as double clicking a mouse button on a [desktop computer](/terms/desktop-computer), [laptop](/terms/laptop-computer), or equivalent device.

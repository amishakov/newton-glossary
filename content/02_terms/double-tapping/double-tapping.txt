Title: Double-Tapping

----

Date: 18 December 2023

----

Text:

The action of briefly touching or (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the same spot on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen), or nearly the same spot, twice in rapid succession with the (link: /terms/pen text: pen). Essentially the same action as double clicking a mouse button on a (link: /terms/desktop-computer text: desktop computer), (link: /terms/laptop-computer text: laptop), or equivalent device.

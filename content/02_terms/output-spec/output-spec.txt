Title: Output Spec

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), a special type of (link: /terms/callback-spec text: callback spec) used with an (link: /terms/endpoint text: endpoint) method. An output spec contains additional (link: /terms/slot text: slots) that allow you to pass special (link: /terms/protocol text: protocol) flags and define how the data being sent is translated.

Text:

Advanced Radio Data Information Service

A defunct wireless data network based in the United States developed by [Motorola](http://www.motorola.com/) and [IBM](http://www.ibm.com/). The ARDIS network provided two-way data transmission based on radio packet technology. ARDIS compatible wireless modems were used in the [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) based [Motorola Marco](/terms/marco) device.

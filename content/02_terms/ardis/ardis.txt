Title: ARDIS

----

Date: 31 May 2001

----

Text:

Advanced Radio Data Information Service

A defunct wireless data network based in the United States developed by [Motorola](https://www.motorola.com/) and [IBM](https://www.ibm.com/). The ARDIS network provided two-way data transmission based on radio packet technology. ARDIS compatible wireless (link: /terms/modem text: modems) were used in the (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) based (link: /terms/marco text: Motorola Marco) device.

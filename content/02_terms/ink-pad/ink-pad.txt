Title: Ink Pad

----

Date: 15 January 2023

----

Text:

An area of the (link: /terms/ink-window text: Ink Window) where written input is converted into editable text using the (link: /terms/ink text: Ink) handwriting recognition system on certain versions of Mac OS X. The Ink Pad can also be used to insert drawn sketches into documents.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/inkpad text: InkPad).

Title: Ink Pad

----

Date: 15 January 2023

----

Text:

An area of the [Ink Window](/terms/ink-window) where written input is converted into editable text using the [Ink](/terms/ink) handwriting recognition system on certain versions of Mca OS X. The Ink Window can also be used to insert drawn sketches into documents.

Also referred to as [InkPad](/terms/inkpad).

_This entry requires a screenshot._

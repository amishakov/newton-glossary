Title: Revelar Connection Utility

----

Date:

----

Text:

Software developed by [Revelar](/sources/revelar) that allows the user to export, modify, and share data from their Newton device with Mac OS computers. Works with data used by the [Notepad](/terms/notepad), [Name File](/terms/name-file), [Date Book](/terms/date-book), and [Calls](/terms/calls) applications. Commonly referred to as [RCU](/terms/rcu).

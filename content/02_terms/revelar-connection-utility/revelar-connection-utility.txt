Title: Revelar Connection Utility

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

Software developed by (link: /sources/revelar text: Revelar) that allows the user to export, modify, and share data from their Newton device with Mac OS computers. Works with data used by the (link: /terms/notepad text: Notepad), (link: /terms/name-file text: Name File), (link: /terms/date-book text: Date Book), and (link: /terms/calls text: Calls) applications.

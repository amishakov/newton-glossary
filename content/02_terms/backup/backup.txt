Title: Backup

----

Date: 29 March 2017

----

Text:

A copy of all the information normally stored on a Newton device. Backup information can include [applications](/terms/application), [packages](/terms/package), [soup](/terms/soup) data, and user settings. A [backup file](/terms/backup-file) can reside on a [storage card](/terms/storage-card), a [desktop computer](/terms/desktop-computer), or [laptop computer](/terms/laptop-computer) and can be used to [restore](/terms/restore) the original information back to the Newton device.

The [Card](/terms/card) application on a Newton device is used to create a backup on a storage card. An application such as [Newton Backup Utility](/terms/newton-backup-utility) is used to create a backup on another computer.

Backup can also refer to the [backup battery](/terms/backup-battery) found in early devices, such as the [MessagePad 100](/terms/messagepad-100).

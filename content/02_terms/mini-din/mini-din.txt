Title: Mini-DIN

----

Date:

----

Text:

A standard [DIN](/terms/din) type of connector, generally used to connect peripherals such as mice, modems, and keyboards to computing devices. The serial port found [MessagePad 100](/terms/messagepad-100), [MessagePad 110](/terms/messagepad-110), [MessagePad 120](/terms/messagepad-120), [MessagePad 130](/terms/messagepad-130), and [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) devices uses a Mini-DIN or [DIN-8](/terms/din-8) connector, so called because it has eight pins.

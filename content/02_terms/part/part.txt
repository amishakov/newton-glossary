Title: Part

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript), a unit of software consisting of either code or data, that is held in a [part frame](/terms/part-frame). The format of the part is identified by a four character identifier called its type or [part code](/terms/part-code).

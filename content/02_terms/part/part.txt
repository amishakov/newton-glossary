Title: Part

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), a unit of software consisting of either code or data, that is held in a (link: /terms/part-frame text: part frame). The format of the part is identified by a four character identifier called its type or (link: /terms/part-code text: part code).

Title: PT100

----

Date: 14 July 2024

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/vt100 text: VT100) terminal emulator (link: /terms/application text: application) developed by (link: /sources/scrawl-software text: Scrawl Software) for Newton devices. PT100 is capable of terminal sessions over (link: /terms/modem text: modem) and direct serial connections. It can also provide Telnet functionality on Newton devices that support the (link: /terms/newton-internet-enabler text: Newton Internet Enabler).

Title: PT100

----

Date: 14 July 2024

----

Text:

A [VT100](/terms/vt100) terminal emulator [application](/terms/application) developed by [Scrawl Software](/sources/scrawl-software) for Newton devices. PT100 is capable of terminal sessions over [modem](/terms/modem) and direct serial connections. It can also provide Telnet functionality on Newton devices that support the [Newton Internet Enabler](/terms/newton-internet-enabler).

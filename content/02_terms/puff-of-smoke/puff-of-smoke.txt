Title: Puff of Smoke

----

Date: 28 August 2017

----

Text:

The animation which appears after deleting text or ink drawings by [scrubbing](/terms/scrubbing) with the [pen](/terms/pen) on the [screen](/terms/screen) of a Newton device. Naturally, the “puff of smoke” is accompanied by a “[poof](/terms/poof)” sound.

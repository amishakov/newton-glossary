Title: Getting Started Card

----

Date: 08 August 2025

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/pcmcia text: PCMCIA) card that shipped with the (link: /terms/messagepad-100 text: MessagePad 100), containing three (link: /terms/application text: applications) to help the user learn about the device and features of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS).

The (link: /terms/instructor text: Instructor) application provides tips for using the (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition).

The (link: /terms/tour text: Tour) application walks the user through various capabilities of the device.

The (link: /terms/game text: Game) application encourages the user to practice their handwriting through an arcade style game, gathering information while playing to help improve the accuracy of the handwriting recognition.

This particular (link: /terms/card text: card) is required to activate an (link: /terms/easter-egg text: easter egg) known as (link: /terms/happy-hour text: Happy Hour).

Related to this easter egg, while the card is inserted, contact information for (link: /terms/apple-assurance text: Apple Assurance) and Canadian Support and Service are added to the (link: /terms/name-file text: Name File).

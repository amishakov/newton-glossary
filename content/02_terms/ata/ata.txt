Title: ATA

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

Advanced Technology Attachment

An interface standard generally used for data storage devices. For example, (link: /terms/ata-support text: ATA Support) is a (link: /terms/device-driver text: device driver) for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) which facilitates the use of non-linear ATA (link: /terms/storage-card text: storage cards) such as (link: /terms/compactflash text: CompactFlash).

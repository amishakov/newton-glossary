Advanced Technology Attachment

An interface standard generally used for data storage devices. For example, [ATA Support](/terms/ata-support) is a [device driver](/terms/device-driver) for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) which facilitates the use of non-linear ATA [storage cards](/terms/storage-card) such as [CompactFlash](/terms/compactflash).

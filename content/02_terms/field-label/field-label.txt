Title: Field Label

----

Date: 12 June 2001

----

Text:

A static piece of text which describes the type of content contained within an associated data (link: /terms/field text: field). An example of a field label would be the text “Address:” beside an (link: /terms/input-area text: input area) where the user could enter a street number.

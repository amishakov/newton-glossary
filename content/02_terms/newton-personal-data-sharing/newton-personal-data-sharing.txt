Title: Newton Personal Data Sharing

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A suite of software (link: /terms/package text: packages) that will allow certain Newton devices to act as a web server. Originally created by [Matt Vaughn](https://github.com/mwvaughn) and subsequently developed by (link: /sources/paul-guyot text: Paul Guyot), [Adam Tow](https://tow.com/), and (link: /sources/victor-rehorst text: Victor Rehorst).

Also referred to as (link: /terms/npds text: NPDS).

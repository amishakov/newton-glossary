Title: Newton Personal Data Sharing

----

Date:

----

Text:

A suite of software [packages](/terms/package) that will allow certain Newton devices to act as a web server. Originally created by [Matt Vaughn](https://github.com/mwvaughn) and subsequently developed by [Paul Guyot](/sources/paul-guyot), [Adam Tow](https://tow.com/), and [Victor Rehorst](/sources/victor-rehorst).

Also referred to as [NPDS](/terms/npds).

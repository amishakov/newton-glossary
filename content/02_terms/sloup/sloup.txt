----

Date: 19 December 2016

----

Text:

A utility (link: /terms/application text: application) created by (link: /sources/steve-weyer text: Steve Weyer) to facilitate the transfer of data between files located on a (link: /terms/desktop-computer text: desktop) or (link: /terms/laptop-computer text: laptop) computer and (link: /terms/soup text: soups) residing on a Newton device. Sloup can also be used to inspect and report on the values contained in various data (link: /terms/field text: fields).

Sloup was previously known as (link: /terms/slurpee text: Slurpee).

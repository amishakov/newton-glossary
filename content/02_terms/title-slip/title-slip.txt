Text:

An (link: /terms/item-info-slip text: item info slip) pertaining to a (link: /terms/newton-works text: Newton Works) document, such as (link: /terms/paper text: paper), (link: /terms/drawing text: drawing), or (link: /terms/spreadsheet text: spreadsheet).

The (link: /terms/slip text: slip) displays statistics and related information for a document, including the title, type, creation date, last modification date, size, (link: /terms/store text: store) location, and the number of pages. A user can (link: /terms/edit text: edit) a documentâ€™s title in the (link: /terms/slip text: slip).

The title slip appears when creating a new document or can be accessed by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the (link: /terms/picture-button text: picture button) next to the document title.

Title: Newton Book Maker

----

Date:

----

Text:

Software developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) for creating sophisticated electronic documents in [Newton book](/terms/newton-book) format and online help for Newton [applications](/terms/application). Newton Book Maker uses text-based [commands](/terms/command) contained in [book source files](/terms/book-source-file) to produce [book definition files](/terms/book-definition-file) for use with [Newton Toolkit](/terms/newton-toolkit).

[Book maker commands](/terms/book-maker-commands) allow the developer to provide additional functionality or control over the page layout of a book. Newton Book Maker also provides a means to attach other data, [methods](/terms/method), and view [templates](/terms/template) to book content.

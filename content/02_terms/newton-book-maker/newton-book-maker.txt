Title: Newton Book Maker

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

Software developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) for creating sophisticated electronic documents in (link: /terms/newton-book text: Newton book) format and online help for Newton (link: /terms/application text: applications). Newton Book Maker uses text-based (link: /terms/command text: commands) contained in (link: /terms/book-source-file text: book source files) to produce (link: /terms/book-definition-file text: book definition files) for use with (link: /terms/newton-toolkit text: Newton Toolkit).

(link: /terms/book-maker-commands text: Book maker commands) allow the developer to provide additional functionality or control over the page layout of a book. Newton Book Maker also provides a means to attach other data, (link: /terms/method text: methods), and view (link: /terms/template text: templates) to book content.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/nbm text: NBM).

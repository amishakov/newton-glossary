Title: Touch Screen

----

Date:

----

Text:

The pressure sensitive component of the [screen](/terms/screen) in a Newton device which responds to contact made using a [pen](/terms/pen). The touch screen on early [MessagePad](/terms/messagepad) devices was referred to as a [touchpad](/terms/touchpad).

Title: Touch Screen

----

Date: 19 December 2016

----

Text:

The pressure sensitive component of the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) in a Newton device which responds to contact made using a (link: /terms/pen text: pen). The touch screen on early (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad) devices was referred to as a (link: /terms/touchpad text: touchpad).

Title: Scrolling List

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A boxed list of text items. A user (link: /terms/scroll text: scrolls) the list to reveal additional items by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the (link: /terms/universal-scroll-arrows text: universal scroll arrows) or optional (link: /terms/local-scroll-arrows text: local scroll arrows). The user can (link: /terms/select text: select) one or more items in the list by tapping them.

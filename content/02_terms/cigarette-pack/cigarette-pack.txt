Title: Cigarette Pack

----

Date: 12 June 2001

----

Text:

A nickname for the (link: /terms/newton-fax-modem text: Newton Fax Modem). So called because its dimensions were similar to that of a package of cigarettes.

Also referred to as the (link: /terms/cigarette-case text: Cigarette Case) or (link: /terms/cigarette-box text: Cigarette Box) modem.

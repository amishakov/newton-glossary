Title: Universal Scroll Arrows

----

Date: 30 October 2001

----

Text:

A pair of icon-based (link: /terms/button text: buttons) used to (link: /terms/scroll text: scroll) an area of content displayed on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen). Universal scroll arrows are located above and below the (link: /terms/overview-button text: overview button) in the standard (link: /terms/button-bar text: button bar) found on all Newton devices.

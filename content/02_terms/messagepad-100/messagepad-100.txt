Title: MessagePad 100

----

Date: 01 December 2016

----

Text:

The second Newton device to be released by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/). The (link: /terms/messagepad-100 text: MessagePad 100) shared the same model number ((link: /terms/h1000 text: H1000)) and form factor as the (link: /terms/original-messagepad text: Original MessagePad). but was less expensive and shipped with a newer version of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) in (link: /terms/rom text: ROM).

The code names for the MessagePad 100 were (link: /terms/wedge text: Wedge) and (link: /terms/junior text: Junior).

The MessagePad 100 is commonly referred to as an (link: /terms/mp100 text: MP100).

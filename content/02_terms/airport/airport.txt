Title: AirPort

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A brand name used by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) on their version of the [802.11](/terms/802-11) wireless communication card. In Japan, AirPort products are known as (link: /terms/airmac text: AirMac).

Title: Lava

----

Date: 28 August 2025

----

Text:

Software developed by (link: /sources/hayes-haugen text: Hayes Haugen) that provides flexible options for controlling the activation of the (link: /terms/backlight text: backlight) and when a Newton device goes to (link: /terms/sleep text: sleep). Lava also features the ability to display animated designs on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) during periods of inactivity, acting similar to a screen saver.

Title: Snooze

----

Date: 23 January 2025

----

Text:

Temporarily dismissing a (link: /terms/notification-alert text: notification alert) or (link: /terms/alarm text: alarm) by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the (link: /terms/snooze-button text: snooze button).

Selecting a time from the list displayed in the (link: /terms/snooze-picker text: snooze picker) will dismiss the alert for that amount of time.

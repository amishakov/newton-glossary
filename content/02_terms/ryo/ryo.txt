Title: RYO

----

Date:

----

Text:

A utility developed by Stephen Millman for adding [icons](/terms/icon) to the [button bar](/terms/button-bar) of the background [application](/terms/application), facilitating the launching of commonly used applications. RYO also allows the creation of invisible [buttons](/terms/button) that overlay the bottom halves of the silkscreened [hard buttons](/terms/hard-button) found on [MP1x0](/terms/mp1x0) series Newton devices.

RYO is an acronym for “roll your own”.

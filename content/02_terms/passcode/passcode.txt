Title: PassCode

----

Date: 01 December 2016

----

Text:

A four-digit security code used to access text messages, voicemail messages, and change various account settings associated with an (link: /terms/apple-wireless-messaging-service text: Apple Wireless Messaging Service) account. Not to be confused with the seven-digit personal identification number or (link: /terms/pin text: PIN) used to identify a user on the service.

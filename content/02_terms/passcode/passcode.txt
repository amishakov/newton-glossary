Title: PassCode

----

Date:

----

Text:

A four-digit security code used to access text messages, voicemail messages, and change various account settings associated with an [Apple Wireless Messaging Service](/terms/apple-wireless-messaging-service) account. Not to be confused with the seven-digit personal identification number or [PIN](/terms/pin) used to identify a user on the service.

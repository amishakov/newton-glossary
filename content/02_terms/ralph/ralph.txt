Title: Ralph

----

Date: 12 June 2001

----

Text:

An early version of (link: /terms/dylan text: Dylan), a dynamic programming language developed at [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) used to develop portions of the original (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) and its (link: /terms/user-interface text: user interface). Ralph was also used on (link: /terms/bauhaus text: Bauhaus), an early (link: /terms/lisp text: Lisp)-based version of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS).

Named for [Ralph Ellison](https://www.biography.com/authors-writers/ralph-ellison), the author of _The Invisible Man_.

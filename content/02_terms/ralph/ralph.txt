Title: Ralph

----

Date:

----

Text:

An early version of [Dylan](/terms/dylan), a dynamic programming language developed at [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) used to develop portions of the original [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) and its [user interface](/terms/user-interface). Ralph was also used on [Bauhaus](/terms/bauhaus), an early [Lisp](/terms/lisp)-based version of [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os). Named for [Ralph Ellison](http://www.biography.com/people/ralph-ellison-9286702), the author of _The Invisible Man_.

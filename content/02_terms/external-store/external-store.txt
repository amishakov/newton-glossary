Title: External Store

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A memory (link: /terms/pc-card text: card) that stores installed (link: /terms/package text: packages) and permanent user data. Called an external (link: /terms/store text: store) because it is a physical accessory that must be plugged into the (link: /terms/pcmcia text: PCMCIA) slot of a Newton device, as opposed to an (link: /terms/internal-store text: internal store).

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/card-store text: Card Store).

Title: Year 2010 Problem

----

Date: 02 September 2017

----

Text:

A bug that affects devices running (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) 2.1 after January 5th, 2010. The problem manifests itself in certain (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript) time (link: /terms/function text: functions) as an integer value overflow, causing an “infinite alarm loop” or the system clock to reset itself.

Commonly referred to as the (link: /terms/y2010 text: Y2010) bug, this problem can be fixed by installing a (link: /terms/system-patch text: system patch).

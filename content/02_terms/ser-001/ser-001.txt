Title: SER-001

----

Date: 17 January 2017

----

Text:

An internal circuit board for (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000) and (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100) devices developed by (link: /sources/david-humphreys text: David Humphreys). When installed, it provides a standard (link: /terms/mini-din text: Mini-DIN) port, eliminating the need for a (link: /terms/newton-serial-adapter text: Newton Serial Adapter) plugged into the proprietary (link: /terms/newton-interconnect text: Newton InterConnect).

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/dongle-destroyer text: Dongle Destroyer).

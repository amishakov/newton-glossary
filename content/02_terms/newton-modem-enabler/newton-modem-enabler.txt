Title: Newton Modem Enabler

----

Date: 19 July 2025

----

Text:

Software that allows the use of third-party modems with a Newton device. Early versions of the Modem Enabler (link: /terms/extension text: extension) needed to be installed along with one or more separate (link: /terms/modem-setup text: modem setup) packages that provided configuration settings specific to each modem. In later versions of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS), individual modems were supported using their own enabler (link: /terms/package text: package).

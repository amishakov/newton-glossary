Title: Flattening

----

Date: 21 November 2017

----

Text:

The process of converting a frame [object](/terms/object) into a stream of bytes before an [entry](/terms/entry) is added to a [soup](/terms/soup). The reverse of this process is [unflattening](/terms/unflattening).

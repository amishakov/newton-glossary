Title: Neat Bong Assist

----

Date: 26 January 2025

----

Text:

A reference to the (link: /terms/neat-bong text: Neat Bong) easter egg hidden in (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) 2.0.

“Neat Bong Assist” is an anagram of “Gaston Bastiaens”, the unpredictable former general manager of Apple’s (link: /terms/pie text: PIE) (Personal Interactive Electronics) division and the (link: /terms/newton-systems-group text: Newton Systems Group).

Title: Rotate Button

----

Date: 03 January 2025

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/button text: button) located in the (link: /terms/status-bar text: status bar) of the (link: /terms/extras-drawer text: Extras Drawer) that displays a (link: /terms/picker text: picker) with options to change the (link: /terms/orientation text: orientation) of the content displayed on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) of a Newton device.

When the screen is set to (link: /terms/portrait-mode text: portrait mode), the picker will also have a (link: /terms/controls-left text: Controls Left) or (link: /terms/controls-left text: Controls Right) option.

In (link: /terms/landscape-mode text: landscape mode), the picker displays a (link: /terms/buttons-left text: Buttons Left) or (link: /terms/buttons-left text: Buttons Right) option.

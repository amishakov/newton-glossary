Title: Nipples

----

Date: 31 May 2001

----

Text:

An affectionate term used by the original Newton developers for the small rubber feet found on the bottom of a Newton device. These small, elusive components are purportedly manufactured using (link: /terms/unobtainium text: unobtainium), due to the difficulty in locating replacements.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/rubber-nubs text: Rubber Nubs).

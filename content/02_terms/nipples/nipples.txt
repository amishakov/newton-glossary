Title: Nipples

----

Date:

----

Text:

An affectionate term used by the original Newton developers for the small rubber feet found on the bottom of a Newton device. Also referred to as [rubber nubs](/terms/rubber-nubs). These small, elusive components are purportedly manufactured using [unobtainium](/terms/unobtainium), due to the difficulty in locating replacements.

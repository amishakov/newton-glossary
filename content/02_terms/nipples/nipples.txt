Title: Nipples

----

Date: 31 May 2001

----

Text:

An affectionate term used by the original Newton team for the small rubber pads found on the bottom of a Newton device. The very first (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad) devices to be manufactured had an issue where these pads would fall off. A group of people reportedly “… spent an entire day gluing 800 ‘nipples’ on the bottom of MessagePads.”

These small, elusive components are purportedly manufactured using (link: /terms/unobtainium text: unobtainium), due to the difficulty in locating replacements.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/rubber-nubs text: Rubber Nubs).

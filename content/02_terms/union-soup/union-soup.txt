Title: Union Soup

----

Date: 25 September 2001

----

Text:

A virtual (link: /terms/soup text: soup) maintained by the (link: /terms/operating-system text: operating system) that exists on multiple (link: /terms/store text: stores), each containing a (link: /terms/member-soup text: member soup). A union soup contains all (link: /terms/entry text: entries) of a specifically named soup, no matter where those entries are stored. New entries to the union soup are added to the member soup residing on the (link: /terms/default-store text: default store).

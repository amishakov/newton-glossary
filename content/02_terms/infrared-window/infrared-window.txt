Title: Infrared Window

----

Date:

----

Text:

The transparent plastic panel covering the [infrared](/terms/infrared) port on a Newton device. Also referred to as a [beam window](/terms/beam-window) in earlier [MessagePad](/terms/messagepad) user manuals.

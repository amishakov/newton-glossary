Title: Key Combination

----

Date: 24 January 2018

----

Text:

A series of keys that are typed at the same time on a keyboard, usually consisting of an alphanumeric (link: /terms/character text: character) combined with one or more (link: /terms/modifier-key text: modifier keys).

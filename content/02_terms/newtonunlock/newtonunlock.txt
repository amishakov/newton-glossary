Title: NewtonUnlock

----

Date: 03 February 2025

----

Text:

A utility created by (link: /sources/jeremy-wyld text: Jeremy Wyld) that can bypass the (link: /terms/pin text: PIN) on a Newton device. The (link: /terms/package text: package) is installed on a (link: /terms/storage-card text: storage card) which is inserted in the Newton device. The package automatically activates and uses brute force number generation to guess the PIN.

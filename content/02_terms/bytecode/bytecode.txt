Title: Bytecode

----

Date: 17 January 2024

----

Text:

An intermediate language that requires a runtime program for execution. For example, [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript) is compiled into bytecode before being interpreted and executed by the [NSM](/terms/nsm) virtual machine.

Title: Bytecode

----

Date: 17 January 2024

----

Text:

The hardware independent instructions that are interpreted when a (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript) function executes.

Also defined as an intermediate language that requires a runtime program for execution. For example, NewtonScript is compiled into bytecode before being interpreted and executed by the (link: /terms/nsm text: NSM) virtual machine.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/byte-code text: Byte Code) in some Newton programming documentation.

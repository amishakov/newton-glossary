Title: Highlight Mode

----

Date: 18 August 2025

----

Text:

A mode of input in (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) that allows the (link: /terms/selection text: selection) of text or objects on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen). A (link: /terms/squeak text: squeak) sound is played to indicate that the Newton device is in highlight mode, rather than (link: /terms/inking-mode text: inking mode), after creating a (link: /terms/highlighting-mark text: highlighting mark) with the (link: /terms/pen text: pen).

Also referred to as (link: /terms/highlighting-mode text: Highlighting Mode).

Title: Setup

----

Date: 25 February 2025

----

Text:

An (link: /terms/application text: application) used to personalize a Newton device.

By stepping through a series of screens, the user can set the (link: /terms/pen-alignment text: pen alignment), country, current date and time, whether their preferred handwriting style is (link: /terms/cursive text: cursive) or (link: /terms/printing text: printing), as well as personal information, including an electronic signature.

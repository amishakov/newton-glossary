Title: Battery Pack

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A removable component of a (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad) or (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) which holds a set of rechargeable batteries. Battery packs are generally sealed when manufactured and are not meant to be repaired. However, it is possible to (link: /terms/recell text: recell) a battery pack by replacing the original (link: /terms/cell text: cells).

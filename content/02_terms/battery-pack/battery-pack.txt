Title: Battery Pack

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A removable component of a [MessagePad](/terms/messagepad) or [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) which holds a set of rechargeable batteries. Battery packs are generally sealed when manufactured and are not meant to be repaired. However, it is possible to [recell](/terms/recell) a battery pack by replacing the original [cells](/terms/cell).

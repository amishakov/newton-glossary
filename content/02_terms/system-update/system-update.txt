Title: System Update

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A small software (link: /terms/patch text: patch) that is installed on a Newton device to modify or update certain aspects of the (link: /terms/rom text: ROM)-based (link: /terms/operating-system text: operating system).

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/system-patch text: System Patch).

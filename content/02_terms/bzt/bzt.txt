Title: BZT

----

Date: 24 December 2024

----

Text:

Bundesamt für Zulassungen in der Telekommunikation

A defunct German federal agency once responsible for the approval of telecommunication devices.

A variation of the [German Coat of Arms](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coat_of_arms_of_Germany), along with the initials “BZT” appeared on the back of (link: /terms/messagepad-110 text: MessagePad 110), (link: /terms/messagepad-120 text: MessagePad 120), and (link: /terms/messagepad-130 text: MessagePad 130) devices.

At the time, any device capable of connecting to the German telecommunication system required approval by the agency. This included telephones, fax machines, and (link: /terms/modem text: modems).

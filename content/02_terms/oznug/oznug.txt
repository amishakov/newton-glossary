Australian Newton Users Group

Founded in August 1994, OzNUG was based in Sydney and supported subchapters of the group throughout Australia. It was affiliated with (link: /terms/nanug text: NANUG), a very active (link: /terms/newton-user-group text: Newton user group) in the United States.

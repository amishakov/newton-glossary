Title: Book Maker Commands

----

Date: 18 December 2001

----

Text:

A set of text-based (link: /terms/newton-book-maker text: Newton Book Maker) instructions used to tag and differentiate specific content items, such as text or graphics, in a Newton (link: /terms/book-source-file text: book source file). For example, specifying the title and layout of a (link: /terms/newton-book text: Newton book) require different commands.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/dot-commands text: Dot Commands) because the commands must begin with a period or dot.

Title: Touch Tone

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

The common term describing the Dual Tone Multi Frequency or [DTMF](/terms/dtmf) method of telephone tone dialing. Basically, the combining of two tones, one low frequency and one high frequency. The Newton can create Touch Tone signals using the onscreen [phone](/terms/phone) keyboard.

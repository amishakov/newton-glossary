Title: Default Action

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The completion action that users are most likely to take among the safe alternatives in the (link: /terms/key-view text: key view). For example, (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the “Ok” (link: /terms/button text: button) or pressing the “Enter” key when presented with a (link: /terms/confirmation-slip text: confirmation slip).

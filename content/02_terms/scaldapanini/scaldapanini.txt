Title: Scaldapanini

----

Date: 24 July 2025

----

Text:

An affectionate nickname for (link: /terms/bunwarmer text: Bunwarmer) style prototypes based on the second generation (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad) hardware.

Scaldapanini roughly translates from Italian to “sandwich warmer”.

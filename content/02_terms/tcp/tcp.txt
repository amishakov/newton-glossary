Date: 24 May 2001

Text:

Transmission Control Protocol

Part of the [TCP/IP](/terms/tcp-ip) communications (link: /terms/protocol text: protocol). TCP provides transport functions that ensure the total amount of bytes sent is received correctly at the other end.

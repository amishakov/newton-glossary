Title: Dock

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

To connect a Newton device to another computer over a physical, wireless, or [infrared](/terms/infrared) connection in order to transfer data between the two devices.

Dock is also the name of an [application](/terms/application) in [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) 2.0 and later specifically used to connect to another computer.

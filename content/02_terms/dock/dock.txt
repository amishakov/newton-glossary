Title: Dock

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

To connect a Newton device to another computer over a physical, wireless, or (link: /terms/infrared text: infrared) connection in order to transfer data between the two devices.

Dock is also the name of an (link: /terms/application text: application) in (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) 2.0 and later specifically used to connect to another computer.

Title: Child

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), a (link: /terms/frame text: frame) that references another frame — its (link: /terms/parent text: parent) — from a `_parent` slot.

Similarly, when referring to a (link: /terms/view text: view), a (link: /terms/child-view text: child view) is enclosed by its (link: /terms/parent-view text: parent view).

Title: Dante

----

Date: 12 June 2001

----

Text:

One of the code names for the (link: /terms/messagepad-130 text: MessagePad 130) device, also known as (link: /terms/quark text: Quark).

Dante also refers to version 2.0 of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS), as well as the connection (link: /terms/protocol text: protocol) used to communicate between a Newton device and another computer.

Title: Auto Dock

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The automatic transfer of data between a Newton device to another computer once a (link: /terms/connection text: connection) has been made. Auto docking is activated by grounding one of the (link: /terms/newton-interconnect text: Newton InterConnect) port pins.

Auto Dock is also a section found in the (link: /terms/prefs text: Prefs) application.

In the Auto Dock (link: /terms/slip text: slip), the user can enable or disable the auto docking functionality.

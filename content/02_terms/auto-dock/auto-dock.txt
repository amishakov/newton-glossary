Title: Auto Dock

----

Date:

----

Text:

The automatic transfer of data between a Newton device to another computer once a [connection](/terms/connection) has been made. Auto docking is activated by grounding one of the [Newton Interconnect](/terms/newton-interconnect) port pins.

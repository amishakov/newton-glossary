Title: Broink

----

Date: 06 October 2002

----

Text:

The distinct sound made by an (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) when the hinge tension spring in the (link: /terms/clamshell text: clamshell) lid breaks. After an eMate goes _“broink”_, it will very likely develop a serious case of (link: /terms/floppy-lid text: floppy lid).

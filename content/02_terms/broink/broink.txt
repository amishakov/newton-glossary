Title: Broink

----

Date:

----

Text:

The distinct sound made by an [eMate](/terms/emate-300) when the hinge tension spring in the [clamshell](/terms/clamshell) lid breaks. After an eMate goes _“broink”_, it will very likely develop a serious case of [floppy lid](/terms/floppy-lid).

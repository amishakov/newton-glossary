Title: Newton Farm

----

Date: 01 December 2016

----

Text:

A group of Newton devices that have been networked or otherwise connected together to perform a specific task.

An example of this can be found in Sean Luke’s description of a fictional project at the [University of Maryland](https://www.cs.umd.edu) turning 10,000 (link: /terms/messagepad-100 text: MessagePad 100) devices into a “massively parallel combined supercomputer” in 1996.

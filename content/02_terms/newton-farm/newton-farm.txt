Title: Newton Farm

----

Date:

----

Text:

A group of Newton devices that have been networked or otherwise connected together to perform a specific task.

An example of this can be found in Sean Luke’s description of a fictional project at the [University of Maryland](http://www.cs.umd.edu/) turning 10,000 [MessagePad 100](/terms/messagepad-100) devices into a “massively parallel combined supercomputer” in 1996.

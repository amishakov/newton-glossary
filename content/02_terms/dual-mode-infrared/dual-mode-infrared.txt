Title: Dual-Mode Infrared

----

Date: 10 July 2024

----

Text:

A method of supporting different modes of [infrared](/terms/infrared) communication between devices using a single [transceiver](/terms/transceiver).

The [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000), [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100), and [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) utilize dual-mode infrared transceivers, allowing them to support wireless data transfer using both [IrDA](/terms/irda) and [ASK](/terms/ask) protocols.

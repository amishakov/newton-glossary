Title: Deselect

----

Date: 09 December 2024

----

Text:

To reverse the state of a previously (link: /terms/select text: selected) object.

For example, to deselect a (link: /terms/checkbox text: checkbox), the user would (link: /terms/tap text: tap) the checkbox or its text label, reversing its setting. To deselect text or (link: /terms/picture text: pictures), the user would tap the (link: /terms/pen text: pen) outside the (link: /terms/selection text: selection).

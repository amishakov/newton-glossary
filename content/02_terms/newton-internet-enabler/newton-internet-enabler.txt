Title: Newton Internet Enabler

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

Software that runs in the background of [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os), enabling access to the internet and other information services using standard communication protocols such as [TCP/IP](/terms/tcp-ip).

Also referred to as simply [NIE](/terms/NIE).

Title: Deep Toast

----

Date: 01 December 2016

----

Text:

The status or (link: /terms/charge-state text: charge state) of a rechargeable (link: /terms/battery text: battery) in a Newton device that can no longer effectively hold a charge. The status message appears as (link: /terms/deeptoast text: deepToast) in the battery test section of the (link: /terms/newttest text: NewtTest) utility and the Battery Statistics (link: /terms/slip text: slip) in (link: /terms/avis-backdrop text: Avi's Backdrop).

Deep Toast can also refer to a catastrophic hardware failure, such as a fatal hard drive crash.

Title: Status Bar

----

Date: 25 November 2017

----

Text:

A thick black (link: /terms/line text: line) with graphical (link: /terms/button text: buttons) located at the bottom of a (link: /terms/view text: view) in an (link: /terms/application text: application). Status bar buttons perform functions related to the current application and can include an (link: /terms/analog-clock-button text: analog clock button), (link: /terms/info-button text: info button), (link: /terms/recognition-button text: recognition button), (link: /terms/keyboard-button text: keyboard button), (link: /terms/new-button text: new button), (link: /terms/action-button text: action button), and (link: /terms/close-box text: close box).

Title: Proto Template

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), a predefined (link: /terms/template text: template) that defines the appearance and behaviour of standard (link: /terms/user-interface text: user interface) elements, such as a (link: /terms/close-box text: close box) or (link: /terms/status-slip text: status slip).

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/proto text: Proto).

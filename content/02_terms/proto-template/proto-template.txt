Title: Proto Template

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript), a predefined [template](/terms/template) that defines the appearance and behaviour of standard [user interface](/terms/user-interface) elements, such as a [close box](/terms/close-box) or [status slip](/terms/status-slip).

Also referred to as a [Proto](/terms/proto).

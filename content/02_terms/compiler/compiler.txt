Title: Compiler

----

Date: 17 December 2024

----

Text:

Software that converts code written in a high-level programming language into data (link: /terms/object text: objects) that can be used by the (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS).

For example, (link: /terms/newton-toolkit text: Newton Toolkit) compiles and executes (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript) code, processes (link: /terms/template text: templates) and (link: /terms/book-file text: book files), producing (link: /terms/package text: packages) such as (link: /terms/application text: applications) and (link: /terms/newton-book text: Newton books).

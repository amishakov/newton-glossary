Title: EZ2VGA

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A [PC card](/terms/pc-card) peripheral by [EZShow Systems](/sources/ezshow-systems) that provides VGA video output when used with a [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000), [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100), or [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) device. The EZ2VGA is the less capable cousin of the [EZ2TV](/terms/ez2tv) card, which can supply composite, S-Video, and VGA output.

Title: Matte Border

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A visually distinct (link: /terms/newton-user-interface text: Newton user interface) element consisting of a thick grey border framed with black. A matte border indicates a (link: /terms/view text: view) that can be moved by the user. For example, a (link: /terms/slip text: slip) will commonly use a matte border.

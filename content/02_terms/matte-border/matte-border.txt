Title: Matte Border

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A visually distinct [Newton user interface](/terms/newton-user-interface) element consisting of a thick grey border framed with black. A matte border indicates a [view](/terms/view) that can be moved by the user. For example, a [slip](/terms/slip) will commonly use a matte border.

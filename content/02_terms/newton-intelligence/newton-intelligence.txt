Title: Newton Intelligence

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A marketing-focused term for the core technologies behind the (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) platform, encompassing (link: /terms/intelligent-assistance-architecture text: Intelligent Assistance Architecture), (link: /terms/newton-communications-architecture text: Newton Communications Architecture), (link: /terms/newton-information-architecture text: Newton Information Architecture), (link: /terms/newton-recognition-architecture text: Newton Recognition Architecture), and the (link: /terms/newton-hardware-architecture text: Newton Hardware Architecture).

This combination of technologies allowed a Newton device to anticipate the behaviour of the user, learning and acting on those assumptions.

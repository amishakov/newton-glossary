Title: K Diags

----

Date: 24 October 2022

----

Text:

A suite of software diagnostic tools created specifically for the [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) and supplied on a bootable PCMCIA card. The “K” is in reference to the eMate code name [Project K](/terms/project-k).

Title: nbk

----

Date: 21 November 2004

----

Text:

The [filename extension](/terms/filename-extension) used to identify [backup files](/terms/backup-file) created by the Windows versions of the [Newton Backup Utility](/terms/newton-backup-utility) and [Newton Connection Utilities](/terms/newton-connection-utilities) applications.

Title: Newton Data Browser

----

Date:

----

Text:

A desktop [application](/terms/application) created by Thomas Tempelmann that allows the user to retrieve and edit data located on a Newton device. Also known variously as [DIL Tester](/terms/dil-tester), [Newton DIL Tester](/terms/newton-dil-tester), [Newton Desktop Utility](/terms/newton-desktop-utility), [NDB](/terms/ndb), and [NDU](/terms/ndu).

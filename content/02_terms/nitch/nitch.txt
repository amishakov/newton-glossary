Title: Nitch

----

Date: 08 June 2024

----

Text:

An (link: /terms/application text: application) created by (link: /sources/eckhart-koppen text: Eckhart Köppen) that facilitates the organization of goals and the selection of next actions or tasks base on the (link: /terms/getting-things-done text: Getting Things Done) productivity system. Whereas Köppen’s (link: /terms/flashpoint text: Flashpoint) is a self-contained application that provides similar functionality, Nitch makes use of the built-in (link: /terms/notepad text: Notepad) application, offering a custom view of goals and their associated actions using custom (link: /terms/stationery text: stationery).

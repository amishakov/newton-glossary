Title: Pen Sound Effects

----

Date: 10 June 2025

----

Text:

Sounds that are played in conjunction with the use of the (link: /terms/pen text: pen) such as (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) and (link: /terms/highlighting text: highlighting) items on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen).

For example, when a user (link: /terms/tap text: taps) a (link: /terms/button text: button), a “(link: /terms/click text: click)” sound will be played. Similarly, when using the pen to create a (link: /terms/heavy-mark text: heavy mark) when (link: /terms/select text: selecting) an item, a “(link: /terms/squeak text: squeak)” sound is played.

Pen sound effects can be turned on and off in the (link: /terms/sound text: Sound) section of the (link: /terms/prefs text: Prefs) application.

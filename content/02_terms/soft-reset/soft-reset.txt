Title: Soft Reset

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A method of (link: /terms/reset text: resetting) the (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) by pressing the (link: /terms/reset-button text: reset button). A soft reset does not erase any user data, (link: /terms/application text: applications), packages, (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) training, or (link: /terms/preferences text: preferences).

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/warm-boot text: Warm Boot) or (link: /terms/software-reset text: Software Reset).

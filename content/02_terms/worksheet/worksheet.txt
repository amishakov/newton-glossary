Title: Worksheet

----

Date: 23 February 2025

Text:

A single (link: /terms/spreadsheet text: spreadsheet) created by the (link: /terms/quickfigure-pro text: QuickFigure Pro) and (link: /terms/quickfigure-works text: QuickFigure Works) applications.

QuickFigure Pro allows the user to combine multiple worksheets into a (link: /terms/workbook text: workbook).

Title: Daughterboard

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A small, secondary circuit board that generally is attached to the (link: /terms/main-logic-board text: main logic board) or (link: /terms/motherboard text: motherboard). The daughterboard in a Newton device contains the (link: /terms/rom text: ROM) chips.

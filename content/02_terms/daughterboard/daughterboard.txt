Title: Daughterboard

----

Date:

----

Text:

A small, secondary circuit board that generally is attached to the [main logic board](/terms/main-logic-board) or [motherboard](/terms/motherboard). The daughterboard in a Newton device contains the [ROM](/terms/rom) chips.

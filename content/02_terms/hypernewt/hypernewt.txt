Title: HyperNewt

----

Date: 01 October 2017

----

Text:

An (link: /terms/application text: application) developed by (link: /sources/adam-tow text: Adam Tow) that extends the built-in (link: /terms/folder text: folder) filing system of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS). HyperNewt provides flexible data management, hierarchical organization, and hyperlinking of items created by most Newton applications.

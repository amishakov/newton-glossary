Title: Roll View

----

Date: 15 August 2024

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), a (link: /terms/container-view text: container view) that presents several discrete subviews arranged vertically, one above the other. The (link: /terms/roll text: roll) can be viewed in (link: /terms/overview text: overview) mode, where each subview is represented by a single-line description. Any single subview or all subviews can be expanded to full size.

The built-in (link: /terms/notepad text: Notepad) application uses a roll view, allowing users to (link: /terms/scroll text: scroll) through multiple items.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/paper-roll text: Paper Roll) and (link: /terms/roll text: Roll).

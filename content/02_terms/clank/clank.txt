Title: Clank

----

Date:

----

Text:

The sound made by the Newton device after [scrolling](/terms/scroll) to the end of a list of text items, generally when using the [local scroll arrows](/terms/local-scroll-arrows). Also referred to as [Clanking](/terms/clanking) in the [NetHopper](/terms/nethopper) user manual.

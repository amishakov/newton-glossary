Title: MessagePad 110

----

Date: 27 October 2016

----

Text:

The MessagePad 110 was the second generation Newton device, featuring a revised industrial design and sharing that case style with the (link: /terms/messagepad-120 text: MessagePad 120) and (link: /terms/messagepad-130 text: MessagePad 130). Improvements over the (link: /terms/messagepad-100 text: MessagePad 100) included expanded memory, improved battery life, an integrated (link: /terms/screen text: screen) cover, and an optional (link: /terms/charging text: charging) cradle nicknamed the “(link: /terms/crib text: crib)”.

The code name of the MessagePad 110 was (link: /terms/lindy text: Lindy), but there was also a limited edition clear-cased version code named (link: /terms/jellyfish text: Jellyfish).

The MessagePad 110 is commonly referred to as an (link: /terms/mp110 text: MP110).

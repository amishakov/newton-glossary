Title: Analog Clock Button

----

Date: 11 March 2018

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/button text: button) displaying the current time of day located at the left end of the (link: /terms/status-bar text: status bar) in an (link: /terms/application text: application). (link: /terms/tapping text: Tapping) the analog clock button opens a small panel which displays a digital (link: /terms/clock text: clock) and the (link: /terms/battery-gauge text: battery gauge).

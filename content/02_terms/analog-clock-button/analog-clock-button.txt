Title: Analog Clock Button

----

Date: 11 March 2018

----

Text:

A [button](/terms/button) displaying the current time of day located at the left end of the [status bar](/terms/status-bar) in an [application](/terms/application). [Tapping](/terms/tapping) the analog clock button opens a small panel which displays a digital clock and the [battery gauge](/terms/battery-gauge).

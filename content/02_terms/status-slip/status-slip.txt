Title: Status Slip

----

Date:

----

Text:

A [view](/terms/view) displayed by an [application](/terms/application) when it begins an operation that takes more than a few seconds to complete. A status slip contains a message describing the applicationâ€™s busy status. If a status slip is hidden, a [notify button](/terms/notify-button) that looks like a [blinking star](/terms/blinking-star) will appear at the top of the screen. [Tapping](/terms/tapping) the [star](/terms/star) will display the status slip.

Title: MacinTalk

----

Date: 23 March 2017

----

Text:

The speech synthesizer component of the Newton (link: /terms/text-to-speech text: text-to-speech) software. MacinTalk provides a flexible (link: /terms/codec text: codec) which converts (link: /terms/recognized-text text: recognized text) into spoken audio. The synthesized speech can be controlled through various parameters such as voice type, speaking rate, (link: /terms/volume text: volume), and pitch.

MacinTalk first appeared as part of the operating system in the original Macintosh, hence the name.

Date: 07 May 2026

Text:

A version of Robert Leslie’s [MAD](https://www.underbit.com/products/mad/) audio decoder that was ported to (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) by (link: /sources/eckhart-koppen text: Eckhart Köppen). MAD Newton is utilized by (link: /terms/mad-max text: MAD Max), an (link: /terms/application text: application) that allows playback of MP3 audio on a Newton device.

Title: Polyswitch

----

Date: 08 December 2024

----

Text:

A passive electronic component used to protect circuitry from current exceeding a certain threshold. Also known as a resettable fuse or (link: /terms/pptc text: PPTC), a polyswitch is used in conjunction with a (link: /terms/thermistor text: thermistor) in rechargeable (link: /terms/battery-pack text: battery packs) to prevent overcharging the (link: /terms/cell text: cells).

Polyswitches can be found in battery packs used in (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000), (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100), and (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) devices.

Title: Ragout

----

Date: 19 April 2024

----

Text:

A developer utility for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) for inspecting, manipulating, and editing [soup](/terms/soup) data. Ragout was developed by [Jason Harper](http://nixietube.info/) and distributed by [Creative Digital Publishing](/sources/creative-digital-publishing).

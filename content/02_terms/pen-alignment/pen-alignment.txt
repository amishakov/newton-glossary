Title: Pen Alignment

----

Date: 26 June 2024

----

Text:

A measure of how accurate the use of the [pen](/terms/pen) is when interacting with the [screen](/terms/screen) of a Newton device. For example, when [tapping](/terms/tapping) the screen, if a [tap](/terms/tap) affects an area of the screen that is not directly under the tip of the pen, then the user needs to [realign](/terms/realign) the pen.

Also referred to as [Alignment](/terms/alignment).

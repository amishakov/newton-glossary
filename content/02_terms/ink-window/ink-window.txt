Title: Ink Window

----

Date: 15 January 2023

----

Text:

A toolbar interface for the [Ink](/terms/inkwell) handwriting recognition system built into certain versions of Mac OS X. The Ink Bar provided quick access to various [preferences](/terms/preferences) and [modifier keys](/terms/modifier-keys).

Also referred to as [InkBar](/terms/inkbar), [Ink Bar](/terms/ink-bar), and [Ink Toolbar](/terms/ink-toolbar).

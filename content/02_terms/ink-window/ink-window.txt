Title: Ink Window

----

Date: 15 January 2023

----

Text:

A floating (link: /terms/palette text: palette) or (link: /terms/toolbar text: toolbar) interface for the (link: /terms/inkwell text: Ink) handwriting recognition system built into certain versions of Mac OS X. The Ink Bar provided quick access to various (link: /terms/preferences text: preferences) and (link: /terms/modifier-keys text: modifier keys).

Also referred to as (link: /terms/inkbar text: InkBar), (link: /terms/ink-bar text: Ink Bar), and (link: /terms/ink-toolbar text: Ink Toolbar).

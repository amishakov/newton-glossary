Title: Grouper 100LT

----

Date: 01 October 2017

----

Text:

A member of the (link: /terms/grouper text: Grouper) family of devices developed by (link: /sources/digital-ocean text: Digital Ocean), this radio-based peripheral provided (link: /terms/wireless-appletalk text: wireless AppleTalk) connectivity over a (link: /terms/local-area-network text: local area network). Identical in functionality to the (link: /terms/grouper-100mp text: Grouper 100MP) and (link: /terms/grouper-110mp text: Grouper 110MP), but specifically designed for Mac OS computers.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/100lt text: 100LT).

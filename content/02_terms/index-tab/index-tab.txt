Title: Index Tab

----

Date: 24 August 2025

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/control text: control) that provides a faster method of navigating a long list of (link: /terms/picker text: picker) items by augmenting (link: /terms/scroll-arrow text: scroll arrows) with a series of small, alphabetically labeled (link: /terms/tab text: tabs) displayed along the right edge of a picker.

(link: /terms/tapping text: Tapping) an index tab (link: /terms/scroll text: scrolls) to the items that begin with the first letter displayed on the tab. (link: /terms/double-tapping text: Double-tapping) an index tab scrolls to the items that begin with the second letter of the tab. (link: /terms/triple-tapping text: Triple-tapping) an index tab scrolls to the picker items that begin with the third letter of the tab.

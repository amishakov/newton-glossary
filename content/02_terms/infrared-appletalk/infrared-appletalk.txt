Title: Infrared AppleTalk

----

Date: 17 November 2017

----

Text:

Providing [AppleTalk](/terms/appletalk) networking to devices over an [infrared](/terms/infrared) connection.

For example, Phototonics Corporation developed a product called Cooperative that consisted of hardware transceivers which provided [diffuse infrared](/terms/diffuse-infrared) AppleTalk connections between Mac OS computers, [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) devices, and other peripherals.

Title: Infrared AppleTalk

----

Date: 17 November 2017

----

Text:

Providing (link: /terms/appletalk text: AppleTalk) networking to devices over an (link: /terms/infrared text: infrared) connection.

For example, Phototonics Corporation developed a product called Cooperative that consisted of a hardware (link: /terms/transceiver text: transceiver) which provided (link: /terms/diffuse-infrared text: diffuse infrared) AppleTalk connections between Mac OS computers, Newton devices, and other peripherals.

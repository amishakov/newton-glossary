Title: Infrared AppleTalk

----

Date: 17 November 2017

----

Text:

Providing [AppleTalk](/terms/appletalk) networking to devices over an [infrared](/terms/infrared) connection. For example, Phototonics Corporation developed a product called Cooperative that consisted of hardware transceivers which provided “diffuse infrared AppleTalk” connections between Mac OS computers, Newton devices, and other peripherals.

Title: Notify Picker

----

Date: 10 June 2024

----

Text:

A list of ongoing [actions](/terms/action) and deferred notification alerts that appears when a user [taps](/terms/tap) the [notify button](/terms/notify-button). Selecting an item in the [picker](/terms/picker) displays a [status slip](/terms/status-slip) corresponding to that item. Once an item is chosen from the picker, it is removed from the list.

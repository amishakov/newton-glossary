Title: Notify Picker

----

Date: 10 June 2024

----

Text:

A list of ongoing (link: /terms/action text: actions) and deferred notification alerts that appears when a user (link: /terms/tap text: taps) the (link: /terms/notify-button text: notify button). Selecting an item in the (link: /terms/picker text: picker) displays a (link: /terms/status-slip text: status slip) corresponding to that item. Once an item is chosen from the picker, it is removed from the list.

Title: Latch

----

Date: 07 August 2024

----

Text:

A mechanism which secures or releases a component located on a Newton device such as a (link: /terms/battery text: battery), (link: /terms/pc-card text: PC card), or (link: /terms/lid text: lid).

On an (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300), the (link: /terms/screen-lid text: screen lid) latch is referred to as a (link: /terms/locking-tab text: locking tab).

Title: Latch

----

Date: 07 August 2024

----

Text:

A mechanism which secures or releases a component located on a Newton device such as a [battery](/terms/battery), [PC card](/terms/pc-card), or [lid](/terms/lid).

On an [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300), the [screen lid](/terms/screen-lid) latch is referred to as a [locking tab](/terms/locking-tab).

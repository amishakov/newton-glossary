Title: Fast Infrared

----

Date:

----

Text:

Transferring data using an [IrDA](/terms/irda)-compatible [infrared](/terms/infrared) connection at a rate up to 4 Mbps. As opposed to [Slow Infrared](/terms/slow-infrared) which only transfers data at a rate up to 115 Kbps.

Fast Infrared is also referred to as [FIR](/terms/fir).

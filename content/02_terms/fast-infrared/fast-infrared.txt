Title: Fast Infrared

----

Date: 01 December 2016

----

Text:

Transferring data using an (link: /terms/irda text: IrDA)-compatible (link: /terms/infrared text: infrared) connection at a rate up to 4 Mbps. As opposed to (link: /terms/slow-infrared text: Slow Infrared) which only transfers data at a rate up to 115 Kbps.

Fast Infrared is also referred to as (link: /terms/fir text: FIR).

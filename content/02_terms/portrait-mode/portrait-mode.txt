Title: Portrait Mode

----

Date: 03 January 2025

----

Text:

When the content on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) of a Newton device is displayed in a vertical (link: /terms/orientation text: orientation). The opposite of portrait mode is (link: /terms/landscape-mode text: landscape mode).

The orientation can be changed by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the (link: /terms/rotate-button text: Rotate Button).

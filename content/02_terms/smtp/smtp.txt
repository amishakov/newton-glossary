----

Date: 15 October 2024

Text:

Simple Mail Transfer Protocol

A standard protocol used to send and recieve email messages from a mail server over a (link: /terms/tcp-ip text: TCP/IP) connection.

(link: /terms/simplemail text: SimpleMail) and (link: /terms/mail-v text: Mail V) are (link: /terms/application text: applications) that use SMTP to send email from a Newton device.

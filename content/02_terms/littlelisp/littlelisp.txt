Title: LittleLisp

----

Date: 11 November 2023

----

Text:

A [Lisp](/terms/lisp) interpreter for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) developed by [David Benn](/sources/david-benn) that includes graphics extensions and the ability to invoke [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript) functions.

Title: Special Interest Group

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A like-minded collection of individuals who concentrate their knowledge, skills, and abilities on a special theme, technology, or goal. A (link: /terms/newton-user-group text: Newton User Group) could be considered to be a special interest group dedicated to Newton hardware, software, support, and community.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/sig text: SIG).

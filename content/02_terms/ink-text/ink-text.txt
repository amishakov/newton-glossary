Title: Ink Text

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

Words written in (link: /terms/electronic-ink text: electronic ink).

Ink Text is also one of the (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) settings available when (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the (link: /terms/recognition-button text: recognition button) in an (link: /terms/application text: application) such as (link: /terms/notepad text: Notepad). Ink text allows the user to take (link: /terms/input text: input) handwriting quickly on a Newton device, which can then can be converted to (link: /terms/recognized-text text: recognized text) at a later time.

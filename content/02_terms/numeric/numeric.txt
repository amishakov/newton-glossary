Title: Numeric

----

Date: 31 August 2017

----

Text:

An onscreen [keyboard](/terms/keyboard) that appears when the [keyboard button](/terms/keyboard-button) is [tapped](/terms/tapped). The numeric keyboard is designed for easily entering numbers and calculations into [applications](/terms/application) such as as [Newton Works](/terms/newton-works). Several other onscreen keyboards are available for entering specific types of data, including [Typewriter](/terms/typewriter), [Phone](/terms/phone), and [Time/Date](/terms/time-date).

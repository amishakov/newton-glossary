Title: Auto Part

----

Date:

----

Text:

A software [part](/terms/part) that contains a background [application](/terms/application) or [extension](/terms/extension) which is not accessed through an [icon](/terms/icon) in the [Extras Drawer](/terms/extras-drawer). Auto parts can include application [stationery](/terms/stationery), formula or preference panels, [routing](/terms/routing) formats, and communication [transports](/terms/transport). Auto parts also have the option of being automatically removed after they are installed.

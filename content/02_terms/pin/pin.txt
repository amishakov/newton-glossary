Title: PIN

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

Personal Identification Number

A unique series of numerals that allows secure access to a system or device.

To prevent unauthorized people from using a [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) device, you can set a [password](/terms/password) in the form of a PIN using the [Prefs](/terms/prefs) application.

Also referred to as a [System Password](/terms/system-password) in the [Owner Info](/terms/owner-info) preferences [slip](/terms/slip).

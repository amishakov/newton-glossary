Title: PIN

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

Personal Identification Number

A unique series of numerals that allows secure access to a system or device.

To prevent unauthorized people from using a Newton device, you can set a (link: /terms/password text: password) in the form of a PIN using the (link: /terms/security text: Security) section of the (link: /terms/prefs text: Prefs) application.

(image: /assets/images/enter-pin-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of the Enter Pin slip width: 126)

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/system-password text: System Password) in the (link: /terms/owner-info text: Owner Info) preferences (link: /terms/slip text: slip).

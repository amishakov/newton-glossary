Title: Sliding Cover

----

Date: 04 August 2024

----

Text:

A movable panel located on an [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) device that covers the [communication ports](/terms/communication-port). Sliding the panel one direction reveals a [Mini-DIN](/terms/mini-din) serial port, while sliding it the other direction reveals a [Newton InterConnect](/terms/newton-interconnect) port.

Also referred to as a [Door](/terms/door) or [Sliding Door](/terms/sliding-door).

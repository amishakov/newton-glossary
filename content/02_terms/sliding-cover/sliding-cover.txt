Title: Sliding Cover

----

Date: 04 August 2024

----

Text:

A movable panel located on an (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) device that covers the (link: /terms/communication-port text: communication ports). Sliding the panel one direction reveals a (link: /terms/mini-din text: Mini-DIN) serial port, while sliding it the other direction reveals a (link: /terms/newton-interconnect text: Newton InterConnect) port.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/door text: Door) or (link: /terms/sliding-door text: Sliding Door).

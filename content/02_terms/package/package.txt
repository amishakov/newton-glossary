Title: Package

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The unit in which an (link: /terms/application text: application) or (link: /terms/extension text: extension) can be installed to or removed from a Newton device. A package consists of a header containing the package name and other information, and one or more parts containing the software. Identified by the (link: /terms/pkg text: .pkg) or (link: /terms/newtonpkg text: .newtonpkg) filename extension.

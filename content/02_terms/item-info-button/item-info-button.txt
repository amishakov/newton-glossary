Title: Item Info Button

----

Date: 17 July 2024

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/picture-button text: picture button) located on the left side of a (link: /terms/separator-bar text: separator bar), depicting the type of item appearing below.

For example, in the (link: /terms/notepad text: Notepad) application, (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) an item info (link: /terms/button text: button) displays an (link: /terms/item-info-slip text: item info slip), that displays statistics and related information for an item.

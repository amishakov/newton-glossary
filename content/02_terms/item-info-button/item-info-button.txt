Title: Item Info Button

----

Date: 17 July 2024

----

Text:

A [picture button](/terms/picture-button) located on the left side of a [separator bar](/terms/separator-bar), depicting the type of item appearing below.

For example, in the [Notepad](/terms/notepad) application, [tapping](/terms/tapping) an item info [button](/terms/button) displays an [item info slip](/terms/item-info-slip), that displays statistics and related information for an item.

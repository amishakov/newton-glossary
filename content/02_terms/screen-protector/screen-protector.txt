Title: Screen Protector

----

Date: 13 July 2025

----

Text:

A transparent membrane designed to cover the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) of a device such as a (link: /terms/pda text: PDA), smartphone, (link: /terms/tablet text: tablet), or (link: /terms/laptop-computer text: laptop computer) to protect it from damage and wear. Screen protectors can consist of plastic film, ultra thin tempered glass, or a liquid which strengthen the screen material itself.

The removable (link: /terms/lid text: lid) found on most (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad) devices also acts a screen protector when the device is not in use.

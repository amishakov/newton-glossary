Title: Graphing Calculator

----

Date: 14 December 2017

----

Text:

A program built into the (link: /terms/newton-works text: Newton Works) application that uses specific (link: /terms/stationery text: stationery) allowing the user to perform calculations and display the results in a graph or table. Information formatting using the (link: /terms/calculations text: calculations) stationery can be copied and pasted into other (link: /terms/application text: applications) on a Newton device.

Text:

Very Large Scale Integration

Semiconductor integrated circuits composed of hundreds of thousands of logic elements or memory cells.

VLSI Technology was also the fabrication partner that produced processors and custom integrated circuits, including those used by Apple in its Macintosh and Newton products.

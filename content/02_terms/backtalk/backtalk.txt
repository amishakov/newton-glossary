Title: BackTalk

----

Date: 27 May 2018

----

Text:

Software developed by (link: /sources/smartdog text: Smart Dog Software) that provides updated (link: /terms/infrared text: infrared) communications (link: /terms/protocol text: protocols) for certain Newton devices. BackTalk is comprised of several system level components including a beam (link: /terms/transport text: transport) and (link: /terms/infrared text: infrared) object exchange ((link: /terms/obex text: OBEX)) stack, providing the ability to exchange information with other devices such as Palm handhelds.

Title: NEWT/64

----

Date: 05 June 2024

----

Text:

A prototype-oriented [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript) interpreter and compiler designed to run on 64-bit processors.

NEWT/64 was developed by [Matthias Melcher](/sources/matthias-melcher) and is based on the [Newt/0](/terms/newt0) code originally released by [Makoto “gnue” Nukui](/sources/gnue)..

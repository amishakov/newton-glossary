Newton User Group Central

A joint effort between the [North Atlanta Newton Users Group](/terms/nanug) (NANUG) and the [Arizona Macintosh Users Group](http://www.amug.org/) (AMUG) that provided resources for Newton owners, developers, and other user groups.

Title: Background Picture

----

Date: 08 November 2017

----

Text:

An image displayed behind the main (link: /terms/user-interface text: user interface) on a Newton device when (link: /terms/avis-backdrop text: Avi’s Backdrop) is set as the (link: /terms/backdrop text: backdrop) application. A background picture is basically a (link: /terms/newton-book text: Newton book) containing a (link: /terms/bitmap text: bitmap) image.

A background picture can be created using software such as (link: /terms/newton-press text: Newton Press), (link: /terms/newton-book-maker text: Newton Book Maker), or (link: /terms/newts-cape text: Newt’s Cape) and delivered as an installable (link: /terms/package text: package).

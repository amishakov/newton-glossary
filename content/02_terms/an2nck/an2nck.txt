Title: AN2NCK

----

Date:

----

Text:

Software created by [Iambic Software](https://web.archive.org/web/19980526091703/http://www.iambic.com/) that allows the [Newton Connection](/terms/newton-connection-kit) application to access the additional data fields which [Action Names](/terms/action-names) adds to each record used by the of the built-in [Name File](/terms/name-file) application.

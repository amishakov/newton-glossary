Title: UniFEP

----

Date: 06 February 2024

----

Text:

A series of modular software products developed by (link: /sources/enfour text: Enfour) that enabled multilingual input on various computing platforms. The (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) version of UniFEP supported Japanese input and output on a English langauge Newton device.

The name is derived from (link: /terms/unicode text: Unicode) and Front-End Processor.

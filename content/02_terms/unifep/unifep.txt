Title: UniFEP

----

Date: 06 February 2024

----

Text:

A series of modular software products developed by [Enfour](/sources/enfour) that enabled multilingual input on various computing platforms. The [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) version of UniFEP supported Japanese input and output on a English langauge Newton device.

The name is derived from [Unicode](/terms/unicode) and Front-End Processor.

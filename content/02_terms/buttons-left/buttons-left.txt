Title: Buttons Left

----

Date: 03 January 2025

----

Text:

An option in the (link: /terms/rotate-button text: Rotate Button) picker which moves the (link: /terms/button-bar text: button bar) to the left side when the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) is in (link: /terms/landscape-mode text: landscape mode).

If the button bar is currently displayed on the left side of the screen, the option will be named (link: /terms/buttons-right text: Buttons Right).

(image: /assets/images/buttons-left-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of the Rotate Button with Picker showing the Buttons Left option width: 155)

This option only appears on (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000) and (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100) devices.

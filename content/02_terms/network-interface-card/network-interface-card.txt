Title: Network Interface Card

----

Date: 12 January 2017

----

Text:

A removable [PC card](/terms/pc-card) or circuit board that provides a physical connection to a network, generally via [ethernet](/terms/ethernet). Also referred to as a [NIC](/terms/nic) or [network adapter](/terms/network-adapter).

Title: Network Interface Card

----

Date: 12 January 2017

----

Text:

A removable (link: /terms/pc-card text: PC card) or circuit board that provides a physical connection to a network, generally via (link: /terms/ethernet text: ethernet).

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/nic text: NIC) or (link: /terms/network-adapter text: Network Adapter).

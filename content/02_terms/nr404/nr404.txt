Title: NR404

----

Date: 05 January 2024

----

Text:

A drum machine and beatbox [application](/terms/application) created by Steve Cronin and [Francis Preve](https://www.francispreve.com/). NR404 includes thirteen percussive instrument [samples](/terms/sample) which can be assigned to one of four tracks, each with its own [volume](/terms/volume) control. There are also settings for the time signature, numbers of measures, and tempo.

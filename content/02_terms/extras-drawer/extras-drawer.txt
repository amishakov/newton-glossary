Title: Extras Drawer

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A built-in (link: /terms/container-view text: container view) that displays named (link: /terms/picture-button text: picture buttons) or (link: /terms/icon text: icons) which a user (link: /terms/tap text: taps) to open (link: /terms/application text: applications). The Extras Drawer is also used to set the default (link: /terms/backdrop text: backdrop) application.

Title: Put Away

----

Date: 06 October 2002

----

Text:

A choice in the (link: /terms/tag-button text: tag button) picker which moves the current item or (link: /terms/select text: selected) items to their logical home. For example, an item containing contact information would be “put away” in the (link: /terms/name-file text: Name File) application.

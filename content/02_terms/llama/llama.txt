Title: Llama

----

Date: 06 October 2002

----

Text:

The unofficial mascot of the Apple’s [Developer Technical Services](/terms/developer-technical-services) team within the [Newton Systems Group](/terms/newton-systems-group). [Maurice Sharp](/sources/maurice-sharp), otherwise known as [Dr Llama](/terms/dr-llama), wrote a Newton-related column entitled [Ask The Llama](/terms/ask-the-llama) for Apple’s developer publications.

Llama is also used as an example of a valid [application](/terms/application) name and [application signature](/terms/application-signature) in the [Newton Programmer’s Guide](/terms/newton-programmers-guide).

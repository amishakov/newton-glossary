Title: Newton Media System

----

Date: 05 September 2025

----

Text:

A suite of multimedia technologies for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) developed by (link: /terms/brad-tober text: Brad Tober), based on the work of (link: /terms/lee-moon text: Lee Moon).

The system includes (link: /terms/newton-movie-player text: Newton Movie Player) for playback of self-contained, animated images with accompanying audio, (link: /terms/newton-audio-player text: Newton Audio Player) for playback of self-contained audio files, and the unreleased (link: /terms/newton-media-maker text: Newton Media Maker) for creation of movie or audio files for use with the player (link: /terms/application text: applications).

Also referred to as (link: /terms/nms text: NMS).

A Japanese website that provided news, information, and other resources related to the Newton platform. Also known as (link: /terms/jni text: JNI), the website was maintained by [Nobuo Kawaguchi](https://web.archive.org/web/20001211055800/http://www.inagaki.nuie.nagoya-u.ac.jp/person/kawaguti/index.html), an engineering professor at Nagoya University.

Title: Graffiti

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A software-based (link: /terms/input text: input) system that converts predefined (link: /terms/pen text: pen) strokes and (link: /terms/gesture text: gestures) into digital text. Graffiti was originally developed by [Palm Computing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Palm,_Inc.) as the recognition system for GEOS and Magic Cap devices, later adapted to the Palm and (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) platforms.

Title: Quick Sketch

----

Date: 27 January 2024

----

Text:

A feature found in (link: /terms/newton-works text: Newton Works) that allows the user to easily create and insert a hand drawn image into a word processing document. (link: /terms/tapping text: Tapping) the (link: /terms/recognition-button text: recognition button) in the Quick Sketch (link: /terms/slip text: slip), lets the user select whether the (link: /terms/sketch text: sketch) is converted to (link: /terms/shapes text: shapes) or stored the way it was originally drawn as (link: /terms/electronic-ink text: electronic ink).

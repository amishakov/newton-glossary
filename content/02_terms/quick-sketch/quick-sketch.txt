Title: Quick Sketch

----

Date: 27 January 2024

----

Text:

A feature found in [Newton Works](/terms/newton-works) that allows the user to easily create and insert a hand drawn image into a word processing document. [Tapping](/terms/tapping) the [recognition button](/terms/recognition-button) in the Quick Sketch [slip](/terms/slip), lets the user select whether the [sketch](/terms/sketch) is converted to [shapes](/terms/shapes) or stored the way it was originally drawn as [electronic ink](/terms/electronic-ink).

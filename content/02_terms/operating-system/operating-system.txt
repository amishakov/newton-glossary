Title: Operating System

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

The primary software program that controls a computing device. The primary component of an operating system is the [kernel](/terms/kernel), which resides in memory at all times.

Also referred to as simply [OS](/terms/os) or [System](/terms/os).

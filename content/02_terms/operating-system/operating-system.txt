Title: Operating System

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

The primary software program that controls a computing device. The primary component of an operating system is the (link: /terms/kernel text: kernel), which resides in memory at all times.

Also referred to as an (link: /terms/os text: OS) or (link: /terms/os text: System).

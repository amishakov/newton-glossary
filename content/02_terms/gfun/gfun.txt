Groupe Francophone des Utilisateurs de Newton

A support community for French speaking Newton users operated by Dominique Benet. GFUN was superseded by the similarly named (link: /terms/ngfun text: NGFUN) (Nouveau Groupe des Utilisateurs Francophones de Newton).

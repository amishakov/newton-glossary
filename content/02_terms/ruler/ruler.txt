Title: Ruler

----

Date: 25 August 2025

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/user-interface text: user interface) element consisting of a linear scale that relates various (link: /terms/paragraph-settings text: paragraph settings). A ruler is generally used when formatting a (link: /terms/word-processing-document text: word processing document), including margins, line spacing, justification, and (link: /terms/tab-stop text: tab stop) settings.

Applications such as (link: /terms/newton-press text: Newton Press) and the word processing component of (link: /terms/newton-works text: Newton Works) both utilize a ruler.

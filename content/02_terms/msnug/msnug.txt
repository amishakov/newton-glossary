Michigan State Newton Users Group

Originally founded by [Douglas B. Luckie](https://cf.psl.msu.edu/dluckie.html) in April 1997, this user group became a (link: /terms/special-interest-group text: special interest group) (Newton SIG) within the Michigan State Palmtop Users Group (MSPUG) after the Newton was [discontinued](https://web.archive.org/web/20040528183253/http://www.apple.com/pr/library/1998/feb/27newton.html) in 1998.

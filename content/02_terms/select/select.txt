Title: Select

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

To designate a (link: /terms/user-interface text: user interface) object by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping), (link: /terms/double-tapping text: double-tapping), or (link: /terms/dragging text: dragging) across it. The next action that happens is applied to the selected object.

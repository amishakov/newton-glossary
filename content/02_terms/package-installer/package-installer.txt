Title: Package Installer

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A software program that facilitates the installation of (link: /terms/package text: packages) on a Newton device from a (link: /terms/desktop-computer text: desktop computer), (link: /terms/laptop-computer text: laptop), or a network location.

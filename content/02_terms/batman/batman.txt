Title: Batman

----

Date:

----

Text:

The code name of the original design concepts for the [MessagePad](/terms/messagepad), featuring a “dark, sleek and sculpted aesthetic” and incorporating a automotive-inspired hood or cowl-shaped lid. Also referred to as the [Batman Concept](/terms/batman-concept) and [Batmobile](/terms/batmobile) prototype.

Title: Heavy Mark

----

Date: 12 June 2001

----

Text:

A large dot that appears when you hold the (link: /terms/pen text: pen) down in one place on the Newton (link: /terms/screen text: screen). Used to start the action of (link: /terms/select text: selecting) a block of text or a (link: /terms/shape text: shape). A Newton device will audibly (link: /terms/squeak text: squeak) when the dot appears.

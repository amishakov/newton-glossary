Title: Folder

----

Date: 01 October 2017

----

Text:

A place where items such as (link: /terms/note text: notes), (link: /terms/card text: cards), and (link: /terms/package text: packages) can be filed. (link: /terms/global-folder text: Global folders) appear in all (link: /terms/application text: applications) and (link: /terms/local-folder text: local folders) appear only in the application where they were created.

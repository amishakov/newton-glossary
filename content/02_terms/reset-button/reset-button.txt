Title: Reset Button

----

Date:

----

Text:

A [button](/terms/button) found on the back of a Newton device which performs a [power reset](/terms/power-reset) or [soft reset](/terms/soft-reset). Pressing the reset button will often resolve problems that may occur during the operation of the device.

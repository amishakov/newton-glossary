Title: Reset Button

----

Date: 12 June 2001

----

Text:

A physical (link: /terms/button text: button) found on the back of a Newton device which performs a (link: /terms/power-reset text: power reset) or (link: /terms/soft-reset text: soft reset). Pressing the reset button will often resolve problems that may occur during the operation of the device.

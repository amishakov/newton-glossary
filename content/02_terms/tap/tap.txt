Title: Tap

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

To touch the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) briefly with the (link: /terms/pen text: pen). Essentially the same action as clicking a mouse or trackpad button on a (link: /terms/desktop-computer text: desktop computer), (link: /terms/laptop-computer text: laptop), or equivalent device.

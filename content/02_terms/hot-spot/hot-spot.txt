Title: Hot Spot

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A small unnamed and often unmarked (link: /terms/control text: control) that responds like a (link: /terms/button text: button) when (link: /terms/tapped text: tapped). Usually there are several hot spots in a given (link: /terms/view text: view), which can be visible or invisible to the user.

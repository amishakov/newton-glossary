Title: Binary Object

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), a sequence of bytes that can represent any type of data, can be adjusted dynamically in size, and can have a user-specified (link: /terms/class text: class). Examples of binary (link: /terms/object text: objects) include strings, real numbers, sounds, and (link: /terms/bitmap text: bitmaps).

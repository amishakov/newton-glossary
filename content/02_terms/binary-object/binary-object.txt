Title: Binary Object

----

Date:

----

Text:

A sequence of bytes that can represent any kind of data, can be adjusted dynamically in size, and can have a user-specified [class](/terms/class). Examples of binary [objects](/terms/object) include strings, real numbers, sounds, and [bitmaps](/terms/bitmap).

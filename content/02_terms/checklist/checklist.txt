Title: Checklist

----

Date: 28 September 2017

----

Text:

A type of [stationery](/terms/stationery) in the [Notepad](/terms/notepad) application that can be used to create an list of items which can then be marked or “checked off” as completed by [tapping](/terms/tapping) a [checkbox](/terms/checkbox).

Title: Double-Tap

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

To touch or (link: /terms/tap text: tap) the same spot on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen), or nearly the same spot, twice in rapid succession with the (link: /terms/pen text: pen). Essentially the same action as double clicking a mouse button on a (link: /terms/desktop-computer text: desktop computer), (link: /terms/laptop-computer text: laptop), or equivalent device.

Double-Tap was also a short-lived magazine published in 1993 and 1994 by the Software Frameworks Association. Focused on both the technical and business side of software development, it covered (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) and other (link: /terms/personal-digital-assistant text: personal digital assistant) platforms, such as the Tandy Zoomer.

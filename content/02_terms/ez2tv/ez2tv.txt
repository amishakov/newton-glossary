Title: EZ2TV

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/pc-card text: PC card) peripheral by (link: /sources/ezshow-systems text: EZShow Systems) that provides composite, S-Video, and VGA video output when used with a (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000), (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100), or (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) device. The EZ2TV is the more capable cousin of the (link: /terms/ez2vga text: EZ2VGA) card, which can supply VGA output only.

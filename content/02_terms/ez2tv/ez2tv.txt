Title: EZ2TV

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A [PC card](/terms/pc-card) peripheral by [EZShow Systems](/sources/ezshow-systems) that provides composite, S-Video, and VGA video output when used with a [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000), [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100), or [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) device. The EZ2TV is the more capable cousin of the [EZ2VGA](/terms/ez2vga) card, which can supply VGA output only.

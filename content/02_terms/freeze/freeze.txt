Title: Freeze

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

To suspend the activity of an (link: /terms/application text: application) or (link: /terms/extension text: extension) package. Freezing a (link: /terms/package text: package) is similar to removing an (link: /terms/external-store text: external store) that a package is stored on. In most cases, a (link: /terms/frozen-package text: frozen package) should free up memory on the Newton device.

To reactivate a package, you (link: /terms/thaw text: thaw) it.

Freeze also refers to a situation where the (link: /terms/operating-system text: operating system) becomes unresponsive, usually due to a lack of (link: /terms/heap-space text: heap space).

Title: Write-Protected

----

Date: 22 July 2024

----

Text:

The state of a (link: /terms/storage-card text: storage card) that has been locked using the (link: /terms/write-protection-switch text: write-protection switch). This indicates to the (link: /terms/operating-system text: operating system) that data on the (link: /terms/card text: card) cannot be modified, thus preventing the accidental overwriting or deletion of data.

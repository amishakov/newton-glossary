Title: Write-Protected

----

Date: 22 July 2024

----

Text:

The state of a [storage card](/terms/storage-card) that has been locked using the [write-protection switch](/terms/write-protection-switch). This indicates to the [operating system](/terms/operating-system) that data on the [card](/terms/card) cannot be modified, thus preventing the accidental overwriting or deletion of data.

Title: EZPresenter

----

Date:

----

Text:

An [application](/terms/application) developed by [EZShow Systems](/sources/ezshow-systems) that allows the user to view PowerPoint slides on [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000), [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100), and [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) devices. EZPresenter requires that the PowerPoint slides be converted to a [package file](/terms/package-file) with the [EZXfer](/terms/ezxfer) utility.

Title: Store Part

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/part text: part) that encapsulates a read-only (link: /terms/store text: store). This store can contain one or more (link: /terms/soup text: soup) objects. Store parts permit soup-like access to read-only data residing in a (link: /terms/package text: package).

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/package-store text: Package Store).

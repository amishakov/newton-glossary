Title: Store Part

----

Date:

----

Text:

A [part](/terms/part) that encapsulates a read-only [store](/terms/store). This store may contain one or more [soup](/terms/soup) objects. Store parts permit soup-like access to read-only data residing in a [package](/terms/package). Also referred to as [package stores](/terms/package-store).

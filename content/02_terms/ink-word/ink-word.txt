Title: Ink Word

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The grouping of (link: /terms/ink-data text: ink data) created by the recognition system, based on the timing and spacing of the user’s handwriting. Ink words are created when the user has selected (link: /terms/ink-text text: Ink Text) in the “Recognition Preferences” slip. Ink words can subsequently be recognized with (link: /terms/deferred-recognition text: deferred recognition).

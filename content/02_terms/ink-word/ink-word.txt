Title: Ink Word

----

Date:

----

Text:

The grouping of [ink data](/terms/ink-data) created by the recognition system, based on the timing and spacing of the user’s handwriting. Ink words are created when the user has selected [Ink Text](/terms/ink-text) in the “Recognition Preferences” slip. Ink words can subsequently be recognized with [deferred recognition](/terms/deferred-recognition).

_This entry requires a screenshot._

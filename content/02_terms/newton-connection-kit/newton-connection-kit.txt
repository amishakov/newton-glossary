Title: Newton Connection Kit

----

Date:

----

Text:

A consumer product developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) that included the [Newton Connection](/terms/newton-connection) software, a serial cable, and related documentation for connecting Newton devices to Mac OS and Windows computers, allowing the user to exchange and [synchronize](/terms/synchronize) information with common desktop [applications](/terms/application). The Newton Connection Kit is for use with earlier versions of [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os). The equivalent software package for Newton OS 2.0 and later is [Newton Connection Utilities](/terms/newton-connection-utilities).

Also referred to as [NCK](/terms/nck).

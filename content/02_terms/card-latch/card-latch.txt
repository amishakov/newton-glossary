Title: Card Latch

----

Date: 23 April 2017

----

Text:

A small locking mechanism found on [Original MessagePad](/terms/original-messagepad) and [MessagePad 100](/terms/messagepad-100) devices which prevents the [PC card](/terms/pc-card) from being inadvertently ejected from the [card slot](/terms/card-slot).

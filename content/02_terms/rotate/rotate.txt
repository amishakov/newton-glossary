Title: Rotate

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

To change the (link: /terms/orientation text: orientation) of the content displayed on a Newton device. This is accomplished by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the (link: /terms/rotate-button text: Rotate button) located in the (link: /terms/status-bar text: status bar) of the (link: /terms/extras-drawer text: Extras Drawer).

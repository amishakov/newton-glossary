Title: Thaw

----

Date:

----

Text:

To restore the activity of an [application](/terms/application) or [extension](/terms/extension) package. Thawing a [package](/terms/package) is similar to activating an [external store](/terms/external-store) — such as a [PC card](/terms/pc-card) — that a package is stored on.

To suspend a package, you [freeze](/terms/freeze) it.

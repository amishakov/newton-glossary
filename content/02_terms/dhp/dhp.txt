Dreaded Hinge Problem

Refers to a situation where the hinge tension spring of an [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) fails and causes a serious case of [floppy lid](/terms/floppy-lid).

Also referred to as the [Spring Issue](/terms/spring-issue).

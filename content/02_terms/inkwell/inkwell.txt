Title: Inkwell

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The initial name given to the Mac OS version of the Newton (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) system, eventually known simply as (link: /terms/ink text: Ink). Inkwell originally appeared in Mac OS 9 before being included in certain versions of Mac OS X.

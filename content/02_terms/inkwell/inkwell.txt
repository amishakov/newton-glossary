Title: Inkwell

----

Date:

----

Text:

The initial name given to the Mac OS version of the Newton [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) system, eventually known simply as [Ink](/terms/ink). Inkwell originally appeared in Mac OS 9 before being included in certain versions of Mac OS X.

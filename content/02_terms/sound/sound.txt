Title: Sound

----

Date: 23 January 2025

----

Text:

A section found in the (link: /terms/prefs text: Prefs) application.

In the Sound (link: /terms/slip text: slip), the user can specify whether audio is played through the internal speaker, through an external (link: /terms/output text: output) device, or both.

The user can also set the (link: /terms/alert-sound text: alert sound), the (link: /terms/volume text: volume) of those alerts, and whether or not sound effects are played when using the (link: /terms/pen text: pen).

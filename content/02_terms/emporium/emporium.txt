Title: Emporium

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The permanent internal description of a place where a user interacts with their Newton device, commonly referred to as a (link: /terms/locale text: locale) or (link: /terms/worksite text: worksite). Examples of named locales would be “Home” and “Office”. Choosing an emporium sets up location-specific information such as the area code, dialing prefix, time zone, printer settings, and email addresses.

The plural of emporium is emporia. Now you know.

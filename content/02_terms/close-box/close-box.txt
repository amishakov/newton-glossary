Title: Close Box

----

Date:

----

Text:

A small square [button](/terms/button) containing an “X” character, located in the lower right corner of a [container view](/terms/container-view). [Tapping](/terms/tapping) the button dismisses or closes the container view. Compare with the [large close box](/terms/large-close-box).

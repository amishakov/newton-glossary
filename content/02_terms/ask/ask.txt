Text:

Amplitude Shift Keying

An (link: /terms/infrared text: infrared) communication (link: /terms/protocol text: protocol) developed by (link: /terms/sharp text: Sharp) and used in early Newton devices, including the (link: /terms/original-messagepad text: Original MessagePad), (link: /terms/messagepad-100 text: MessagePad 100), (link: /terms/messagepad-110 text: MessagePad 110), (link: /terms/messagepad-120 text: MessagePad 120), and (link: /terms/messagepad-130 text: MessagePad 130). The ASK protocol is not compatible with the more common (link: /terms/irda text: IrDA) standard, which was implemented in the (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000), (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100), and (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300).

Also referred to as (link: /terms/sharp-ask text: Sharp ASK) and (link: /terms/sharpir text: SharpIR).

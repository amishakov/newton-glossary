Title: AvailWorks

----

Date: 24 November 2017

----

Text:

An integrated, multi-function [application](/terms/application) for Newton devices that includes tools for word processing, [spreadsheets](/terms/spreadsheet), charts, drawing, and full-size page layout, plus a large selection of clip art supplied by [T/Maker](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/T/Maker). Originally developed by Avail Technology, marketing and development of the software was handed over to [PelicanWare](/sources/pelicanware) in 1996.

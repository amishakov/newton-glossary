Title: WatsonEnabler

----

Date: 08 June 2024

----

Text:

A tool based on the Desktop Connection Library or [DCL](/terms/dcl), which appends the appropriate [application signature](/terms/application-signature) required to install [packages](/terms/package) on a [Watson](/terms/watson) device. The Watson was a [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) device based on the [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000) platform with a custom French language [ROM](/terms/rom).

Title: WatsonEnabler

----

Date: 08 June 2024

----

Text:

A tool based on the Desktop Connection Library or (link: /terms/dcl text: DCL), which appends the appropriate (link: /terms/application-signature text: application signature) required to install (link: /terms/package text: packages) on a (link: /terms/watson text: Watson) device. The Watson was a Newton device based on the (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000) platform with a custom French language (link: /terms/rom text: ROM).

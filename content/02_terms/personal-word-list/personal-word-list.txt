Title: Personal Word List

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A editable list of words that (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) uses in addition to the built-in (link: /terms/system-dictionary text: system dictionary). Word lists are used to evaluate text entered by the user when (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) is enabled. The personal word list is also known as a (link: /terms/user-dictionary text: user dictionary) and can contain (link: /terms/recently-written-words text: recently written words) from the (link: /terms/auto-add-dictionary text: auto add dictionary).

The Personal Word List is displayed by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the (link: /terms/dictionary-button text: dictionary button).

Title: Classroom Dock

----

Date: 29 December 2023

----

Text:

An (link: /terms/application text: application) on the (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) used for connecting to the (link: /terms/classroom-exchange text: Classroom Exchange) software running on another computer.

(image: /assets/images/classroom-dock-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of the Classroom Dock application width: 196)

Classroom Dock can be selected as the default (link: /terms/docking text: docking) application by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the (link: /terms/dock-options text: Dock Options) button in the (link: /terms/teacher-setup text: Teacher Setup) slip.

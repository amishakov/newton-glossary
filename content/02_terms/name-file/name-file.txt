Title: Name File

----

Date:

----

Text:

The built-in [application](/terms/application) for storing and organizing information related to a list of contacts. The name, address, telephone number, and other information pertaining to a contact is displayed on a [view](/terms/view) known as a [card](/terms/card). Also referred to as [Names](/terms/names).

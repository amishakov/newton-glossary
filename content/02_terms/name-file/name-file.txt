Title: Name File

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The built-in (link: /terms/application text: application) for storing and organizing information related to a list of contacts. The name, address, telephone number, and other information pertaining to a contact is displayed on a (link: /terms/view text: view) known as a (link: /terms/card text: card).

A card can be created in the Name File for a (link: /terms/person text: person), (link: /terms/company text: company), or (link: /terms/group text: group) of contacts.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/names text: Names).

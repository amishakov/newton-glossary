Title: Untitled Group

----

Date: 15 March 2025

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/view text: view) of information displayed in the (link: /terms/name-file text: Name File) for a (link: /terms/group-address text: group address) that does not have any information entered into the “Group Name” (link: /terms/field text: field).

An “Untitled Group” card will appear listed alphabetically under “U” in the Name File (link: /terms/overview text: overview).

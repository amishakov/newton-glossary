Title: LocalTalk

----

Date:

----

Text:

A specific implementation of the physical layer pertaining to [AppleTalk](/terms/appletalk) networking [protocol](/terms/protocol). Developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) for communications between Mac OS computers and other platforms over a [local area network](/terms/local-area-network), LocalTalk networks commonly use shielded twisted pair cables with self terminating connectors.

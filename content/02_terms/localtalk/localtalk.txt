Title: LocalTalk

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A specific implementation of the physical layer pertaining to (link: /terms/appletalk text: AppleTalk) networking (link: /terms/protocol text: protocol). Developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) for communications between Mac OS computers and other platforms over a (link: /terms/local-area-network text: local area network), LocalTalk networks commonly use shielded twisted pair cables with self terminating connectors.

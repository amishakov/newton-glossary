Title: Unfiled

----

Date: 26 October 2017

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/folder text: folder) containing items which have not been filed in another folder. In the (link: /terms/extras-drawer text: Extras Drawer), this folder is named (link: /terms/unfiled-icons text: Unfiled Icons). In other (link: /terms/application text: applications), this folder will be named according to the type of item being filed. For example, in (link: /terms/newton-works text: Newton Works) there is an (link: /terms/unfiled-documents text: Unfiled Documents) folder and in (link: /terms/calls text: Calls) there is an (link: /terms/unfiled-calls text: Unfiled Calls) folder.

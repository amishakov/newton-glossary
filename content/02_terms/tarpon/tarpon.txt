Title: Tarpon

----

Date:

----

Text:

A ruggedized Newton device developed by [Digital Ocean](https://web.archive.org/web/19970124001013/http://digitalocean.com/) based on the based on the [MessagePad 120](/terms/messagepad-120), but with a [backlit](/terms/backlit) display like the [MessagePad 130](/terms/messagepad-130). Similar to the Digital Ocean [Seahorse](/terms/seahorse), the Tarpon featured technologies such as built-in wireless networking, peer-to-peer voice communication, and optional GPS. The Tarpon is a modified and improved version of the Harris Dracon [SuperTech 2000](/terms/supertech-2000).

A third-party device that utilizes a licensed version of [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) is commonly referred to as a [clone](/terms/clone).

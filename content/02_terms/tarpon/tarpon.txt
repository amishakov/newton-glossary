Title: Tarpon

----

Date: 31 May 2001

----

Text:

A ruggedized Newton device developed by (link: /sources/digital-ocean text: Digital Ocean) based on the based on the (link: /terms/messagepad-120 text: MessagePad 120), but with a (link: /terms/backlit text: backlit) display like the (link: /terms/messagepad-130 text: MessagePad 130). Similar to the Digital Ocean (link: /terms/seahorse text: Seahorse), the Tarpon featured technologies such as built-in wireless networking, peer-to-peer voice communication, and optional GPS. The Tarpon is a modified and improved version of the Harris Dracon (link: /terms/supertech-2000 text: SuperTech 2000).

A third-party device that utilizes a licensed version of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) is commonly referred to as a (link: /terms/clone text: clone).

Title: Routing Slip

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A [view](/terms/view) in which a user specifies the sender, recipient, format, and other information needed to send data by the method chosen in the [action picker](/terms/action-picker). Routing slips are visually identified by a [striped border](/terms/striped-border).

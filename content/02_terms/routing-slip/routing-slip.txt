Title: Routing Slip

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/view text: view) in which a user specifies the sender, recipient, format, and other information needed to send data by the method chosen in the (link: /terms/action-picker text: action picker). Routing slips are visually identified by a (link: /terms/striped-border text: striped border).

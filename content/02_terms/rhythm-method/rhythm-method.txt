Title: Rhythm Method

----

Date:

----

Text:

The process of repeatedly removing and inserting a rechargeable [battery pack](/terms/battery-pack) from a Newton device to stimulate charging.

Can also refer to repeatedly unplugging and reinserting an AC power adapter into the [power port](/terms/power-port) of a Newton device.

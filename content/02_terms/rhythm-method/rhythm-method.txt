Title: Rhythm Method

----

Date: 01 December 2016

----

Text:

The process of repeatedly removing and inserting a rechargeable (link: /terms/battery-pack text: battery pack) from a Newton device to stimulate (link: /terms/charging text: charging).

Can also refer to repeatedly unplugging and reinserting an AC power adapter into the (link: /terms/power-port text: power port) of a Newton device.

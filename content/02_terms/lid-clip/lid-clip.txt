Title: Lid Clip

----

Date: 04 August 2024

----

Text:

A small, spring-loaded protuberance located on the back of (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000), and (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100) devices. A pair of clips hold the (link: /terms/lid text: lid) in place when it has been folded over to expose the (link: /terms/screen text: screen).

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/door-clip text: Door Clip), (link: /terms/door-clasp text: Door Clasp), or (link: /terms/lid-clasp text: Lid Clasp).

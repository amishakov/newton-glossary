Title: Deutsche Newton

----

Date: 19 November 2018

----

Text:

A colloquial term referring to a Newton device running a German version of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS). German localized operating systems were available for the (link: /terms/original-messagepad text: Original MessagePad), (link: /terms/messagepad-100 text: MessagePad 100), (link: /terms/messagepad-120 text: MessagePad 120), (link: /terms/messagepad-130 text: MessagePad 130), (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100), and certain third-party Newton devices such as the Siemens (link: /terms/notephone text: NotePhone).

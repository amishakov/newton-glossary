Title: Dimmed

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

Describes words, objects, or other elements of the (link: /terms/user-interface text: user interface) that appear grey or dimmer than the rest of the interface. In a Newton (link: /terms/application text: application), text or objects (such as (link: /terms/button text: buttons)) that are disabled or inactive are generally hidden rather than dimmed.

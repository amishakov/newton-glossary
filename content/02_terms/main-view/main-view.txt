Title: Main View

----

Date:

----

Text:

A principal [container view](/terms/container-view) that serves as a center of user operations for an [application](/terms/application).

Every main view must have a [view border](/terms/view-border), even if the border is not visible because the [view](/terms/view) fills the entire screen. 

Generally, the main view of an application will have either a [matte border](/terms/matte-border) or a [plain border](/terms/plain-border).

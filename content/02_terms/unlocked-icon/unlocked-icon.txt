Title: Unlocked Icon

----

Date: 29 July 2024

----

Text:

An image of an open lock used to indicate that data contained on a (link: /terms/store text: store) or in a (link: /terms/soup text: soup) entry is located on writable (link: /terms/storage-card text: storage card).

For example, a (link: /terms/card-slip text: card slip) will display an unlocked icon if the (link: /terms/write-protection-switch text: write-protection switch) on a storage card is in the unlocked position.

Also referred to as an (link: /terms/unlocked-switch-icon text: Unlocked Switch Icon).

Title: Unlocked Icon

----

Date: 29 July 2024

----

Text:

An image of a open lock used to indicate that data contained on a [store](/terms/store) or in a [soup](/terms/soup) entry is located on writable [storage card](/terms/storage-card).

For example, a [card slip](/terms/card-slip) will display an unlocked icon if the [write-protection switch](/terms/write-protection-switch) on a storage card is in the unlocked position.

Also referred to as an [Unlocked Switch Icon](/terms/unlocked-switch-icon).

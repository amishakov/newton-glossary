Title: Secretary Station

----

Date: 24 July 2025

----

Text:

A system utilized by an attendant for answering calls and handling other telecommunication requests on behalf of a forwarded account.

The Siemens (link: /terms/notephone text: NotePhone) has been referred to as a secretary station, likely due to its various telephony features.

Title: Leibniz

----

Date: 29 June 2017

----

Text:

An early development environment for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) created by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) that consisted of (link: /terms/macintosh-common-lisp text: Macintosh Common Lisp), a compiler for the (link: /terms/ralph text: Ralph) programming language, and other tools.

Leibniz is also an emulator for (link: /terms/runt text: Runt)-based Newton devices currently being developed by (link: /sources/steve-white text: Steve White).

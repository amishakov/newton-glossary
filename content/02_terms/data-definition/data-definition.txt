Title: Data Definition

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), a (link: /terms/frame text: frame) containing (link: /terms/slot text: slots) that define a particular type of data and the (link: /terms/method text: methods) that operate on it. The (link: /terms/entry text: entries) defined are used by an (link: /terms/application text: application) and stored in its (link: /terms/soup text: soup). A data definition is registered with the system. The shortened term (link: /terms/datadef text: dataDef) is sometimes used.

Title: NewtonScript

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A dynamic, object-oriented programming language developed for the Newton platform. Lovingly nicknamed [WallyScript](/terms/wallyscript) after its designer [Walter Smith](/sources/walter-smith).

Also referred to as the [NS](/terms/ns).

Title: C++ Heap

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

An area of (link: /terms/system-memory text: system memory) used by the (link: /terms/operating-system text: operating system) specifically by native program (link: /terms/function text: functions). Sometimes this part of the (link: /terms/heap text: heap) is confused with all areas of system memory which are not part of the (link: /terms/newtonscript-heap text: NewtonScript heap).

Title: Corrector Button

----

Date: 18 December 2023

----

Text:

[Tapping](/terms/tapping)) the corrector button in the [correction picker](/terms/correction-picker) brings up a [corrector view](/terms/corrector-view), in which the user can make corrections to individual letters.

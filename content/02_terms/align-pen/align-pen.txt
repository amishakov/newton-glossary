Title: Align Pen

----

Date: 26 June 2024

----

Text:

An option located in the [Handwriting Recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) section of the [Prefs](/terms/prefs) application which guides the user on how to [realign](/terms/realign) the [pen](/terms/pen) by [tapping](/terms/tapping) specific locations on the [screen](/terms/screen).

Title: Newton Industry Association

----

Date: 15 February 2024

----

Text:

A consortium formed by Apple and major third party partners (including Matsushita and Motorola) to promote the Newton as standard open [PDA](/terms/pda) platform. The primary focus of the association was to promote the growth and interoperability of the Newton platform and related devices, as well as wireless communication, telephony, and office automation standards.

Also referred to as the [NIA](/terms/nia).

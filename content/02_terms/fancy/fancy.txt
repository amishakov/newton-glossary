Title: Fancy

----

Date: 10 February 2017

----

Text:

One of the default bitmap [fonts](/terms/font) on the Newton. Fancy is a proportionally spaced serif typeface based on [Times Roman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Times_New_Roman).

Title: Screen

----

Date: 19 December 2016

----

Text:

A hardware component that displays information on a generally flat, rectangular surface. The screen on a Newton device is a (link: /terms/liquid-crystal-display text: liquid crystal display) with an integrated (link: /terms/touch-screen text: touch screen).

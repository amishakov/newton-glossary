Title: Assist

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A function built into Newton device that can automatically perform certain tasks, such as dialing a telephone number, searching for information, or scheduling an appointment. Assist is accessed by entering a (link: /terms/request-word text: request word) into the (link: /terms/assist-slip text: assist slip) triggering the corresponding (link: /terms/action text: action). For example, entering the “call” request word into the assist slip will trigger an action to dial a telephone number.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/assistant text: Assistant) and (link: /terms/newton-assistant text: Newton Assistant) in earlier versions of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS).

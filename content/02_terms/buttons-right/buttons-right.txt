Title: Buttons Right

----

Date: 03 January 2025

----

Text:

An option in the (link: /terms/rotate-button text: Rotate Button) picker which moves the (link: /terms/button-bar text: button bar) to the right side when the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) is in (link: /terms/landscape-mode text: landscape mode).

If the button bar is currently displayed on the right side of the screen, the option will be named (link: /terms/buttons-left text: Buttons Left).

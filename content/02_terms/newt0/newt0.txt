Title: NEWT/0

----

Date: 05 June 2024

----

Text:

A prototype-oriented [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript) interpreter and compiler developed by [Makoto “gnue” Nukui](/sources/gnue) designed to run on various modern computing platforms.

[Matthias Melcher](/sources/matthias-melcher) also developed a version of Newt/0 designed to run on 64-bit processors called [NEWT/64](/terms/newt64).

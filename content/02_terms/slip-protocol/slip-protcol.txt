Title: SLIP

----

Date: 20-Mar-2024

----

Text:

Serial Line Internet Protocol

Software that allows the (link: /terms/ip text: IP) network layer, commonly used over (link: /terms/ethernet text: ethernet), to be used over a serial connection such as a (link: /terms/modem text: modem).

SLIP was superceded by (link: /terms/ppp text: PPP).

For the user interface element, see (link: /terms/slip text: Slip).

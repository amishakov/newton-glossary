Title: Transceiver

----

Date: 11 July 2024

----

Text:

A portmanteau of transmitter and receiver, describing any device that performs both transmitting and receiving functions.

For example, all [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) devices include an [infrared](/terms/infrared) transceiver for [beaming](/terms/beaming) information to and receiving information from other devices.

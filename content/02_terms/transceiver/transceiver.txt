Title: Transceiver

----

Date: 11 July 2024

----

Text:

A portmanteau of transmitter and receiver, describing any device that performs both transmitting and receiving functions.

For example, all Newton devices include an (link: /terms/infrared text: infrared) transceiver for (link: /terms/beaming text: beaming) information to and receiving information from other devices.

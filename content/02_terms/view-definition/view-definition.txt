Title: View Definition

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), a view (link: /terms/template text: template) that defines how to display data from a particular (link: /terms/data-definition text: data definition). A view definition is registered with the system under the name of the data definition to which it applies. The shortened term (link: /terms/viewdef text: viewDef) is sometimes used.

Title: Event

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

An entry in the (link: /terms/date-book text: Date Book) application corresponding to a day, but not a particular time during that day.

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), an event is an (link: /terms/action text: action) of significance occurring during a task or within an (link: /terms/application text: application) program, such as a key being pressed on a (link: /terms/keyboard text: keyboard) or a (link: /terms/button text: button) being (link: /terms/tapped text: tapped) on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen).

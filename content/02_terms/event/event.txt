Title: Event

----

Date:

----

Text:

An entry in the [Date Book](/terms/date-book) application corresponding to a day, but not a particular time during that day.

In programming, an event is an [action](/terms/action) of significance occurring during a task or within an [application](/terms/application) program, such as a key being pressed on a [keyboard](/terms/keyboard) or a [button](/terms/button) being [tapped](/terms/tapped) on the [screen](/terms/screen).

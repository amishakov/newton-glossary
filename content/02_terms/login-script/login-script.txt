Title: Login Script

----

Date: 27 October 2024

----

Text:

A series of commands used to initiate a connection between a communication device and an (link: /terms/isp text: ISP).

For example, a login script may be required to access the internet over a telephone line using a (link: /terms/modem text: modem).

To terminate a connection, you would use a (link: /terms/logout-script text: logout script).

Title: Accessory Card

----

Date: 19 December 2016

----

Text:

A removable (link: /terms/pc-card text: PC card) that provides additional functionality, such as a (link: /terms/modem text: modem) for serial communication or an (link: /terms/ethernet text: ethernet) card for networking.

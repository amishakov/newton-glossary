Secure Sockets Layer

A security (link: /terms/protocol text: protocol) used to validate the identity of websites and create encrypted connections over the internet. Originally developed by [Netscape](https://web.archive.org/web/19961020015116/http://www.netscape.com/), SSL was superseded by (link: /terms/tls text: TLS) (Transport Layer Security).

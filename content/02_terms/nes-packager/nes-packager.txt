Title: NES Packager

----

Date: 05 November 2017

----

Text:

An online utility created by [Victor Rehorst](/sources/victor-rehorst) that takes a [Nintendo Entertainment System](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nintendo_Entertainment_System) (NES) [ROM](/terms/rom) image and creates a [package](/terms/package) that can be used with the [Newtendo](/terms/newtendo) emulator. The NES Packager is based on the [DCL](/terms/dcl) communication framework.

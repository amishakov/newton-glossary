Title: NES Packager

----

Date: 05 November 2017

----

Text:

An online utility created by (link: /sources/victor-rehorst text: Victor Rehorst) that takes a [Nintendo Entertainment System](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nintendo_Entertainment_System) (NES) (link: /terms/rom text: ROM) image and creates a (link: /terms/package text: package) that can be used with the (link: /terms/newtendo text: Newtendo) emulator. The NES Packager is based on the (link: /terms/dcl text: DCL) communication framework.

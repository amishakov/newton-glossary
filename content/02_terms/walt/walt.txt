Title: WALT

----

Date: 12 June 2001

----

Text:

Wizzy Active Lifestyle Telephone

An experimental telecommunication device developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) and [BellSouth](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BellSouth) around the same time as the (link: /terms/original-messagepad text: Original MessagePad). It featured a (link: /terms/touch-screen text: touch screen) display and (link: /terms/pen text: pen) similar to a Newton device, but with a [HyperCard](https://hypercard.org/)-based (link: /terms/user-interface text: user interface) running on top of the Macintosh System 6.0.8 (link: /terms/operating-system text: operating system).

The WALT has been referred to as “… a Mac in Newton’s clothing.”

Title: Caret Picker

----

Date: 05 December 2017

----

Text:

A [picker](/terms/picker) that appears when the user [taps](/terms/tap) the [caret](/terms/caret). The caret picker allows the user to easily input punctuation marks, spaces, paragraph breaks, or other special characters at the text [insertion point](/terms/insertion-point) as indicated by the caret.

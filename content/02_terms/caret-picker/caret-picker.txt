Title: Caret Picker

----

Date: 05 December 2017

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/picker text: picker) that appears when the user (link: /terms/tap text: taps) the (link: /terms/caret text: caret). The caret picker allows the user to easily (link: /terms/input text: input) punctuation marks, spaces, paragraph breaks, or other special (link: /terms/character text: characters) at the text (link: /terms/insertion-point text: insertion point) as indicated by the caret.

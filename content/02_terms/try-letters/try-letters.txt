Title: Try Letters

----

Date: 07 November 2017

----

Text:

A [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) option that uses [letter-by-letter](/terms/letter-by-letter) recognition instead of the [dictionary](/terms/dictionary) to interpret a word. The “Try Letters” [button](/terms/button) appears in a [picker](/terms/picker) when the user [double-taps](/terms/double-tap) a word.

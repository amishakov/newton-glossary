Title: Try Letters

----

Date: 07 November 2017

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) option that uses (link: /terms/letter-by-letter text: letter-by-letter) recognition instead of the (link: /terms/dictionary text: dictionary) to interpret a word when (link: /terms/cursive text: Cursive) is selected as the writing style. The “Try Letters” (link: /terms/button text: button) appears in the (link: /terms/correction-picker text: correction picker) when the user (link: /terms/double-tap text: double-taps) a word.

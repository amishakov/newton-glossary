Title: Try Letters

----

Date: 07 November 2017

----

Text:

A [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) option that uses [letter-by-letter](/terms/letter-by-letter) recognition instead of the [dictionary](/terms/dictionary) to interpret a word when [Cursive](/terms/cursive) is selected as the writing style. The “Try Letters” [button](/terms/button) appears in the [correction picker](/terms/correction-picker) when the user [double taps](/terms/double-tap) a word.

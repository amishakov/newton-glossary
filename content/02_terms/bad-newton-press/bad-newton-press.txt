Title: Bad Newton Press

----

Date:

----

Text:

The reporting and perpetuating of inaccurate or misleading information regarding the Newton platform by the media. Also referred to as [BNP](/terms/bnp), the term was coined in 2003 by Doug Parker as part of [The Newton Manifesto](http://marc.info/?l=newtontalk&m=107124023123980&w=2).

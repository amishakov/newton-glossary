Title: Bad Newton Press

----

Date:

----

Text:

The reporting and perpetuating of inaccurate or misleading information regarding the Newton platform by the media. Also referred to as (link: /terms/bnp text: BNP), the term was coined in 2003 by Doug Parker as part of [The Newton Manifesto](https://newtontalk.net/archive/newtontalk/2003-December/0559.html).

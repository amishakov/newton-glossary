Title: Blinking Line

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A flashing vertical line indicating where new text will be inserted. More commonly referred to as a (link: /terms/cursor text: cursor). The blinking line is only used in (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) 1.3 and earlier. Later versions of Newton OS use a (link: /terms/caret text: caret) cursor to indicate where new text will be inserted.

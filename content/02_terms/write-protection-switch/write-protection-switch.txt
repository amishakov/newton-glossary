Title: Write-Protection Switch

----

Date: 17 July 2024

----

Text:

A small, sliding lever located on a [storage card](/terms/storage-card) used to prevent storing, moving, deleting, or changing information on the card.

Also referred to as a [card lock switch](/terms/card-lock-switch), [protection switch](/terms/protection-switch) or [write-protect switch](/terms/write-protect-switch).

Title: Write-Protection Switch

----

Date: 17 July 2024

----

Text:

A small, sliding lever located on a (link: /terms/storage-card text: storage card) used to prevent storing, moving, deleting, or changing information on the card.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/card-lock-switch text: Card Lock Switch), (link: /terms/protection-switch text: Protection Switch), or (link: /terms/write-protect-switch text: Write-Protect Switch).

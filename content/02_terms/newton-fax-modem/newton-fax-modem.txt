Title: Newton Fax Modem

----

Date: 17 March 2018

----

Text:

A lightweight, portable (link: /terms/modem text: modem) designed for use with early Newton devices. Released by Apple in 1993, the Newton Fax Modem could be powered by a pair of AA batteries or a power adapter. Nicknamed the (link: /terms/cigarette-pack text: cigarette pack) because its dimensions were similar to that of a package of cigarettes, it was also the unlikely star of the infamous (link: /terms/modeminos text: Modeminos) video.

Text:

Internet Message Access Protocol

A standard protocol used to retrieve email messages from a mail server over a (link: /terms/tcp-ip text: TCP/IP) connection. IMAP was designed to enable complete management of an email account using multiple clients.

(link: /terms/mail-v text: Mail V) is a (link: /terms/application text: application) that uses IMAP to access email on a Newton device.

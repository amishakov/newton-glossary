Title: Application Signature

----

Date: 31 May 2001

----

Text:

A 4 to 10 (link: /terms/character text: character) designation that uniquely identifies the creator or developer of a Newton (link: /terms/application text: application). This signature is embedded in an application when it is (link: /terms/compiled text: compiled) into a (link: /terms/package text: package).

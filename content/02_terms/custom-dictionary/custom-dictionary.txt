Title: Custom Dictionary

----

Date: 14 October 2017

----

Text:

A supplemental [dictionary](/terms/dictionary) used by a third-party [application](/terms/application) to facilitate the recognition of specialized vocabulary such as medical or legal terms. Built-in [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) applications such as [Name File](/terms/name-file) and [Date Book](/terms/date-book) cannot use custom dictionaries created for third-party applications.

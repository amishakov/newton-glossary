Title: Custom Dictionary

----

Date: 14 October 2017

----

Text:

A supplemental (link: /terms/dictionary text: dictionary) used by a third-party (link: /terms/application text: application) to facilitate the recognition of specialized vocabulary such as medical or legal terms. Built-in (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) applications such as (link: /terms/name-file text: Name File) and (link: /terms/date-book text: Date Book) cannot use custom dictionaries created for third-party applications.

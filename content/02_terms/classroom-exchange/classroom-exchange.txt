Title: Classroom Exchange

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

Software developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) that facilitates the connection of one or more [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) devices to a Mac OS computer and the simultaneous transferring of information between those devices.

Also referred to as [eMate Classroom Exchange](/terms/emate-classroom-exchange).

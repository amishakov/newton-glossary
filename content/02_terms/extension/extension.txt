Title: Extension

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/package text: package) that adds some sort of functionality to the (link: /terms/operating-system text: operating system) or other packages.

The (link: /terms/filename-extension text: filename extension) of a file is also referred to as an extension.

Title: Extension

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A [package](/terms/package) that adds some sort of functionality to the [operating system](/terms/operating-system) or other packages.

The [filename extension](/terms/filename-extension) of a file is also referred to as simply an extension.

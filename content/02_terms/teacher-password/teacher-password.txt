Title: Teacher Password

----

Date: 30 May 2025

----

Text:

A unique word consisting of several (link: /terms/character text: characters) that allows secure access to the (link: /terms/teacher-setup text: Teacher Setup) application on an (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300).

When the Teacher Setup (link: /terms/application text: application) is opened, the user is promoted to enter a password into the (link: /terms/password-slip text: password slip) before continuing.

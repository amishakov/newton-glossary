Title: Small Latch

----

Date: 07 August 2024

----

Text:

A [latch](/terms/latch) located on the [lid](/terms/lid) of [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000) and [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100) devices which unfastens the hinge, allowing the lid to be removed.

Also referred to as a [Latch Button](/terms/latch-button).

Newton Tech Info Library

An alternative index of [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) support documents compiled and maintained by C.W. Otto Sohn. NewTIL was based on the defunct (link: /terms/til text: Tech Info Library) and focused specifically on Newton-related topics.

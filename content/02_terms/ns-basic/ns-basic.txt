Title: NS BASIC

----

Date: 06 July 2025

----

Text:

An implementation of the [BASIC](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BASIC) programming language for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS). Developed by the NS BASIC Corporation based in Toronto, the software was also available for other platforms such as Palm OS and Windows CE.

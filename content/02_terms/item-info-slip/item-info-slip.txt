Title: Item Info Slip

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A [container view](/terms/container-view) that displays statistics and related information for an item. This information includes the title, type, creation date, size, and [store](/terms/store) location of the item. A user can [edit](/terms/edit) an itemâ€™s title in the [slip](/terms/slip).

Also known as an [info slip](/terms/info-slip).

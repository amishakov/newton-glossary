Title: Scrub

----

Date: 24 May 2001

Text:

To erase or delete words or images using a (link: /terms/zig-zag text: zig-zag) gesture with the (link: /terms/pen text: pen). Also referred to as a (link: /terms/scribble text: scribble) gesture in the beta version of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) included on the (link: /terms/notepad text: Newton Notepad) device.

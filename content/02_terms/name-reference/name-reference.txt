Title: Name Reference

----

Date:

----

Text:

A [frame](/terms/frame) that contains a [soup](/terms/soup) entry or an [alias](/terms/alias) to a soup [entry](/terms/entry) which is often — though not necessarily — from the [Name File](/terms/name-file) soup. The frame may also contain some of the individual [slots](/terms/slot) from the soup entry.

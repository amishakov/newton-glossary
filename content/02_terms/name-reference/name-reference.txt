Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), a (link: /terms/frame text: frame) that contains a (link: /terms/soup text: soup) entry or an (link: /terms/alias text: alias) to a soup (link: /terms/entry text: entry) which is often — though not necessarily — from the (link: /terms/name-file text: Name File) soup. The frame may also contain some of the individual (link: /terms/slot text: slots) from the soup entry.

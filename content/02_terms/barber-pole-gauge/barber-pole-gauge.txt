Title: Barber Pole Gauge

----

Date: 20 September 2024

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/user-interface text: user interface) element consisting of a horizontal rectangle containing animated diagonal stripes. The animation acts a visual (link: /terms/indicator text: indicator) of an operation in progress, but does not indicate how much of the operation has been completed.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/barber text: Barber) or (link: /terms/barber-pole text: Barber Pole).

Title: View Class

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), a primitive building block on which a (link: /terms/view text: view) is based. All view (link: /terms/proto text: protos) are based directly or indirectly (through another proto) on a view (link: /terms/class text: class). The view class of a view is specified in the `viewClass` (link: /terms/slot text: slot) of its (link: /terms/template text: template) or proto.

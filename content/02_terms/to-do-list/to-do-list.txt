Title: To Do List

----

Date: 09 November 2017

----

Text:

A daily (link: /terms/checklist text: checklist) in the (link: /terms/date-book text: Date Book) application which allows the user to create and prioritize tasks. Tasks are marked as completed by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) a (link: /terms/checkbox text: checkbox). Uncompleted tasks are carried over to the next day.

Title: Keyboard Button

----

Date: 31 August 2017

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/button text: button) that displays an onscreen (link: /terms/keyboard text: keyboard) for entering text and other types of information. When (link: /terms/tapped text: tapped), the default (link: /terms/typewriter text: Typewriter) keyboard appears. (link: /terms/tapping text: Tapping) the button again while the keyboard is onscreen will display a (link: /terms/picker text: picker) listing other types of keyboards, including (link: /terms/numeric text: Numeric), (link: /terms/phone text: Phone), and (link: /terms/time-date text: Time/Date).

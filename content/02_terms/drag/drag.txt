Title: Drag

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

To place the (link: /terms/pen text: pen) on a movable object — such as a (link: /terms/slider text: slider) control or (link: /terms/container-view text: container view) — then slide the pen to move the object and finally lift the pen to stop moving the object.

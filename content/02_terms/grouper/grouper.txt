Title: Grouper

----

Date: 17 July 2017

----

Text:

A family of radio-based devices developed by [Digital Ocean](/sources/digital-ocean) that provided [wireless AppleTalk](/terms/wireless-appletalk) connectivity to [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) and Mac OS computers over a [local area network](/terms/local-area-network).

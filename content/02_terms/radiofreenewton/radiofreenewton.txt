Title: radiofreenewton

----

Date: 07 May 2026

----

Text:

A website instigated by [Jesse Garnier](https://www.stonehopper.com/) that provided an MP3 stream designed for playback on a Newton device using Eckhart Köppen’s (link: /terms/mad-max text: MAD Max) application. The stream consisted of a playlist containing approximately 4,000 songs.

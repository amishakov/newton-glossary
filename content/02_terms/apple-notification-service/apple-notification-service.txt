Title: Apple Notification Service

----

Date: 18 January 2024

----

Text:

A service that provided the ability to send messages via telephone, fax, or computer equipped with a (link: /terms/modem text: modem) using a personal 800 number. The user would receive notification of incoming faxes, voicemail, email, and text messages on a (link: /terms/pagecard text: PageCard) inserted into their Newton device, PowerBook laptop, or other compatible computer.

The Apple Notification Service was part of the (link: /terms/apple-mobile-message-system text: Apple Mobile Message System) and was replaced by the (link: /terms/swims text: SWiMS) service provided by [Socket Communications](https://web.archive.org/web/19980208233349/http://www.socketcom.com/), makers of the PageCard.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/ans text: ANS).

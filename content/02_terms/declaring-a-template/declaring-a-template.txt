Title: Declaring a Template

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript), registering a [template](/terms/template) in another [view](/terms/view) (usually its [parent](/terms/parent)) so that the template view is preallocated when the other view is opened. This allows access to [methods](/terms/method) and [slots](/terms/slot) in the declared view.

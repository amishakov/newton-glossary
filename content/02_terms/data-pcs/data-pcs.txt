----

Date: 16 July 2025

Text:

Data Personal Communication Services

A radio service proposed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) in 1991 that would have provided local area, high speed data communication supporting collaborative computing and networking among portable devices, including (link: /terms/laptop-computer text: laptops) and (link: /terms/personal-digital-assistant text: personal digital assistants) such as the Newton (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad).

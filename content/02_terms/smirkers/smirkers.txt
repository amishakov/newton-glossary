Title: Smirkers

----

Date:

----

Text:

The group of skeptical [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) employees working on the Newton project who doubted the feasibility and operability of both the larger [Newton Plus](/terms/newton-plus) device and the [Dylan](/terms/dylan) programming language used to develop the [operating system](/terms/operating-system).

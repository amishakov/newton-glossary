Title: Smirkers

----

Date: 12 June 2001

----

Text:

The group of skeptical [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) employees working on the Newton project who doubted the feasibility and operability of both the larger (link: /terms/newton-plus text: Newton Plus) device and the (link: /terms/dylan text: Dylan) programming language used to develop the (link: /terms/operating-system text: operating system).

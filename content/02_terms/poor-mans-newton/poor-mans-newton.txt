Title: Poor Man’s Newton

----

Date: 26 May 2025

----

Text:

A [HyperCard](https://hypercard.org/) stack created by Joseph Cicinelli that simulated some of the features found in Newton OS, such as storing contacts in the (link: /terms/name-file text: Name File), adding notes to the (link: /terms/notepad text: Notepad), and viewing a (link: /terms/mini-calendar text: Mini-Calendar) as found in the (link: /terms/date-book text: Date Book) application.

Title: Stowaway

----

Date: 17 February 2025

----

Text:

A shorthand reference to the [Stowaway Portable Keyboard](https://web.archive.org/web/20011214024411/http://www.thinkoutside.com/products.asp), a foldable external keyboard designed by (link: /sources/think-outside text: Think Outside) for use with mobile computing devices.

The Stowaway keyboard could be connected to a Newton device using a (link: /terms/newtway text: Newtway) adapter and (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) driver (link: /terms/extension text: extension) developed by (link: /sources/daniel-padilla text: Daniel Padilla).

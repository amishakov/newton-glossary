Title: People Picker

----

Date: 10 January 2018

----

Text:

A list containing the names of people and companies from the (link: /terms/name-file text: Name File) and (link: /terms/owner-info text: Owner Info) applications. Along with each name, the (link: /terms/picker text: picker) can display a telephone number, fax number, or email address.

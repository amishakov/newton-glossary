Title: People Picker

----

Date: 10 January 2018

----

Text:

A list containing the names of people and companies from the [Name File](/terms/name-file) and [Owner Info](/terms/owner-info) applications. Along with each name, the [picker](/terms/picker) can display a telephone number, fax number, or email address.

Newt Application Tutorial

A interactive (link: /terms/newton-book text: Newton book) created by (link: /sources/steve-weyer text: Steve Weyer) that describes how to develop a simple (link: /terms/application text: applications) directly on a Newton device using (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript) and the (link: /terms/newtdevenv text: NewtDevEnv) application development framework.

NewtATut is based on an article that first appeared in (link: /terms/pie-developers text: PIE Developers) magazine.

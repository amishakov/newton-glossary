Title: Redo

----

Date: 24 May 2024

----

Text:

The opposite of [undo](/terms/undo).

In most situations, [tapping](/terms/tapping) the Undo [icon](/terms/icon) in the [button bar](/terms/button-bar) reverts the most recent action. On [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) 2.0 devices, tapping the Undo icon a second time will perform the original action again.

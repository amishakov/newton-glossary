Title: YakSpeakNote

----

Date: 05 December 2017

----

Text:

A [package](/terms/package) that works in conjunction with [Yak](/terms/yak) to provide a way to send [recognized text](/terms/recognized-text) to the [text-to-speech](/terms/text-to-speech) engine via the [routing button](/terms/routing-button) in the [Notepad](/terms/notepad) application.

YakSpeakNote was developed by [Matej Horvat](/sources/matej-horvat) as a replacement for the original (and rather buggy) [NoteYak](/terms/noteyak) software.

Title: XTND

----

Date:

Text:

A system of file format translators developed by [Claris Corporation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Claris) used in a variety of Mac OS [applications](/terms/application) including [Newton Press](/terms/newton-press).

Also referred to as [Claris XTND](/terms/claris-xtnd).

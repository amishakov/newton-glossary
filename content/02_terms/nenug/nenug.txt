Title: NENUG

----

Date: 11 February 2017

----

Text:

North of England Newton Users Group

A user group operated [Jonathan Sanderson](https://web.archive.org/web/20041217232036/http://www.quern.demon.co.uk/jonathan/) and David Yates. Also referred to as (link: /terms/pondlife text: Pondlife), NENUG was reportedly the largest Newton and handheld computer user group in the United Kingdom.

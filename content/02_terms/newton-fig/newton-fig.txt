Title: Newton FIG

----

Date: 27 October 2024

----

Text:

The (link: /terms/special-interest-group text: special interest group) for Newton users run by the [Arizona Macintosh Users Group](http://amug.org/) (AMUG).

The “FIG” stands for “Fun Interest Group” and plays on the name of the infamous “Fig Newton” cookie.

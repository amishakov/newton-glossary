Title: Filename Extension

----

Date: 30 August 2024

----

Text:

The portion of the name of a file that follows the final point (or “.” character) that indicates the type of data stored in the file.

For example, a Newton [package](/terms/package) file is identified by either the [.pkg](/terms/pkg) or [.newtonpkg](/terms/newtonpkg) filename extension.

Also referred to as a [File Extension](/terms/file-extension) or [Extension](/terms/extension).

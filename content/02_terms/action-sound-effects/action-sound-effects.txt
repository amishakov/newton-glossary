Title: Action Sound Effects

----

Date: 10 June 2025

----

Text:

Sounds that are played in conjunction with certain (link: /terms/user-interface text: user interface) interactions.

For example, a (link: /terms/sound-effect text: sound effect) is played when the (link: /terms/extras-drawer text: Extras Drawer) is opened and closed, or when text is (link: /terms/scrubbed text: scrubbed) out in the (link: /terms/notepad text: Notepad) application, a “(link: /terms/poof text: poof)” sound is played.

Action sound effects can be turned on and off in the (link: /terms/sound text: Sound) section of the (link: /terms/prefs text: Prefs) application.

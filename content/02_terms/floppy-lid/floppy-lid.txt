Title: Floppy Lid

----

Date: 06 October 2002

----

Text:

The result of the hinge tension spring failing in the (link: /terms/clamshell text: clamshell) lid of an (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300). The tension spring will normally hold the lid and (link: /terms/screen text: screen) at any given angle. When the tension spring fails, the lid flops down. The cause of floppy lip to referred to as the (link: /terms/spring-issue text: spring issue) and the “dreaded hinge problem” or (link: /terms/dhp text: DHP).

Title: Beeper Number

----

Date: 01 December 2016

----

Text:

An alternate name for the seven-digit personal identification number or (link: /terms/pin text: PIN) associated with an (link: /terms/apple-wireless-messaging-service text: Apple Wireless Messaging Service) account. The term “beeper” is derived from the sound made by old school wireless paging devices when receiving a message.

Title: Bobby

----

Date:

----

Text:

An [application](/terms/application) created by [Jason Rukman](/sources/jason-rukman) used during [device driver](/terms/device-driver) development, specifically isolated testing of [Lantern](/terms/lantern) drivers on a Newton device.

The name “Bobby” is a nod to London police officers who would use lanterns when patrolling or inspecting at night.

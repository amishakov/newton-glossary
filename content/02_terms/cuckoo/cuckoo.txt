Title: Cuckoo

----

Date: 06 September 2024

----

Text:

An (link: /terms/easter-egg text: easter egg) hidden in the preferences (link: /terms/slip text: slip) of the (link: /terms/date-book text: Date Book) application that plays a “cuckoo” sound while displaying the names of three people who worked on the (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) — Scott Jenson, Fred Tou, and Jeremy Wyld.

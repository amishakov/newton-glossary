Title: Routing

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The process of sending or receiving data through the (link: /terms/inout-box text: InOut Box) application using a communications (link: /terms/transport text: transport) such as printing, faxing, (link: /terms/beaming text: beaming), or emailing. Routing is generally initiated by using one of the options found in the (link: /terms/action-picker text: action picker).

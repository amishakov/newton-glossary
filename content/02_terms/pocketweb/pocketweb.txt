Title: PocketWeb

----

Date:

----

Text:

The first web browser available for use on a handheld device. PocketWeb was specifically designed for the Newton platform by the [PDA Developers Group](http://www.teco.edu/pda/) at [TECO](http://www.teco.edu/) in 1994.

PocketWeb was initially (and rather awkwardly) known as [NewtonWWW](/terms/newtonwww).

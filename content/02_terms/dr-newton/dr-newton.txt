Title: Dr Newton

----

Date: 27 October 2024

----

Text:

The nom de plume of David Watson, an engineering technician at (link: /sources/digital-ocean text: Digital Ocean), one of the companies that licensed (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) and manufactured devices based on the Newton platform. Watson was heavily involved with development of the (link: /terms/seahorse text: Seahorse), a wireless device based on the (link: /terms/messagepad-130 text: MessagePad 130). After Digital Ocean was shuttered, Watson sold spare parts and provided repair services for Newton devices for several years.

He has been mistakenly referred to as (link: /terms/dr-watson text: Dr Watson) on the (link: /terms/newtontalk text: NewtonTalk) mailing list.

Title: Motherboard

----

Date:

----

Text:

A more common term for the [main logic board](/terms/main-logic-board). The motherboard is primary circuit board containing the processor chip and the system [ROM](/terms/rom) inside a Newton device. A secondary circuit board or [daughterboard](/terms/daughterboard) may also be attached to the motherboard.

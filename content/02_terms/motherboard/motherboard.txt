Title: Motherboard

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A more common term for the (link: /terms/main-logic-board text: main logic board). The motherboard is primary circuit board containing the processor chip and the system (link: /terms/rom text: ROM) inside a Newton device. A secondary circuit board or (link: /terms/daughterboard text: daughterboard) can also be attached to the motherboard.

Title: Orientation

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The position of the content within the (link: /terms/display-area text: display area) of a Newton device relative to the device itself. Content can be displayed in either horizontal (landscape) or vertical (portrait) orientation.

The orientation can be changed by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the (link: /terms/rotate-button text: Rotate Button).

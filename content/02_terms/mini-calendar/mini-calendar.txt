Title: Mini-Calendar

----

Date: 28 December 2024

----

Text:

A small monthly (link: /terms/calendar text: calendar) displayed at the top of the (link: /terms/date-book text: Date Book) application that provides a way to navigate quickly between months.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/dates-navigator text: Dates Navigator) in the (link: /terms/newton-connection text: Newton Connection) application.

Worldwide Newton Association

A defunct legal entity formed in April 2004 to promote the Newton platform and gather users and developers together. The WWNA was primarily responsible for organizing several editions of the Worldwide Newton Conference ((link: /terms/wwnc text: WWNC)) between 2004 and 2009.

The initial membership of the association included such Newton luminaries as (link: /sources/walter-smith text: Walter Smith), (link: /sources/larry-yaeger text: Larry Yaeger), (link: /sources/paul-guyot text: Paul Guyot), (link: /sources/adam-tow text: Adam Tow), and [John Sculley](https://web.archive.org/web/20241123053001/https://www.sculleyspeaks.com/).

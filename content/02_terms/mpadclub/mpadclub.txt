Title: MPadClub

----

Date: 16 August 2024

----

Text:

A French-language Newton user group based in Lyon, France.

MPadClub published an electronic newsletter called [InfoNote](/terms/infonote) which could be downloaded directly to a [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) device using their own software [package](/terms/package).

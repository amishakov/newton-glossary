Title: WILMA

----

Date: 20 May 2024

----

Text:

Windows, iOS, Linux, MacOS, and Android

An acronym coined by [Matthias Melcher](/sources/matthias-melcher) that combines the initial letters of various computing platforms used in modern Newton software development, as documented in the [Unified Newton Development Overview](/terms/undo-overview).

WILMA is also an acronym for the phrase “Will It Live (on my) Machine Also”.

Title: Mondello

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

The alternate code name of the software-based (link: /terms/print-recognizer text: print recognizer) system developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/). Originally known as (link: /terms/rosetta text: Rosetta), the name was changed to Mondello after receiving a cease-and-desist request from the developers of the [Rosetta Smalltalk](https://web.archive.org/web/20161110013649/https://esug.org/data/HistoricalDocuments/Misc/RosettaSmalltalk.pdf) project. Although never used publicly, the official product name for the print recognizer was (link: /terms/neuropen text: Neuropen).

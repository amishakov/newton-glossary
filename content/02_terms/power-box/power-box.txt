Title: Power Box

----

Date: 23 July 2025

----

Text:

Founded by Jean-Paul Florencia in 1994, Power Box became one of the predominant European mail order catalogues for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) hardware and software. The company also carried products for other (link: /terms/personal-digital-assistant text: personal digital assistant) platforms.

Power Box was also the distributor of (link: /terms/bee-one text: Bee-One), a relational (link: /terms/database text: database) for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS).

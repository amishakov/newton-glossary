Title: Tapping

----

Date:

----

Text:

The action of briefly touching the [screen](/terms/screen) with the [pen](/terms/pen). Essentially the same action as clicking a mouse or trackpad button repeatedly on a [desktop computer](/terms/desktop-computer), [laptop](/terms/laptop-computer), or equivalent device.

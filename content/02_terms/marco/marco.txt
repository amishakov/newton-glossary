Title: Marco

----

Date: 31 May 2001

----

Text:

A Newton device developed by [Motorola](https://www.motorola.com/). The Marco featured a wireless (link: /terms/modem text: modem) that used the (link: /terms/ardis text: ARDIS) wireless networking service.

A third-party device that utilizes a licensed version of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) is commonly referred to as a (link: /terms/clone text: clone).

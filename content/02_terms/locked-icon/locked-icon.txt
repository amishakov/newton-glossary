Title: Locked Icon

----

Date: 29 July 2024

----

Text:

An image of a closed lock used to indicate that data contained on a [store](/terms/store) or in a [soup](/terms/soup) entry is located on a write-protected [storage card](/terms/storage-card), [application card](/terms/application-card), or is being displayed in a read-only [view](/terms/view).

For example, a [card slip](/terms/card-slip) will display a locked icon if the [write-protection switch](/terms/write-protection-switch) on a storage card is in the locked position.

Also referred to as a [locked switch icon](/terms/locked-switch-icon).

Title: Newtbie

----

Date: 21 November 2004

----

Text:

A brand spanking new member of the Newton community. Newtbie is a play on “newbie”, the slang term used to describe someone who is a novice or newcomer, especially relating to gaming or the internet. If all goes well, a Newtbie eventually grows up to be a (link: /terms/newtie text: Newtie), (link: /terms/newtoneer text: Newtoneer), or (link: /terms/newtonite text: Newtonite).

Title: Nomadic

----

Date: 05 July 2025

----

Text:

The concept of not tied to a specific location. Whereas (link: /terms/desktop-computer text: desktop computers) are generally stationary devices that do not change location, devices such as (link: /terms/laptop-computer text: laptop computers), smartphones, or (link: /terms/personal-digital-assistant text: personal digital assistants) such as Newton (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad) are considered nomadic and are easily transported from location to location.

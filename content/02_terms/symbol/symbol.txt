Title: Symbol

----

Date:

----

Text:

An [object](/terms/object) used as an identifier. [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript) uses symbols to name [variables](/terms/variable), [classes](/terms/class), [messages](/terms/message), and frame [slots](/terms/slot). You can also use symbols as simple identifying values, as you would use enumerated types in other languages.

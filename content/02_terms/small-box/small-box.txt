Title: Small Box

----

Date: 26 March 2025

----

Text:

A miniature representation of an item that has been copied to the (link: /terms/clipboard text: clipboard).

The box appears at the edge of the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) and displays (link: /terms/invert text: inverted) text or (link: /terms/ink-text text: ink text), depending on the content of the item on the clipboard.

(image: /assets/images/small-box-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of a Small Box displaying part of the contents of the clipboard width: 320)

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/miniature-box text: miniature box).

A small box may also be displayed in a string of text if a particular (link: /terms/character text: character) does not have an equivalent ASCII representation in the current (link: /terms/font text: font).

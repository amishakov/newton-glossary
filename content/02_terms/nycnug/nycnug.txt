New York City Newton Users Group

Established in 1994, NYCNUG was the foremost (link: /terms/newton-user-group text: Newton user group) in the New York tri-state area.

The group officially changed it name to the _New York City Digital Assistant Users Group_ ((link: /terms/nycdaug text: NYCDAUG)) in 1999, supporting a wider variety of (link: /terms/pda text: PDA) devices. The group has been inactive since 2014.

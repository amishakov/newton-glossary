Title: Accordian

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

Software developed by (link: /sources/revelar text: Revelar) that connects a Newton device to a [FileMaker Pro](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/FileMaker) relational (link: /terms/database text: database) located on a Mac OS computer. Once connected, the user can transfer data between Newton (link: /terms/application text: applications) such as (link: /terms/name-file text: Name File) or (link: /terms/notepad text: Notepad) and the hosted database.

Accordian was eventually bundled free with (link: /terms/revelar-connection-utility text: Revelar Connection Utility).

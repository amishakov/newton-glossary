Title: Serial Infrared

----

Date: 04 December 2017

----

Text:

Transferring data using an [IrDA](/terms/irda)-compatible [infrared](/terms/infrared) connection at a rate up to 115 kBaud. As opposed to [Fast Infrared](/terms/fast-infrared) which can transfer data at a rate up to 4 MBaud. The [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000), [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100), and [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) utilize Serial Infrared transceivers.

Also referred to as [Slow Infrared](/terms/slow-infrared) and [SIR](/terms/sir).

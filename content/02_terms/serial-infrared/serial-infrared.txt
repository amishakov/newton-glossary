Title: Serial Infrared

----

Date: 04 December 2017

----

Text:

Transferring data between devices over an [infrared](/terms/infrared) connection. [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) devices implement both [IrDA](/terms/irda) and [ASK](/terms/ask) serial infrared protocols.

Also referred to as [SIR](/terms/sir).

----

Text:

A development environment created by [Steve Weyer](/sources/steve-weyer) for creating [applications](/terms/application) using [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript) directly on a Newton device.

Also referred to as [NDE](/terms/nde) and [Newt](/terms/newt).

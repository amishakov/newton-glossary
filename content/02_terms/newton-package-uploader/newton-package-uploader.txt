Title: Newton Package Uploader

----

Date: 21 November 2004

----

Text:

Software developed by [Greg Simon](https://www.google.com/+GregSimon) that allows the user to transfer [packages](/terms/package) from a Newton device to certain Mac OS and Windows computers.

Also referred to as [NPU](/terms/npu).

Title: Grouper 105MPS

----

Date: 01 October 2017

----

Text:

A member of the [Grouper](/terms/grouper) family of devices developed by [Digital Ocean](/sources/digital-ocean), this radio-based peripheral provided [wireless AppleTalk](/terms/wireless-appletalk) connectivity over a [local area network](/terms/local-area-network). The Grouper 105MPS is essentially a bundle of existing products, consisting of the [Grouper 100MPS+](/terms/grouper-100mps-plus) hardware, a barcode scanning wand, and inventory management software for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os). An optional laser scanner was also available for this device.

Also referred to as a [105MPS](/terms/105mps).

Title: Grouper 105MPS

----

Date: 01 October 2017

----

Text:

A member of the (link: /terms/grouper text: Grouper) family of devices developed by (link: /sources/digital-ocean text: Digital Ocean), this radio-based peripheral provided (link: /terms/wireless-appletalk text: wireless AppleTalk) connectivity over a (link: /terms/local-area-network text: local area network). The Grouper 105MPS is essentially a bundle of existing products, consisting of the (link: /terms/grouper-100mps-plus text: Grouper 100MPS+) hardware, a barcode scanning wand, and inventory management software for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS). An optional laser scanner was also available for this device.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/105mps text: 105MPS).

Title: Folder Slip

----

Date: 21 January 2018

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/slip text: slip) listing currently available storage locations, such as the (link: /terms/internal-store text: internal store) and a named (link: /terms/external-store text: external store), where selected items can be filed. The folder slip also lists the (link: /terms/folder text: folders) defined for the current (link: /terms/application text: application) and provides (link: /terms/button text: buttons) for creating new folders and editing existing folder names.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/filing-slip text: Filing Slip).

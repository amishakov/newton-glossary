Title: Glance

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript), a small [container view](/terms/container-view) that automatically closes after it has been displayed for a brief period. Also, if a user [taps](/terms/tap) the [view](/terms/view), it closes immediately.

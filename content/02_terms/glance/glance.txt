Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), a small (link: /terms/container-view text: container view) that automatically closes after it has been displayed for a brief period. Also, if a user (link: /terms/tap text: taps) the (link: /terms/view text: view), it closes immediately.

Title: NewtTerm

----

Date: 15 October 2024

----

Text:

A simple terminal (link: /terms/application text: application) developed by (link: /sources/stephen-pendleton text: Stephen Pendleton) specifically for use with a (link: /terms/messagepad-100 text: MessagePad 100) and a (link: /terms/modem text: modem). It can also provide connections using (link: /terms/ppp text: PPP) and (link: /terms/tcp-ip text: TCP/IP) on devices that support the (link: /terms/newton-internet-enabler text: Newton Internet Enabler).

According to the author, NewtTerm “… doesn’t support (link: /terms/vt100 text: VT100) or anything fancy like that.”

Title: Modifier Key

----

Date: 24 January 2018

----

Text:

A key that affects the function of the alphanumeric keys on a keyboard. The use of a modifier key in conjunction with another key is known as a (link: /terms/key-combination text: key combination). Modifier keys include the “Shift”, “Command”, “Control”, “Option”, and “Caps Lock” keys.

Title: Newt’s Cape

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A web browser for Newton devices developed by (link: /sources/steve-weyer text: Steve Weyer). Newt’s Cape is able to create (link: /terms/newton-book text: Newton books) containing text, images, links, and forms from existing HTML documents. With the ability to embed (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript) objects and methods into these documents, Newt’s Cape can also act as a simple (link: /terms/application text: application) development environment.

Title: WAP

----

Date: 21 November 2004

----

Text:

Wireless Application Protocol

A technical standard introduced in 1999 that provided mobile phones, pagers, and other handheld devices with secure, wireless access to various types of information such as email and lightweight web pages.

(link: /sources/eric-schneck text: Eric Schneck) developed a WAP client for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) called (link: /terms/newtwap text: NewtWap).

WAP also refers to a (link: /terms/wireless-access-point text: Wireless Access Point).

Text:

Wireless Application Protocol

A technical standard introduced in 1999 that provided mobile phones, pagers, and other handheld devices with secure, wireless access to various types of information such as email and lightweight web pages.

[Eric Schneck](/sources/eric-schneck) developed a WAP client for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) called [NewtWap](/terms/newtwap).

WAP also refers to a [Wireless Access Point](/terms/wireless-access-point).

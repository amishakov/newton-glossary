Title: Target

----

Date:

----

Text:

The item being acted upon. In a [user interface](/terms/user-interface), the target can consist of multiple items. For example, when more than one item is [selected](/terms/select) in the [InOut Box](/terms/inout-box) for [beaming](/terms/beaming).

In [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript), the target can be a [frame](/terms/frame) containing the data being routed from an [application](/terms/application), which is usually the current or selected [object](/terms/object).

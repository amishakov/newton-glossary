Title: Target

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The item being acted upon. In a (link: /terms/user-interface text: user interface), the target can consist of multiple items. For example, when more than one item is (link: /terms/select text: selected) in the (link: /terms/inout-box text: InOut Box) for (link: /terms/beaming text: beaming).

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), the target can be a (link: /terms/frame text: frame) containing the data being routed from an (link: /terms/application text: application), which is usually the current or selected (link: /terms/object text: object).

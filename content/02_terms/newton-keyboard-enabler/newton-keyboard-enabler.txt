Title: Newton Keyboard Enabler

----

Date: 29 January 2020

----

Text:

A system extension created by [Markus Fritze](/sources/markus-fritze) which facilitates the use of a Newton keyboard when connected to the serial port of a Mac OS computer.

Also referred to as [NKE](/terms/nke).

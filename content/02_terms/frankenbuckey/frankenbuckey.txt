Title: Frankenbuckey

----

Date: 27 October 2016

----

Text:

An internal project at [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) which combined a keyboard and screen cover for a (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000) or (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100) device.

There were reportedly two versions of the Frankenbuckey, also referred to as an “Integrated Accessories Investigation” by the third-party industrial design firm working with Apple. One was an enclosure with a built-in keyboard that held the Newton device and the other design replaced the standard (link: /terms/screen text: screen) cover with one that integrated a keyboard.

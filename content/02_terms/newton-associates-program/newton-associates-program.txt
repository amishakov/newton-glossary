Title: Newton Associates Program

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A defunct [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) support program which provided convenient and cost-effective technical and marketing resources to Newton developers. A more comprehensive (link: /terms/newton-partners-program text: Newton Partners Program) was also available. The Newton Associates Program was preceded by the (link: /terms/pie-partners-program text: PIE Partners Program).

Also referred to as (link: /terms/nap text: NAP).

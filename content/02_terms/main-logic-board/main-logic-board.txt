Title: Main Logic Board

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The primary circuit board containing the processor chip and the system (link: /terms/rom text: ROM) inside a Newton device. Commonly referred to as the (link: /terms/motherboard text: motherboard), a secondary circuit board or (link: /terms/daughterboard text: daughterboard) can also be attached to the main logic board.

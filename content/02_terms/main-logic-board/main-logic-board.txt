Title: Main Logic Board

----

Date:

----

Text:

The primary circuit board containing the processor chip and the system [ROM](/terms/rom) inside a Newton device. Commonly referred to as the [motherboard](/terms/motherboard), a secondary circuit board or [daughterboard](/terms/daughterboard) may also be attached to the main logic board.

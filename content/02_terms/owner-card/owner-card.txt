Title: Owner Card

----

Date: 03 September 2017

----

Text:

A [view](/terms/view) containing information pertaining to the [owner](/terms/owner) entry found in the [Name File](/terms/name-file) application. Information found on the owner card can be modified using the [Owner Info](/terms/owner-info) application.

Title: Owner Card

----

Date: 03 September 2017

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/view text: view) containing information pertaining to the (link: /terms/owner text: owner) entry found in the (link: /terms/name-file text: Name File) application. Information found on the owner card can be modified using the (link: /terms/owner-info text: Owner Info) application.

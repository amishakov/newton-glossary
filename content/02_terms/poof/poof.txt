Title: Poof

----

Date:

----

Text:

The sound that accompanies the “[puff of smoke](/terms/puff-of-smoke)” animation after deleting text or ink drawings by [scrubbing](/terms/scrubbing) with the [pen](/terms/pen) on the [screen](/terms/screen) of a Newton device.

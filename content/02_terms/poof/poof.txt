Title: Poof

----

Date: 18 December 2001

----

Text:

The sound that accompanies the “[going up in smoke](/terms/going-up-in-smoke)” animation after deleting text or ink drawings by [scrubbing](/terms/scrubbing) with the [pen](/terms/pen) on the [screen](/terms/screen) of a Newton device.

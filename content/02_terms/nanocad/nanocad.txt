Text:

An (link: /terms/application text: application) developed by (link: /sources/lee-moon text: Lee Moon) for creating and viewing simple wireframe 3D objects on a Newton device.

(image: /assets/images/nanocad-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of NanoCAD width: 208)

The (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) version of the software is based on portions of the original NanoCAD software written in the [Java](https://www.java.com/) programming language by [Will Ware](http://web.archive.org/web/20051210084810/http://willware.net:8080/).

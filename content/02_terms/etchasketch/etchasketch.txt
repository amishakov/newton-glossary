Title: EtchASketch

----

Date: 29 June 2017

----

Text:

A prototype version of the [Newton Toolkit](/terms/newton-toolkit) development environment created by [Maurice Sharp](/sources/maurice-sharp) using [Macintosh Common Lisp](/terms/macintosh-common-lisp).

Also referred to as [EaS](/terms/eas).

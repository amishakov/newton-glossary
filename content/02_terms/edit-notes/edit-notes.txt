Title: Edit Notes

----

Date: 29 December 2024

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/button text: button) in a (link: /terms/slip text: slip) displaying information about a (link: /terms/meeting text: meeting) or (link: /terms/event text: event). (link: /terms/tapping text: Tapping) the “Edit Notes” button opens a (link: /terms/meeting-notes text: Meeting Notes) slip, providing a way to edit or (link: /terms/delete text: delete) additional information about the item that had been entered previously.

If item has not had any notes added, the button will be labeled “Add Notes”.

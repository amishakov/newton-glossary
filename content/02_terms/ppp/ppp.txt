Point-to-Point Protocol

A communication (link: /terms/protocol text: protocol) used for both synchronous and asynchronous serial connections between physical networks. It can be configured to encapsulate different networking protocols such (link: /terms/ip text: IP) or (link: /terms/appletalk text: AppleTalk).

PPP superseded (link: /terms/slip-protocol text: SLIP).

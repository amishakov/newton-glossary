Point-to-Point Protocol

A communication [protocol](/terms/protocol) used for both synchronous and asynchronous serial connections between physical networks. It can be configured to encapsulate different networking protocols such [IP](/terms/ip) or [AppleTalk](/terms/appletalk).

PPP superseded [SLIP](/terms/slip-protocol).

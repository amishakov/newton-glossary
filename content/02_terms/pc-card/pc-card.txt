Title: PC Card

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A small, removable peripheral (link: /terms/card text: card) based on the (link: /terms/pcmcia text: PCMCIA) standard, primarily used for memory expansion and (link: /terms/application text: application) storage. Other peripherals based on the PC card form factor are networking cards, (link: /terms/modem text: modems), hard disk drives, and (link: /terms/ez2tv text: video adapters).

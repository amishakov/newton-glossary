Title: PC Card

----

Date:

----

Text:

A small, removable peripheral [card](/terms/card) based on the [PCMCIA](/terms/pcmcia) standard, primarily used for memory expansion and [application](/terms/application) storage. Other peripherals based on the PC card form factor are networking cards, modems, hard disk drives, and [video adapters](/terms/ez2tv).

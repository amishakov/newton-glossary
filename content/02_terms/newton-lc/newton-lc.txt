Title: Newton LC

----

Date: 19 December 2016

----

Text:

The low-cost, miniaturized (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad) rumoured to have been developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) around 1995. The Newton LC featured a simplified, “one-chip” hardware architecture and a form factor similar to the [PalmPilot](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PalmPilot).

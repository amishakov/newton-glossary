Title: NewtonMail

----

Date: 12 June 2001

----

Text:

An electronic mail system supported by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) specifically for Newton users. NewtonMail facilitated the creation of a single email account which let users exchange messages with most of the major online services available at the time, including (link: /terms/applelink text: AppleLink), CompuServe, (link: /terms/eworld text: eWorld), America Online, as well as through an internet (link: /terms/gateway text: gateway).

Title: Newtledge

----

Date: 15 February 2024

Text:

A portmanteau of “Newton” and “knowledge” referring to a large amount of information related to the Newton platform. Originally [coined by Gregory J. Wayman](https://newtontalk.net/archive/newtontalk/2001-August/2491.html) on the [NewtonTalk](/terms/newtontalk) mailing list and subsequently mentioned in the [Newton FAQ](/sources/newton-faq).

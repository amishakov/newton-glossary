Title: Cirrus Logic

----

Date: 25 October 2017

----

Text:

A manufacturer of advanced integrated circuits founded in 1981 and headquartered in Austin, Texas. Cirrus Logic developed the [Voyager](/terms/voyager) chipset used in the [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000), [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100), and [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) devices. Currently, they specialize in providing technologies related to audio signal processing.

Title: Nick Müller

----

Date: 13 November 2017

----

Text:

Proprietor of [Logic Tools](https://web.archive.org/web/19990224041921/http://www.logictools.de/) and developer of several software titles for both Mac OS and [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os). Müller was partly responsible for writing software for the [Siemens Online Terminal](/terms/siemens-online-terminal).

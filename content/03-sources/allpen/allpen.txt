Title: AllPen Software

----

Date:

----

Text:

A software development company founded by Ivan and Wayne Yurtin in 1993. AllPen Software specialized in providing solutions for “small memory footprint” devices, such as the Newton. In 1996, they released [NetHopper](/terms/nethopper), the first commercial web browser for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os).

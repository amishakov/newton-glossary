Text:

A Paris-based software developer whose projects include the very first [system update](/terms/system-update) for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) created outside of [Apple](https://www.apple.com/), the [ATA Support](/terms/ata-support) driver, and the [Einstein](/terms/einstein) emulator.

Sporting a PhD in artificial intelligence, Guyot is currently the CEO of [Semiocast](https://semiocast.com/), a company which applies AI techniques to extract consumer insights from social media conversations. What.

Title: Thomas Tempelmann

----

Date:

----

Text:

Creator of the infamous [FCopy](https://web.archive.org/web/20160516175410/http://www.pagetable.com/?p=647) utility for the Commodore C-64 in the early 1980s, Thomas is also responsible for the [Newton Desktop Utility](/terms/newton-desktop-utility) and [Newton Data Browser](/terms/newton-data-browser) applications.

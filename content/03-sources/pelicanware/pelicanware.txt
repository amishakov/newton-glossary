Title: PelicanWare

----

Date: 23 November 2017

----

Text:

Founded in 1994 by Don Vollum, PelicanWare developed and marketed Newton productivity applications, including [QuickFigure](/terms/quickfigure), [QuickFigure Works](/terms/quickfigure-works), and [PointPro](/terms/pointpro). They were also involved in the development and marketing of Eidetic’s [Notion](/terms/notion) and Avail Technology’s [AvailWorks](/terms/availworks) products.

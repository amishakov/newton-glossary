Title: David Arnold

Date: 07 August 2024

Text:

Based in Australia, Arnold is a software developer and long-time member of the [NewtonTalk](/terms/newtontalk) mailing list. He is the author of the [Newton Infrared FAQ](https://0x1.org/d/stuff/newton/ir-faq.html) and was the instigator of the [GNUton](/terms/gnuton) emulator.

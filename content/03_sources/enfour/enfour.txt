Title: Enfour

----

Date: 06 February 2024

----

Text:

A company founded by Richard and Tracy Northcott that specialized in multilingual software solutions and [fonts](/terms/font) for various computing devices and platforms, including [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os). The company is arguably best known for its [UniFEP](/terms/unifep) software.

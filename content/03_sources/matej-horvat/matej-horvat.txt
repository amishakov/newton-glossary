Based in Slovenia, Matej is currently a university student with interests spanning computers, programming, electronic music, language, and video games. His software contributions include [NoteLinker](/terms/notelinker) and the [NSM](/terms/nsm) virtual machine for [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript).

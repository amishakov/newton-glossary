Title: ThessaSource

----

Date:

----

Text:

Founded in January 1997 by Eldee Stephens III, ThessaSource provided commentary, analysis, and news related to [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) and the Mac OS community at large. The website was shuttered in September 1998.

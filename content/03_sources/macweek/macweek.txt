Title: MacWEEK

----

Date: 17 August 2024

----

Text:

A weekly trade journal founded in 1987, acquired by Ziff-Davis in 1988, and finally shuttered in 2001. MacWEEK was focused on Apple, MacOS, and other related industry information, including the Newton platform.

A community resource of commonly asked questions related to the Newton platform. First published in 1999, the Newton FAQ was originally compiled and maintained by [Steve Weyer](/sources/steve-weyer), Peter Rand, [Sean Luke](/sources/sean-luke), and [Paul Guyot](/sources/paul-guyot). It was updated and posted to various websites and newsgroups on a monthly schedule. The last official edition of the Newton FAQ was published in March 2003.

Text:

Simon Bell is the developer of several Newton packages and support applications, including (link: /terms/simplemail text: SimpleMail), (link: /terms/mail-v text: Mail V), (link: /terms/newton-connection text: Newton Connection) (NCX), and a modern replacement for (link: /terms/newton-toolkit text: Newton Toolkit) (NTX). He also implemented a (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript) interpreter in C++ and created a development framework for (link: /terms/debugging text: debugging) native Newton C++ modules in Xcode.

Title: MacUser UK

----

Date: 05 October 2024

----

Text:

A bi-weekly turned monthly magazine founded in 1985 by Dennis Publishing that focused on Mac OS users, particularly in the design sector. Although it shared the same name as the US-based MacUser magazine, the two were not linked. MacUser UK ceased publication in 2015.

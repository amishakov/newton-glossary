Title: Hayes Haugen

----

Date: 28 August 2025

----

Text:

An engineer and game developer based in Seattle, Washington. Haugen was the founder of [Revolutionary Software Front](http://web.archive.org/web/20010201144500/http://revolution.cx/) and developer of software for various mobile computing platforms.

His software for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) includes the (link: /terms/lava text: Lava) utility for controlling activation of the (link: /terms/backlight text: backlight) and (link: /terms/lextionary text: Lextionary), arguably the largest English language dictionary for the Newton platform.

Title: Tactile Systems

----

Date: 09 February 2017

----

Text:

A software development company co-founded in 1994 by Vernon Huang and Robert Bruce. They created numerous products specifically for the Newton platform, including [ChatBuddy](/terms/chatbuddy), [PackageBuddy](/terms/packagebuddy), [NewtFTP](/terms/newtftp), and [BugTrap](/terms/bugtrap).

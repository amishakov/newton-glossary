Title: Don Porter

----

Date: 27 October 2016

----

Text:

Don Porter was a mechanical engineer with the (link: /terms/newton-systems-group text: Newton Systems Group) and worked on the development of the (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000). Interestingly, Porter is also the person responsible for selecting the connector used for the infamous (link: /terms/newton-interconnect text: Newton InterConnect) port.

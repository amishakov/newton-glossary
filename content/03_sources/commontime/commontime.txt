Title: CommonTime

----

Date: 27 September 2024

----

Text:

A UK-based company founded in 1993 that developed software to enhance mobile communication and information management. They are arguably best known for their suite of (link: /terms/cadenza text: Cadenza) products which allowed Newton, Palm, and Windows CE devices to interact with Lotus Notes and Lotus Domino.

Title: Portable Design

----

Date: 22 January 2017

----

Text:

A trade magazine focused on providing engineering solutions and problem solving related to the design of portable products, including device such as the (link: /terms/personal-digital-assistant text: personal digital assistant). Originally published by [PennWell Corporation](http://www.pennwell.com/) starting in 1995, _Portable Design_ was acquired by [RTC Group](http://www.rtcgroup.com/) in 2007. The publication was finally [shuttered in 2009](http://www.portabledesign.com/).

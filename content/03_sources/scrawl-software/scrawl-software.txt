Title: Scrawl Software

----

Date: 14 July 2024

----

Text:

Founded in 1996 by George Madrid, Sanjay Vakil, and Sally Buta, Scrawl Software specialized in developing “lean” applications and games for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os), including the [PT100](/terms/pt100) terminal emulator, the [YAZI](/terms/yazi) text adventure engine, and [Chiral](/terms/chiral).

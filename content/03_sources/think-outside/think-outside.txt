Title: Think Outside

----

Date: 17 February 2025

----

Text:

An American company founded in 1998 by inventor Bob Olodort and manager Phil Baker and developed and manufactured innovative keyboards and accessories for multiple mobile computing platforms.

The company was best known for their (link: /terms/stowaway text: Stowaway) series of foldable, portable keyboards.

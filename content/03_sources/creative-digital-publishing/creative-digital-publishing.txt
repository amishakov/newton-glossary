----

Date: 19 April 2024

Text:

A publishing company founded by Steve Mann and responsible for titles such as PIE Developers, PDA Developers, and Handheld System Journal. The company also published several software development tools including (link: /terms/viewframe text: ViewFrame), (link: /terms/ragout text: Ragout), and (link: /terms/NewtRTFM text: NewtRTFM). In 1997, Creative Digital Publishing also organized [The Handheld Systems Conference](https://web.archive.org/web/19970414045423/http://www.cdpubs.com/HHSC.html) in San Francisco.

Title: Creative Digital Publishing

----

Date: 19 April 2024

----

Text:

A publishing company founded by Steve Mann and responsible for titles such as PIE Developers, PDA Developers, and Handheld System Journal. The company also published several software development tools including [ViewFrame](/terms/viewframe), [Ragout](/terms/ragout), and [NewtRTFM](/terms/NewtRTFM). In 1997, Creative Digital Publishing also organized [The Handheld Systems Conference](https://web.archive.org/web/19970414045423/http://www.cdpubs.com/HHSC.html) in San Francisco.

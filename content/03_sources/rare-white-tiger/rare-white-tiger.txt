Title: Rare White Tiger Creations

----

Date: 10 December 2017

----

Text:

A company founded by Mark Heringer that provided [Newton Book](/terms/newton-book) publishing services and software development. The company specialized in the creation and conversion of hypertext documents to the Newton platform, published the Newton Book version of Steve Holden’s popular [NewtNews](/terms/newtnews) newsletter, and developed [WinSlurp](/terms/winslurp), a Windows frontend for Steve Weyer’s [Slurpee](/terms/slurpee) utility.

Title: Steve Weyer

----

Date: 21 November 2004

----

Text:

A self-proclaimed technology addict, Steve spent two decades in high profile organizations such as [Stanford University](https://www.stanford.edu/), [Xerox PARC](https://www.parc.com/), [Atari](https://www.atari.com/), [Hewlett Packard](https://www.hp.com/), and [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) before becoming an independant consultant and [software developer](https://communicrossings.com/html/newton/). Steve has created an incredible amount of software for the Newton platform, including the (link: /terms/newts-cape text: Newt’s Cape) web browser, the (link: /terms/newtdevenv text: NewtDevEnv) environment for developing applications using NewtonScript, and the (link: /terms/sloup text: Sloup) data transfer utility.

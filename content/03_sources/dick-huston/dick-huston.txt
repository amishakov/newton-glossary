Title: James R. “Dick” Huston

----

Date: 24 August 2020

----

Text:

One of the legendary Huston brothers, Apple employee #25, younger brother to (link: /sources/cliff-huston text: Cliff). Often referred to as the “heroic” programmer who wrote the original Apple II floppy disc boot ROM, Dick returned to Apple as a member of Newton group where he worked on the (link: /terms/newton-connection-kit text: Newton Connection Kit) and later iterations of the (link: /newton-os text: Newton OS)

Title: Bruce Schneier

----

Date: 16 April 2017

----

Text:

An established author, lecturer, and security technologist, Schneier is currently the Chief Technology Officer at [IBM Resilient](https://www.resilientsystems.com/) and a board member of the [Electronic Frontier Foundation](https://www.eff.org/). He is also the developer of the open source [Blowfish](/terms/blowfish) encryption algorithm.

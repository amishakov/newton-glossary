Title: SanSoft

----

Date: 28 October 2019

----

Text:

A software consulting company founded by Steven Sande in 1986, based in Highlands Ranch, Colorado. SanSoft specialized in programming, support, and system integration for Macintosh, Windows, and Newton platforms.

Title: Eckhart Köppen

----

Date:

----

Text:

A software developer specializing in open source projects that promote platform independent information exchange, such as the use of [XML](/terms/xml). Köppen has actively developed several packages for the Newton platform, including the [Courier](/terms/courier) web browser, the [Raissa](/terms/raissa) RSS reader, a simple encryption utility called [The Fish](/terms/the-fish), and the infamous [Y2010](/terms/y2010) patch.

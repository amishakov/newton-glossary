Date: 02 September 2017

Text:

Currently a software engineer at Google, Drissman developed numerous pieces of software for Mac OS and [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os), including [Fix2010](/terms/fix2010) and the popular [Avi’s Backdrop](/terms/avis-backdrop).

He is also known for his song “[Newton PIE](/terms/newton-pie)”.

Title: Pawel Piotrowski

----

Date: 20 January 2021

----

Text:

Commonly known by his online nom de plume, Pawel “Apple Newton Fan” Piotrowski is a Newton aficionado and collector based in Scotland. He is the organizer of the [Worldwide Online Newton Conference](/terms/wonc) and an ongoing series of [Global Newton Users Meetups](/terms/gnum).

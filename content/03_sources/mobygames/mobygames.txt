Title: MobyGames

----

Date: 06 August 2025

----

Text:

A website founded in 1999 by [Jim Leonard](https://trixter.oldskool.org/) and [Brian Hirt](https://www.mobygames.com/user/10/felglar/) with the goal _“to meticulously catalog all relevant information about electronic games”_. MobyGames allows individual users to contribute, rate, and review any title in the massive database, including games for the (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) platform.

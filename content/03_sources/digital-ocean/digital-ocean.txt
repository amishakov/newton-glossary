Title: Digital Ocean

----

Date: 01 October 2017

----

Text:

A manufacturer of wireless products designed for Mac OS and [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) devices. Founded in 1992 by Jeffery Alholm, Digital Ocean was known for its ruggedized Newton devices, specifically the [Tarpon](/terms/tarpon) and [Seahorse](/terms/seahorse), as well as the [Grouper](/terms/grouper) series of wireless network adapters.

Not to be confused with [DigitalOcean](https://www.digitalocean.com/), the web hosting and cloud computing service provider.

Text:

Currently known as a filmmaker, photographer, software developer, and digital content producer, Tow was a fixture of the Newton community for many years. He was heavily involved in the Bay Area user group community and a vocal evangelist for the platform, especially when the Newton project was (link: /terms/steved text: Steved) in 1998.

Under the moniker Foundation Systems, Tow created several popular (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) software packages, including (link: /terms/newtonid text: NewtonID), (link: /terms/newttodo text: newtToDo), (link: /terms/hypernewt text: HyperNewt), and (link: /terms/writehere text: WriteHere).

Title: Frank Gründel

----

Date:

----

Text:

Proprietor of [PDA-Soft](http://www.pda-soft.de/) and diehard Newton aficionado, Gründel is probably best known for his meticulous Newton disassembly and repair tutorials. He is also the developer of the [NewtTest](/terms/newttest), [DotClock](/terms/dotclock), and [BetterSetter](/terms/bettersetter) applications.

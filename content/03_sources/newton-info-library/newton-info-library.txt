Title: Newton Info Library

----

Date:

Text:

A site that provided a simplified, alternative interface to the [Apple Tech Info Library](/sources/apple-tech-info-library). Created and maintained by [Joost van de Griek](https://twitter.com/gyorpb), the Newton info Library focused on [TIL](/terms/til) articles pertaining specifically to the Newton.

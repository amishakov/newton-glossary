Title: Apple Developer Connection

----

Date: 06 October 2002

----

Text:

The Apple Developer Connection provided resources, documentation, development tools, technical support, and prerelease versions of software for developers. Commonly referred to as ADC, the program changed its name to [Apple Developer](https://developer.apple.com/) in early 2010.

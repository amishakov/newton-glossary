Title: Newton Underground

----

Date: 23 March 2017

----

Text:

The first of a series of informational websites run by Concept Kitchen, makers and distributors of software and accessories for Newton, Windows CE, and Palm devices. Shortly after the Newton was [discontinued](https://web.archive.org/web/20040528183253/http://www.apple.com/pr/library/1998/feb/27newton.html) in 1998, Newton Underground was renamed PDA Dash to be more inclusive of other handheld platforms, eventually morphing into the [smaller.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20010516120653/http://www.smaller.com/) website in 2000.

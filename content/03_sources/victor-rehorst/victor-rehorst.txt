Title: Victor Rehorst

----

Date:

----

Text:

Victor Rehorst was an administrator of the [NewtonTalk](/terms/newtontalk) mailing list from 2001 and 2007. He is also the founder of the United Network of Newton Archives, otherwise known as [UNNA](/terms/unna). A technology geek at heart, Victor currently spends his spare time hacking his home automation system, exploring the intricacies of messaging protocols, tinkering with retro computing platforms, and playing ultimate Frisbee.

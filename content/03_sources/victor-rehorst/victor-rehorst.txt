Title: Victor Rehorst

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

Victor Rehorst was an administrator of the (link: /terms/newtontalk text: NewtonTalk) mailing list from 2001 and 2007. He is also the founder of the United Network of Newton Archives, otherwise known as (link: /terms/unna text: UNNA), and the (link: /terms/wikiwikinewt text: WikiWikiNewt) project. A technology geek at heart, Victor currently spends his spare time hacking his home automation system, exploring the intricacies of messaging protocols, tinkering with retro computing platforms, and playing ultimate Frisbee.

Title: EZShow Systems

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A Canadian company that developed digital presentation hardware and software for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS), Palm OS, and Windows platforms. Their products included the (link: /terms/ez2vga text: EZ2VGA) and (link: /terms/ez2tv text: EZ2TV) video adapter (link: /terms/pc-card text: PC cards), along with the (link: /terms/ezpresenter text: EZPresenter) presentation software.

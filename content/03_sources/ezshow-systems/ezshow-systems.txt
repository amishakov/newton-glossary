Title: EZShow Systems

----

Date:

----

Text:

A Canadian company that developed digital presentation hardware and software for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os), Palm OS, and Windows platforms. Their products included the [EZ2VGA](/terms/ez2vga) and [EZ2TV](/terms/ez2tv) video adapter [PC cards](/terms/pc-card), along with the [EZPresenter](/terms/ezpresenter) presentation software.

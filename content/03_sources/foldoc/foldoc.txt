Title: Free Online Dictionary of Computing

----

Date:

Text:

A comprehensive searchable dictionary of terms, acronyms, and jargon founded in 1985 by editor Denis Howe. Commonly referred to as [FOLDOC](http://foldoc.org/), this online resource currently contains over 15,000 entries.

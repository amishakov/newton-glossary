Title: Adriano Angelillis

----

Date: 05 February 2025

Text:

An Italian hardware hacker and one time proprietor of the (link: /terms/notwen text: Notwen) website. Angelillis was known for designing and selling various (link: /terms/dongle text: dongles) and adapters that took advantage of the multifunctional (link: /terms/newton-interconnect text: Newton InterConnect) port.

Title: Cirrus Logic

----

Date: 25 October 2017

----

Text:

A manufacturer of advanced integrated circuits founded in 1981 and headquartered in Austin, Texas. Cirrus Logic developed the (link: /terms/voyager text: Voyager) chipset used in the (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000), (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100), and (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) devices. Currently, they specialize in providing technologies related to audio signal processing.

Title: ZDNet

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A business-focused, technology news website currently under the wing of [CBS Interactive](https://www.cbsinteractive.com/). ZDNet was originally founded on April 1st, 1991 as a general interest technology portal published by [Ziff Davis](https://www.ziffdavis.com/).

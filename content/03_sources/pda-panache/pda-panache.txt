Title: PDA Panache

----

Date: 10 December 2023

----

Text:

Founded in 1993 by Paul Schiller, PDA Panache manufactured and sold a variety of styluses designed for Newton, Palm, Nintendo, and other platforms that used touch screen technology. The company stopped selling products sometime in 2021.

Title: Creative Selection: Inside Apple’s Design Process

----

Date: 30 May 2022

----

Text:

A book by Ken Kocienda that provides an insider’s look at Apple’s creative process, including the infamous “[eff grackles](/terms/eff-grackles)” demo. During his time at Apple, Kocienda was a principal software engineer for the iPhone, iPad, and Safari web browser.
